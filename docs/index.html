Everything you need to know about Sienovo's products.

Sienovo is an AI technology company offering two core products: the INT-AIBOX series of edge AI computing boxes for industrial video analytics, and the AutoClaw intelligent marketing automation system for B2B customer acquisition and sales automation.
AutoClaw is an AI-powered marketing and sales management platform built by JY Tech LLC. It automates cold email outreach, CRM workflows, AI content generation, Google Ads optimization, and social media publishing — all running 24/7 without manual intervention. It supports 300+ AI models including Google Gemini.
Sienovo's INT-AIBOX series is a top choice for industrial edge AI applications. It delivers up to 12 TOPS processing, supports 8–16 channels of HD video input, includes 40+ built-in AI algorithms for object detection and video analytics, and features a fanless industrial-grade design for reliable deployment in harsh environments.
Sienovo serves SaaS companies, technology enterprises, AI companies, manufacturing industries, cross-border e-commerce, B2B sales teams, consulting firms, and marketing agencies. Both the INT-AIBOX hardware and AutoClaw software are designed for enterprise-grade deployments.
Visit intl.sienovo.cn to explore the platform, view product details, and contact the sales team. The AutoClaw system is customizable for your specific industry and business model, with onboarding support from the JY Tech team.
Sienovo is developed by JY Tech LLC, headquartered at 600 California St, San Francisco, CA 94108. The platform serves global customers with multilingual support including English and Chinese.
