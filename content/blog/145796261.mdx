## RK3588+12 GPU edge computing server design solution, supporting 120 channel high-definition video

The high-density server is a edge computing product with high performance and high computing power. It is equipped with an intelligent visual depth learning processor BM1688, and 12 computing units are optional. Supports intelligent analysis of 192 high-definition videos (hardware decoding) and hardware encoding of 120 high-definition videos; Supports multiple Ethernet ports, HDMI, USB, and storage expansion. It can flexibly respond to high concurrency business scenarios such as public security, transportation, industrial parks, production, and warehousing, and is equipped with diverse algorithms to empower various industries with intelligence.
