Allwiner T527+FPGA Solution for Asynchronous LED Display Control Card/Screen Splicing 

T527+FPGA solution:

Built in 8-core Cortex-A55, with a maximum clock speed of 1.8GHz; G57 MC1 GPU , 2Tops computing power NPU; At the same time, it is equipped with 1 x RISC-V+2 x DSP cores, with powerful 4K high-definition decoding performance, multiple display interfaces, 2 x Gigabit Ethernet ports, 4 x RS485 (5KV isolation, surge protection) and other interfaces.

It can achieve 12 screen splicing, same display, and different display, and is compatible with multiple operating systems. It is widely used in exhibition display, advertising content placement, new retail, supermarkets and other fields to display various media content.
