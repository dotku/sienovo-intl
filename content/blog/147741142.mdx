**Introduction**

The ECU-4674 product is compliant with Electricity Certificate level IV with higher reliability and stability, which is suitable for Global P&E automation market and harsh environment.

ECU-4674 is designed for fanless heat dissipation and can provide up to 16 Ethernet ports and 18 isolated COM ports. ECU-4674 is positioned in the global power and energy

automation sector to meet the data gateway requirements of smart substations and more needs of other different applications.
