Scenario Requirements: Collaborative robots often require "real-time, stable, and precise" visual feedback in high-precision, highly flexible operations. They also need to adapt to complex, dynamic industrial environments with electromagnetic interference, while demanding flexible system deployment and controllable costs. Traditional vision solutions are often limited by transmission bandwidth, distance, anti-interference capabilities, and wiring complexity.

Product Application: Sienovo Machine Vision GMSL series GMSL cameras, with their advantages such as low latency, high bandwidth, long distance, anti-interference, simplified integration, and high reliability, can effectively address the core pain points of collaborative robots in terms of visual perception.

■ Multi-resolution output, high frame rate: 1MP (1280H x 960V) resolution and 2MP (1920H x 1200V) resolution optional, with 30-60fps frame rate optional.
■ GMSL 2 Transmission: Uses GMSL 2 communication protocol for data transmission, featuring high bandwidth and low latency.
■ Global Shutter: Employs a global shutter to reduce distortion and motion artifacts between frames in high-speed scenarios.
