In recent years, with the rapid development of electronic information technology, office methods trending towards globalization, networking, and intelligence have become increasingly popular across various industries. Against this backdrop, domestic software video conferencing systems have emerged. Software video conferencing systems enable people in different locations to hold meetings anytime, instantly meet, talk, exchange documents, share information resources, and collaborate on decision-making. They offer unparalleled communication advantages and can fully leverage existing networks and equipment to flexibly deploy a "remote conferencing communication platform," establishing applications for daily communication, work deployment, training and recruitment, and work exchanges within the system.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138302666/01_44856d21b19cf4297335f3584998b38e.png)

The high-definition video communication system based on the Phytium chip architecture offers multiple network options such as private networks, the internet, and mobile networks, depending on the application scenario. It employs dual security measures for communication safety: link security and data security. The server-side provides cluster deployment and redundant backup with seamless switching to ensure business continuity. It uses data encapsulation for transparent transmission ports, allowing specific data transmission ports to be designated, thereby ensuring the security of data information.
