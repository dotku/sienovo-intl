## Edge Computing Box

Powerful Built-in TPU | 32 TOPS INT8 Computing Power

● TPU platform box supporting floating-point operations, with high computing power of 32 TOPS@INT8, 16 TFLOPS@FP16, and 2 TFLOPS@FP32.

● Single chip supports real-time decoding of up to 32 channels of H.264 & H.265.

● Supports the domestic AI framework PaddlePaddle, and is compatible with mainstream deep learning frameworks such as Caffe /TensorFlow/MxNet/PyTorch/ONNX.

● Supports Docker containerized deployment and Python development environment.

● Features OTA (Over-The-Air) upgrade capability.

● Low-power design, combined with passive cooling from the enclosure.

● Supports wide operating temperature range of -20℃ to 60℃.

● Compatible with the domestic Kylin operating system.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/134754410/01_a4518a6ae6e01789da38e6b90d592e20.png)

This highly integrated smart workstation offers ultra-strong computing performance with a powerful built-in TPU, delivering up to 32 TOPS of INT8 computing power. It features an 8-core ARM Cortex-A53 CPU processor clocked at up to 2.3GHz. A comprehensive development and migration toolchain is provided, enabling rapid algorithm porting and project implementation. The smart workstation can operate in wide temperature environments and can be flexibly deployed in various AI scenarios, finding extensive applications in smart factories, smart construction sites, smart urban management, smart gas stations, and other fields.
