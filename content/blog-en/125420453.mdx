The Xinming XM3568A motherboard features Rockchip's RK3568 quad-core 64-bit Cortex-A55 processor, with a maximum clock frequency of 2.0GHz, delivering significantly improved performance. Built on a 22nm advanced process, it offers high efficiency with low power consumption.  
Equipped with a dual-core GPU architecture, high-performance VPU, and NPU. Supports OpenGL ES3.2/2.0/1.1 and Vulkan 1.1; enables 4K 60fps H.265/H.264/VP9 video decoding and 1080P 100fps H.265/H.264 video encoding.  
Rich expansion interfaces: includes 2 USB 3.0 ports, 8 USB 2.0 ports, MiPi DSI, eDP, I2C, UART, ADC, GPIO, and more, suitable for external device control and expansion.  
Supports Android and Linux OS upgrades, and system booting via USB drive, eMMC, and other methods. Diverse OS support makes entertainment, office work, programming, learning, and creative development effortless.
