Powered by the Sophon BM1684 chip, this computing accelerator card is designed for AI inference. It can be integrated into servers and industrial control computers, efficiently supporting all mainstream AI algorithms on the market to enable applications such as video structuring, facial recognition, behavior analysis, and status monitoring. It delivers AI empowerment to smart city, intelligent transportation, smart energy, smart finance, smart telecom, and smart industrial fields.
