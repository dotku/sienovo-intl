### 16 AI (16-bit, 400 kS/s), 2 AO (250 kS/s), Up to 32 DIO USB Multifunction I/O Device

The USB‑6212 is a multifunction DAQ device. It provides analog input, digital input, digital output, and two 32-bit counters. The device features an onboard amplifier designed for fast settling times at high scan rates. It also employs streaming technology to enable DMA-like high-speed bidirectional data transfer over USB. This device is ideal for test, control, and design applications, including portable data logging, field monitoring, embedded OEM systems, in-vehicle data acquisition, and academic use.
