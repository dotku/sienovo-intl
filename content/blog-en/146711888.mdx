A high-reliability standard 6U VPX board based on the Phytium FT2000/4 processor and X100 chipset, featuring Ethernet, SATA, PCIe, and display interfaces. The product functional block diagram is shown in Figure 1:

Table 1 Product Functional and Performance Specifications
