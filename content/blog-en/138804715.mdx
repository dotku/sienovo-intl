#### The AIoT-3568A-based smart bus LED display enables dynamic route information presentation and voice-based station alerts, reducing instances of passengers overshooting or missing stops. It significantly improves the efficiency and quality of public transportation services, delivering a more comfortable, safer, and convenient travel experience while adding new vitality to urban development.

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138804715/01_c7f96bfe4516a48618e01f01c4cd492e.jpeg)

Project Requirements

1. **Information Broadcasting**: Real-time display of bus arrival times, route details, weather forecasts, news, and other relevant information.

2. **Voice Announcements**: Supports voice announcements for arriving stations, helping passengers stay informed and reducing the risk of missing stops due to smartphone usage.

3. **Emergency Alerts**: In emergency situations, the smart bus LED display can trigger built-in alarm systems to alert passengers and guide them toward appropriate safety measures.

Advantages of the Motherboard Solution

The AIoT-3568A intelligent terminal motherboard provides stable and reliable core support for smart bus LED display devices.

**01/Outstanding Performance**

The AIoT-3568A features the RK3568 (Cortex-A55x4) quad-core processor with 1T computing power, ensuring real-time data refresh and stable display performance.

**02/Supports 4K Cropped Display**

Supports HDMI OUT with 4K cropped resolution output.

**03/High Reliability and Stability**

The motherboard uses immersion gold technology, corrosion-resistant gold fingers on the core board, and is securely mounted with four screw pillars. It supports 7x24-hour continuous operation without failure in environments ranging from 0°C to 60°C, offering superior environmental adaptability.

**04/Customization Support**

Supports configuration customization to meet specific requirements, enabling effective cost control. System customization is also available, with compatibility for Linux/OpenHarmony operating systems based on customer needs, providing a more secure and stable system environment for end products.

**Motherboard Specifications**

**Core Configuration:**

CPU: RK3568 quad-core processor, up to 2.0GHz  
NPU: 1T computing power  
Operating System: Android 11.0 (supports Linux/OpenHarmony)  
Memory: Standard 2GB (optional 4GB/8GB)  
Storage: Standard 16GB (optional 32GB/64GB/128GB)

**Display Interfaces:**

HDMI Output: 1 channel, standard Type-A female connector, supports up to 4Kx2K@60Hz  
LVDS Output: 1 channel, supports single/dual 6–8bit, directly drives 50/60Hz LCD panels, maximum resolution 1920\*1080  
eDP Output: 1 channel, directly drives various-resolution eDP LCD panels

**Peripheral Interfaces:**

USB: 1 x USB3.0, 5 x USB2.0  
Serial Ports: 2 x RS232, 2 x TTL, 1 x RS485  
I2C Interface: 1 channel, compatible with I2C touch panels or peripherals  
CAN: 1 channel  
Storage Card: TF card slot

**Audio Configuration:**

Audio Input/Output: Speaker output (supports stereo, default 6W, configurable to 10W with resistor change), 1 x MIC IN  
Headphone Output: Supports one 3- or 4-pole headphone jack

**Network Communication:**

Wireless Network: Built-in Wi-Fi; Bluetooth 4.2  
Wired Network: 10/100/1000M adaptive Ethernet  
Mobile Network:  
1. Supports 4G internet via PCI-E interface  
2. Supports 5G internet via M.2 5G interface (optional)

Case Studies

This solution has been successfully deployed in bus and metro systems in Shenzhen, Nanchang, Chengdu, and Xiamen.
