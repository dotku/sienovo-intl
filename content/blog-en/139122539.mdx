### 8-Channel AI (16-bit, 2 MS/s), 2-Channel AO (3.33 MS/s), 24-Channel DIO, USB Multifunction I/O Device

The USB-6366 is a simultaneous sampling multifunction DAQ device. This module provides analog I/O, digital I/O, and four 32-bit counter/timers for applications such as PWM, encoder, frequency, and event counting. Onboard NI-STC3 timing and synchronization technology provides advanced timing capabilities, including independent analog and digital timing engines and retriggerable measurement tasks. The USB-6366 is suitable for a wide range of applications, from basic data logging to control and test automation.
