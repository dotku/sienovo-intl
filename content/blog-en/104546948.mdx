Industrial 4.0 High-Speed Data Acquisition Solution Based on AM5728 DSP+ARM

****1 AM5728 Features****

*   Based on TI AM5728 floating-point dual DSP C66x + dual ARM Cortex-A15 industrial control and high-performance audio/video processor;
*   Multi-core heterogeneous CPU, integrating dual-core Cortex-A15, dual-core C66x floating-point DSP, dual-core PRU-ICSS, dual-core Cortex-M4 IPU, dual-core GPU and other processing units, supporting OpenCL, OpenMP, SysLink IPC multi-core development;
*   Powerful video encoding and decoding capabilities, supporting 1 channel 1080P60 or 2 channels 720P60 or 4 channels 720P30 hardware video encoding/decoding, and H.265 software video decoding;
*   Supports up to 1 channel 1080P60 full HD video input and 1 channel LCD + 1 channel HDMI 1.4a output;
*   Dual-core PRU-ICSS industrial real-time control subsystem, supporting industrial protocols such as EtherCAT, EtherNet/IP, PROFIBUS;
*   High-performance GPU, dual-core SGX544 3D accelerator and GC320 2D graphics acceleration engine, supporting OpenGL ES2.0;
*   Rich peripheral interfaces, integrating dual Gigabit Ethernet, PCIe, GPMC, USB 2.0, UART, SPI, QSPI, SATA 2.0, I2C, DCAN and other industrial control buses and interfaces, supporting high-speed USB 3.0 interface;
*   The development board leads out a V-PORT video interface, allowing flexible access to video input modules;
*   Extremely small size, only 86.5mm*60.5mm;
*   Industrial-grade precision B2B connectors, 0.5mm pitch, stable, easy to plug and unplug, anti-reverse insertion, with high-speed connectors used for critical large data interfaces to ensure signal integrity.

The XM5728-IDK is a development board designed by Shenzhen Xinmai based on the TI AM5728 (floating-point dual DSP C66x + dual ARM Cortex-A15) XM5728-SOM core board. It provides users with a test platform for the XM5728-SOM core board, used for rapid evaluation of the overall performance of the XM5728-SOM core board.

The XM5728-IDK baseboard adopts a 4-layer board design with immersion gold lead-free process. It not only provides customers with rich AM5728 getting started tutorials but also assists customers in baseboard development, providing long-term, comprehensive technical support to help customers achieve rapid secondary development of products and quick time-to-market.

It not only provides rich demo programs but also offers DSP+ARM multi-core communication development tutorials and comprehensive technical support to assist users in baseboard design and debugging, as well as DSP+ARM software development.
