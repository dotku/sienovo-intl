### 16 AI (16-bit, 250 kS/s), 2 AO (250 kS/s), 4 DI, 4 DO USB Multifunction I/O Device

The USB‑6211 is a multifunction DAQ device. It provides analog I/O, digital inputs, digital outputs, and two 32-bit counters. The device features an onboard amplifier specifically designed for fast settling times at high scan rates. It also employs streaming technology to enable DMA-like high-speed bidirectional data transfer over USB. This device is ideal for test, control, and design applications, including portable data logging, field monitoring, embedded OEM systems, in-vehicle data acquisition, and educational use.
