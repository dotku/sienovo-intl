## NI Domestic Alternative: USB‑6211 Multifunction I/O Device

The USB‑6211 is a multifunction DAQ (data acquisition) device that offers a range of features and capabilities for various applications. In this article, we will explore the key features and benefits of the USB‑6211, as well as its suitability for different use cases.

### Key Features and Benefits

The USB‑6211 provides analog I/O, digital inputs, digital outputs, and two 32-bit counters. It features an onboard amplifier specifically designed for fast settling times at high scan rates, making it suitable for applications that require high-speed data acquisition. The device also employs streaming technology to enable DMA-like high-speed bidirectional data transfer over USB.
