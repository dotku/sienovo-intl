The XM-5143 is a high-performance industrial computer designed for GigE camera applications in the machine vision industry. It can utilize Intel 6th/7th generation 65W high-performance CPUs to handle rapid inspection tasks. It features four independent Intel network chips and supports PoE power supply, which reduces wiring. It is also equipped with fast isolated programmable DI/O chips.
