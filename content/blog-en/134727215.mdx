Industrial parks are predominantly composed of manufacturing and logistics enterprises, with a dense workforce, a large number of temporary and migrant workers, high personnel turnover, and heavy vehicle traffic. These factors often lead to traffic accidents, vandalism of public facilities, and damage to landscaping projects. Yingma's smart park solution enables 100% management of all personnel entering and exiting the park. It provides trajectory tracking for all people and vehicles within the园区, along with fire and smoke detection. Through optimized route planning, the solution improves operational efficiency. Additionally, the accuracy of intruder detection exceeds 95%, while smoke and flame detection accuracy exceeds 90%, ensuring the park operates safely, orderly, and efficiently.
