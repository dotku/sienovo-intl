#### **I. Core Configuration Solutions**

1.  **Waterproof Enclosure Design**
    *   304 stainless steel fully sealed structure, IP66/IP67 certified, capable of withstanding high-pressure water jet cleaning and chemical detergent corrosion2.
    *   Interfaces use M12/M20 waterproof aviation connectors (Ethernet ports, serial ports, power terminals), eliminating the risk of liquid ingress26.
2.  **Multi-Ethernet and Multi-Serial Port Support**
    *   Basic configuration: **8 independent Gigabit Ethernet ports** (Intel I210AT chip) + **8 isolated RS-232/485 serial ports**6.
    *   Expansion capability: Via PCIe or modular slots, upgradable to a **10 serial port + 6 Ethernet port** combination (custom backplane required)56.
3.  **Industrial-Grade Processor Platform**
    *   Low-power solution: Intel Atom® x7433RE/x7835RE, supporting -40℃~75℃ wide-temperature operation2.
    *   High-performance solution: Intel® Celeron J1900 quad-core processor (2.0GHz), fanless cooling design6.
