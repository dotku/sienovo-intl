PXIe, 8-Channel AI (16-bit, 14 MS/s/ch), 2-Channel AO, 24-Channel DIO, PXI Multifunction I/O Module
The PXIe-6386 is a simultaneous-sampling multifunction DAQ device. This module provides analog I/O, digital I/O, four 32-bit counters, and analog and digital triggering. Onboard NI-STC3 timing and synchronization technology provides advanced timing capabilities, including independent analog and digital timing engines and retriggerable measurement tasks. The PXIe-6386 is suitable for numerous applications such as IF digitization, transient recording, manufacturing test for ISDN, ADSL, and POTS in the telecommunications industry, ultrasound and sonar testing, and high-energy physics. The included NI-DAQmx driver and configuration utility simplify configuration and measurements.
