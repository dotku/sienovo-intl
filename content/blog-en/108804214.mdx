This system utilizes the Xinming XM5728-IDK-V3 AM5728 development board and IoT technology to assist water management by enabling remote, real-time online monitoring of water quality, water level, and flow rate. It provides early warnings for water pollution, statistical analysis of water resource usage, and water level alerts. Through visualization, it organically integrates water management authorities with water supply and drainage infrastructure, forming a "Water Resources Internet of Things." The water quality online monitoring IoT system can promptly analyze and process massive amounts of water-related data, generating actionable insights and decision-making support, thereby managing the entire production, management, and service processes of water systems in a refined and dynamic manner—achieving a "smart" state.

**Features of the Water Resources Monitoring IoT System**

1. Supports real-time monitoring of water level, flow rate, velocity, pressure, and water quality in rivers, lakes, reservoirs, and canals  
2. Supports real-time alarm functions; alarm thresholds can be set for each monitored parameter, with alerts delivered via software notifications, SMS, voice, and other methods  
3. Supports GIS (Geographic Information System) based on electronic maps for unified and intuitive water resource management  
4. Supports storage, analysis, and statistical processing of monitoring data, with visualized output in the form of curves, bar charts, and other reports  
5. Allows viewing of monitoring data curves from various monitoring points, with free zooming and scaling of curves  
6. The intelligent water resource monitoring device supports integration with meters from different manufacturers, including water meters, flow meters, and water level sensors  
7. The intelligent water resource monitoring device supports data storage; when communication signals are poor, monitoring data can be temporarily stored and automatically uploaded once connectivity is restored  
8. The intelligent water resource monitoring device features a waterproof enclosure design  
9. The system implements multi-level permission management, supporting hierarchical access by province, city, county, or department, enabling concurrent management by multiple users with different roles  
10. The system supports data integration with other systems, allowing seamless connection with existing management systems within an organization for unified, integrated management
