Industrial Motherboard Supporting AI Medical Imaging Equipment Applications

In the context of China's accelerating aging population, continuously growing demand for diagnostic imaging, and the successive introduction of national regulations and policies favorable to the development of the high-end medical imaging market, the AI medical imaging equipment industry is entering a golden period of development. Keeping pace with this development trend, we have developed a motherboard suitable for AI medical imaging equipment, based on 12th/13th generation Intel Core processors.

![1702618251956614.jpg](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/140547972/01_c70c2c13a09206ebeac02569327beafa.jpeg)

AI medical imaging is one of the most mature technological fields in the "Healthcare + AI" development. Enhanced by technologies such as computer-aided diagnosis, AI-assisted treatment, and AI quantitative analysis, it enables medical imaging equipment like CT, MRI, X-ray, and ultrasound to achieve multi-functionality, greater precision, higher efficiency, remote capabilities, and integrated diagnosis and treatment. This assists doctors in more efficient, timely, and accurate disease monitoring and diagnosis, with wide-ranging clinical applications.

However, in practical applications, the realization of functions such as high-resolution imaging, real-time or remote image analysis services, and high-efficiency, high-precision screening for various diseases by AI-powered medical imaging equipment requires underlying support from computer hardware that supports AI accelerated computing, possesses excellent multimedia performance, and offers multiple expansion capabilities.

Supporting DDR5 and 1\*PCIe x16 (16-lanes) GEN5 channels, it enables efficient processing of larger datasets and superior image processing/high-definition display. With rich I/O functional interfaces, it provides leading industrial control hardware solutions for customers in the AI medical imaging field.

![华北工控ATX-6159工控主板.png](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/140547972/02_79e3eb02ed1002fca2300d8ef9af53ea.png)

The main technical features of the industrial motherboard are as follows:

Supports Intel Q670/Z690/H610 chipsets and 12th/13th generation Intel Core i3/i5/i7/i9 processors, TDP up to 125W;

Supports up to 4\*UDIMM memory slots (4\*DIMM supported with Q670/Z690 chipsets, 2\*DIMM supported with H610 chipset), supports DDR5 4800MHz, with a maximum of 128GB RAM on the entire board;

Supports 4\*SATA 3.0, and 1\*M_KEY M.2 2280 NVME SSD for high-speed caching and further expanded storage capacity;

Supports integrated Intel Iris® Xe Graphics and Intel® UHD Graphics (model depends on CPU processor), and supports 1\*PCIe x16 (16-lanes) GEN5 to meet the mechanical installation requirements of large graphics cards like RTX3080/4090, allowing high-end graphics cards to deliver ultimate performance;

Supports 1\*VGA, 1\*HDMI, 2\*DP display interfaces, supporting independent multi-display and up to 4K resolution display;

Supports 2\*adaptive Gigabit RJ45 network ports, 1\*M.2 B Key 3042/3052 interface for 4G/5G module access, 1\*M.2 E Key 2230 interface for Wifi/Bluetooth access, and 1\*SIM for wired/wireless network communication;

Supports 8\*RS232, 2\*RS232/RS422/RS485 interfaces for higher-speed data transmission over long/short distances;

Supports 6\*USB3.2 GEN1 5Gbps (4\*USB3.2 GEN1 when equipped with H610 chipset), 7\*USB2.0 (3\*USB2.0 when equipped with H610 chipset) for higher-speed interconnection of multiple smart devices;

Supports 2\*Line out, 2\*Mic in, 1\*Line in, and 1\*AMP amplifier interface, meeting the playback needs of various audio devices;

Supports 4\*PCI for compatibility with traditional data acquisition cards/motion control cards;

Supports 2\*PCIe x4 GEN3 (4-lanes when equipped with Q670/Z690 chipsets, 1-lane when equipped with H610 chipset) for multiple expansion;

Supports 8\*GPIO, 1\*CPUFAN, 1\*SYSFAN, 1\*JFP, 1\*PS/2 interface for connecting more peripherals;

Supports TPM2.0, Intel AMT and Intel vPro Technology for hardware-based security enhancements (this feature is not supported when equipped with Z690/H610 chipsets);

Utilizes AMI EFI BIOS, supports Windows 10 and Linux operating systems, and features a developed software watchdog function to provide a safer and more stable system operating environment;

Supports standard ATX 24Pin +8Pin power supply, meets wide temperature operation requirements from 0℃ to 60℃, and can operate stably under various conditions, including complex electromagnetic environments;

Dimensions are 305mm x 244mm, with high functional integration, easy deployment, and suitability for multiple scenarios.
