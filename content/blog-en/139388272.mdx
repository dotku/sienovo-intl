Due to the unique nature of the gas station industry, oil depots and gas stations often store large quantities of refined fuel to meet sales demands, making the safety of personnel and property paramount. Xinmai Technology's Smart Gas Station Solution focuses on gas station safety, based on digital gas stations, to create a one-stop smart safety gas station solution; the solution covers safety management for service areas and refueling operation zones.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139388272/01_290b41c1a5ef4b727243eee2b8afe928.jpeg)

This solution utilizes intelligent recognition and analysis technologies, such as AI edge computing boxes and cameras, to analyze real-time video content, detect abnormal information, and predict risks, thereby enhancing gas station safety coefficients in the most efficient and direct way. It performs detection for fire/smoke in operation areas, phone call recognition, personnel intrusion, staff behavior compliance, on-duty/off-duty status, smoking recognition, license plate recognition, and more. It processes corresponding situations to prevent incidents before they occur, and its lightweight deployment also facilitates project implementation.

1.  1.  Product Overview

The XM-AIBOX-32 Smart Edge Analysis All-in-One Machine is a high-performance, low-power edge computing product. Equipped with the BM1684X main chip, it delivers up to 32 TOPS INT8 computing power, 16 TFLOPS FP16/BF16 computing power, and 2 TFLOPS FP32 computing power. It can process 32 channels of high-definition video simultaneously, supporting 32 channels of 1080P HD video hardware decoding and 12 channels of encoding.

This product highly integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms based on computer vision and deep learning networks, as well as a comprehensive intelligent video management platform. The AI intelligent algorithms cover various scenarios such as parks, communities, construction sites, and campuses, and can be combined and configured as needed for specific scenarios. The comprehensive intelligent video management platform supports front-end device management, real-time video preview, alarm push, forensic capture, online algorithm loading and optimization, data situational analysis large screen display, and more. The device is easy to operate, plug-and-play, and also features rich northbound API interfaces to empower upper-layer business application platforms.
