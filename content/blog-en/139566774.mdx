## Plug-in Instrument Module: Waveform Generator Module

The waveform generator module is a crucial component in various industrial and scientific applications, including data acquisition, testing, and simulation. ### Key Specifications

The waveform generator module boasts an impressive set of features, including:

* **16-bit resolution**: Providing high accuracy and precision in waveform generation
* **125 MHz refresh rate**: Enabling fast and smooth waveform updates
* **Supports FSK/ASK signal generation**: Facilitating the creation of frequency-shift keying (FSK) and amplitude-shift keying (ASK) signals
* **Generates arbitrary standard or user-defined waveforms**: Allowing for the creation of custom waveforms tailored to specific applications
* **Simulates specific waveforms in special protocol communications**: Enabling the simulation of specific waveforms in special protocol communications, such as wireless charging (signal modulation)

* **Channel**: 1x

* **Output Range**: ± 5 V
* **Coupling**: DC

* **Output Accuracy**: ± (0.5% of Reading + 0.2% of F.S.) (Channel 1) and ± (1% of Reading + 0.2% of F.S.) (Channel 2)
