Medical Endoscope

Endoscopes are inserted into the human body through natural orifices or small surgical incisions. When in use, the endoscope is guided into the organ to be examined, allowing direct visualization of changes in the relevant areas. The quality of the image directly impacts the effectiveness of the endoscope and signifies the level of development in endoscope technology.

A certain company leverages the domestically produced RK3568 core board to support high-end localized medical imaging instruments. With deep customization of kernel drivers, it has overcome USB signal transmission attenuation, achieving stable and clear image quality, dual-screen mirrored/extended display, and adaptability to multiple scenarios, leading to its widespread adoption.

#### Introduction to FSPI and PCIe Buses

FSPI (Flexible Serial Peripheral Interface) is a high-speed, full-duplex, synchronous serial communication bus. The RK3568J processor includes an FSPI controller, which can be used to connect FSPI devices. It has the following features:

(1) Supports serial NOR FLASH and serial NAND FLASH;

(2) Supports SDR mode;

(3) Supports single-wire, dual-wire, and quad-wire modes.

PCIe, or PCI-Express (Peripheral Component Interconnect Express), is a high-speed serial computer expansion bus standard. It is primarily used to expand computer system bus data throughput and enhance device communication speed.
