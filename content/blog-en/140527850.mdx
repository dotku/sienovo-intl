### Rack-mount System

This 2U 19-inch rack-mount system features a high-performance, low-power processor, capable of operating in wide temperature environments. It meets the requirements of European railway application standard EN50155 for electromagnetic compatibility, shock, and vibration tests, making it an ideal product for railway and metro Passenger Information Systems (PIS).

*   *   6th generation embedded low-power, high-performance platform
    *   High-quality railway-specific M12 network interfaces
    *   Easy-maintenance, fully removable expansion bay design
    *   Compliant with EN50155 European railway standard
    *   High-reliability, long-life supercapacitor
