**PCIe Switch Expansion Card**

•  
Host interface: PCIe 4.0 x16  
•  
Data interface: PCIe 4.0 x32  
•  
Connectors: 4×SFF-8654 SlimSAS×8  
•  
NVMe drive support: 8×4, 16×2  
•  
Backplane management: VPP, UBM  
•  
Out-of-band management: I2C, MCTP over I2C/PCIe  
•  
Key features: NVMe drive hot-swap protection  
•  
Status indicators: Heartbeat LED, over-temperature indicator  
•  
Other interfaces: UART serial port, JTAG debug interface, recovery interface, etc.
