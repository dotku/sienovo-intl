**CameraLink development board, supporting both Full mode and Base mode. Image data can be transmitted to an ARM processor via a ribbon cable for processing.**

**FPGA: Virtex-4**

**CameraLink Interface Chip: DS90CR288AMTD, dual-channel**

**Supported Modes: Full mode and Base mode**

**Onboard RAM: IS61LV25616AL\*4 = 1M\*16**

**Onboard VGA Interface: ADV7123**

**Camera Interface: Can be directly connected to common off-the-shelf ARM and DSP development boards via FPC ribbon cable.**

**Peripheral Interfaces: Two serial ports, GPIO ports**
