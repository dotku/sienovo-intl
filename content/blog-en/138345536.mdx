### 10 kS/s/ch, 120 Ω Quarter-Bridge Strain Gauge, 8-Channel C Series Strain/Bridge Input Module

The NI‑9235 can simultaneously measure dynamic strain on all channels, enabling high-speed synchronized measurements. This feature is crucial for applications requiring comparison of multiple channels at a specific moment, such as impact testing.\\n\\nThe NI‑9235 features built-in voltage excitation for quarter-bridge sensors. It also features 60 VDC isolation and 1,000 Vrms transient isolation, providing high common-mode noise rejection and enhanced safety.
