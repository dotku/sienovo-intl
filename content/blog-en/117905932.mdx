IoT Gateway Solution Based on AM335X

*   **Background Analysis**

Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) refers to IoT applications in industrial fields and represents the deep integration of industrial automation and information technology. Compared to general IoT, IIoT has stricter requirements, such as ultra-low latency, high reliability, and strong information security.

A typical IIoT architecture consists of four layers: perception, network, platform, and application. These layers are embodied in intelligent industrial devices, sensors, cloud platforms, smart control devices, gateways, and edge computing systems. Among these components, industrial IoT gateways and cloud platforms play foundational roles, closely tied to the characteristics of IIoT—such as performance, reliability, and information security requirements. Additionally, the gateway's unique topological position makes it ideal for integration with edge computing.

****Pain Points Analysis:****

1. Poor scalability—any new requirement necessitates a complete redesign of the circuit board.
2. Inconvenient remote maintenance.
3. Lack of support for the Linux operating system.

## **2. Solution Features**

To address the challenges outlined above, Xinming proposes the XM-335X-GW development platform based on TI’s AM335x series processor, designed to meet evolving market demands. This platform adopts a core board + baseboard architecture. The XM-335X-GW core board integrates power management ICs, DDR3/DDR3L, eMMC/NAND Flash, EEPROM, and a 160-pin board-to-board signal expansion interface (80×2). The baseboard provides 12V power input, dual Gigabit Ethernet ports, one fiber optic port, LVDS, RGB LCD, a Mini PCI-E interface for 4G LTE, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules, USB HOST, RS485, RS232, and other peripheral interfaces.

### **1.1 Support for Multiple IoT Protocols Enables Seamless Cloud Connectivity**

Designed specifically for gateway applications in the IIoT era, the XM-335X-GW development board supports various IoT protocols such as MQTT and TCP/IP. This simplifies and accelerates edge computing, ensures stable and reliable device connectivity, and enables smart devices to easily connect to the cloud.

### **1.2 Compact Size with Reliable Connectivity**

The XM-335X-GW core board is powered by TI's AM335x series processor, delivering strong performance with a maximum clock speed of 1 GHz. It integrates onboard DDR3, flash/eMMC, EEPROM, and a PMIC power management chip. Designed with an 8-layer high-density, high-speed PCB and using a board-to-board connector (80-pin × 2) for signal expansion, the core board ensures stable and reliable connections while maintaining a compact footprint.

### **1.3 Rich Communication Interfaces + High-Performance HMI Display**

The product supports high-speed communication interfaces including dual Gigabit Ethernet, one fiber optic port, Wi-Fi/Bluetooth, and 4G LTE. It also provides two display options: RGB LCD and LVDS. These features not only meet the high-speed communication requirements of gateway designs but also deliver excellent HMI display capabilities.

### **1.4 Comprehensive Software Support for Easier Development and Greater Expandability**

To assist users in product design, we provide extensive resource support, including:

1. Core board pin definitions, editable baseboard schematics, and editable baseboard PCB files to shorten hardware design cycles;
2. System flashing images, kernel driver source code, file system source code, and a wide range of demo programs;
3. Complete platform development packages and getting-started tutorials to reduce software setup time and ease onboarding;
4. Product design manuals based on the core board, covering hardware design, system software porting, driver development, and application software development environments. These significantly accelerate product development, reduce development cycles by over 50%, and enable product designs in as little as one week;
5. Main chip datasheets, software development SDKs, and software development environments to help you quickly start software development;

## **3. Conclusion**

The Xinming AM335X development board is purpose-built for industrial-grade gateways and features excellent HMI interface design, making it widely applicable across various industries.

As a rapidly growing field, the Industrial Internet of Things is advancing quickly alongside technologies such as 5G and AIoT. We believe the Xinming XM-335X-GW development platform will effortlessly empower diverse devices to connect to the cloud, offering tailored solutions for industrial gateway and HMI designs—enabling high-speed, low-latency, multi-connection operations with enhanced stability and security for ubiquitous connectivity.
