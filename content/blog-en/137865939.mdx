##### **Applications in the Mining Vehicle Industry**

**Background Introduction**
The modern mining industry faces many challenges, with safety being paramount. Whether working underground in mines or on the surface, employee safety must be guaranteed. Therefore, advanced mining vehicles must integrate dedicated in-vehicle computers that can operate stably in extremely harsh mining environments to ensure task completion.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/137865939/01_92fb312954c97ee5beca6ef808474588.png)

**Solution Requirements**
**●** Industrial-grade fanless embedded in-vehicle computers
**●** Equipped with wireless communication and precise GPS positioning capabilities
**●** Accommodate unstable in-vehicle battery voltages of various mining vehicles
**●** Requires connection to various sensor devices via I/O interfaces

**Solution**
In-vehicle computers, designed specifically for the mining industry environment, feature an all-weather rugged design and integrate precise GPS and GIS systems. This ensures that mining vehicles can proactively avoid collisions during transport, thereby reducing accidents. CAN modules are provided for communication with vehicle systems, allowing real-time monitoring of vehicle operating status to ensure proper maintenance. Integrated 3G/WiFi communication capabilities enable timely contact with the dispatch center, allowing managers to obtain information promptly and achieve maximum operational efficiency.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/137865939/02_cb10ca62fa14bff18d8059ba1875886a.png)

****Xinmai Solution Advantages****
**●** A comprehensive range of products is available, offering customers a variety of in-vehicle computers to choose from based on different usage scenarios. These include various display sizes, all-in-one and split-type designs, with touchscreens or integrated keyboards, and different operating systems, allowing customers to select the most suitable in-vehicle computer for their current needs.
**●** Optional wireless cameras / PoE cameras. To enhance driver safety, we have designed cameras that support wireless communication and cameras that can be powered over Ethernet (PoE), enabling drivers to clearly see every corner of large trucks.
**●** Beidou/GPS dual-mode positioning, various wired/wireless sensors
**●** Xinmai facilitates the easy integration of various sensor and image information into the in-vehicle computer, supporting various sensor signal transmission methods on the vehicle (e.g., CAN, Zigbee, RFID, GPIO, Bluetooth, RS-484).
**●** Provides LTE / 3G high-speed wireless network cards. To enhance data communication between the in-vehicle computer and the data center, the in-vehicle computers are equipped with various high-speed wireless network cards, including common 3G and next-generation LTE solutions.
**●** Wide-voltage in-vehicle power supply. To enable the in-vehicle computer to better adapt to the ambulance environment, we designed special features such as wide-voltage power input and delayed power on/off, allowing Xinmai's in-vehicle computers to operate more smoothly in vehicles.

**Industry Applications**
Open-pit mining vehicles, tanker trucks, etc.
