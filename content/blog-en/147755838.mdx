Developed on the Android system, integrating 5G and 4G modules, GPS/BD dual-mode positioning module (high-precision differential inertial navigation), WIFI module, Bluetooth module, RFID module, audio playback, video signal input (AHD or CVBS) module, and other functionalities, this multifunctional in-vehicle positioning and navigation smart information terminal supports vehicle positioning, location tracking, video surveillance, work photo capture, audio monitoring, local video recording, overspeed warning, business data interaction, live voice broadcast, etc. ![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/147755838/01_77b680a558994054a791e392cebb74c7.jpeg)
