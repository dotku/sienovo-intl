**I. Background of the Solution**

**1. The Rise of Fitness Mirrors**  
In 2020, the global pandemic led to widespread lockdowns, making staying at home a key preventive measure. As a result, home workouts surged in popularity, driving demand for home fitness equipment—among which the aesthetically pleasing and multi-functional smart fitness mirror stood out.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/134387450/01_bfd67280147d3567c3ad8a12694439ed.png)

**II. Solution Overview**

**1. Introduction to the Fitness Mirror Solution**

Our fitness mirror solution is built on a hardware mainboard powered by the RK3399 hexa-core chipset. It features a high-performance A72+A53 hexa-core processor with a maximum clock speed of 1.8GHz and a Mali-T860 GPU. On the software side, we've integrated "personalized" fitness programs including dance, aerobic exercise, boxing, running, and martial arts; multimedia features such as music playlists, online challenges, workout leaderboards, and interactive live comments; AI-powered interaction capabilities including AI-based motion counting/timing, voice guidance, real-time human demonstration, and voice-enabled error correction; as well as professional health assessments covering body fat and weight, aerobic capacity, health habits, strength endurance, flexibility, and coordination.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/134387450/02_1d14682b7f1aa4495bf67d1e8e9b9362.png)

**2. Key Features of the Fitness Mirror**

1. **Practicality**: Sleek, streamlined design; when powered off, the large display functions as a full-length mirror ideal for trying on clothes. Supports multimedia playback (music and video) and offers diverse fitness programs including dance, aerobics, boxing, running, and martial arts.

2. **Functionality**:  
   - **Large Display**: Ultra-large HD screen with cinematic visual quality, ensuring no detail is missed.  
   - **Professional Health Assessment**  
   - **Entertainment**: Music and video playback, fitness course streaming, live human demonstrations, etc.  
   - **AI Interaction Features**

3. **Training Effectiveness**:  
   - **Precise Motion Tracking**: Accurately detects 33 skeletal keypoints. Users can follow along with on-screen instructors, with every movement scientifically recorded.  
   - **Effective Error Correction**: Enables scientific, data-driven workouts with real-time feedback.
