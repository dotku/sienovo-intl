### Video Matrix, Video Splicing, Seamless Switching

1.  Supports up to 144 HDMI video inputs and up to 144 HDMI outputs, with full cross-point switching.

2.  Compatible with all HDTV resolutions including 1080p/60, and PC resolutions up to 1920*1200.

3.  Supports HDMI 1.3a, HDCP 1.3, HDCP 1.4, and DVI 1.0 protocols. Supports high color depth and data rates up to 6.5Gbps.

4.  Using high-quality HDMI 1.4 cables, the input transmission distance can reach 20 meters, and the output transmission distance can reach 30 meters.

5.  Features 2 RS-232 communication interfaces; supports RS-232 to RS-485 communication interface.

6.  Supports button control, remote control, video wall software control, touchscreen control, central control, and network control.
