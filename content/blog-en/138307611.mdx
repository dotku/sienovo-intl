## XM-FT2000-VPX Mainboard

The XM-FT2000-VPX mainboard is a standard 6U VPX architecture board card, providing interfaces such as DVI, USB, Ethernet, UART, and PCIe.

*   Processor: FT2000/4 quad-core domestic processor
*   Chipset: Zhaoxin ZX-200 chipset
*   Memory: Domestic memory chips, dual-channel DDR4, 16GB capacity
*   SSD: Onboard mSATA SSD, standard 1TB capacity
*   Interfaces: Supports 6 USB ports, 1 PCIe 2.0 x16 interface, 4 Gigabit Ethernet ports, UART interfaces, etc.
*   Dimensions: 233.35mm (L) × 162mm (H), board thickness 2mm
*   Operating Environment: -40℃ to +65℃ (Industrial grade)
*   Firmware: UEFI BIOS
*   Operating System: Adapted for Galaxy Kylin Operating System (Desktop Edition) 4.0.2 SP4
