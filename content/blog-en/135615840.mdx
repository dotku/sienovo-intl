204.8 kS/s, 114 dB, Two Gain Ranges, AC/DC Coupling, 16-Input PXI Sound and Vibration Module
The PXIe‑4497 is designed for high-channel-count sound and vibration applications. The PXIe‑4497 features 24-bit analog inputs and IEPE constant current signal conditioning, making it ideal for precise measurements using microphones, accelerometers, and other sensors with a large dynamic range. The PXIe‑4497 offers simultaneous sampling on all channels. In addition, the module includes built-in anti-aliasing filters that automatically adjust to the sampling rate, six software-selectable input gain ranges, and TEDS smart sensor support to ensure proper setup and configuration.\\n\\nCommon applications include noise, vibration, and harshness (NVH) analysis, large microphone arrays, and dynamic structural testing.
