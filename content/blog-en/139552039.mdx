### Battery Simulator

*   Rapidly simulates charging and discharging functions of 3C product batteries
*   Outputs flexible and adjustable voltage/current
*   Built-in bidirectional DC-DC buck converter
*   Provides leakage detection for 3C products

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139552039/01_0c4eb1d89b846c295ea0d9eaa4ce9e16.png)

The battery simulator series includes three specifications: single-cell dual-channel (1S), dual-cell dual-channel (2S), and triple-cell single-channel (3S). These models output flexible and adjustable voltage/current for application scenarios with different power requirements, rapidly simulating the charging and discharging functions of 3C product batteries, thereby enabling product testing during the battery-free stage. It also provides leakage detection for 3C products.
