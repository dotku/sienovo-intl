Array Camera - Light Field Camera - AI Binocular Thermal Camera - Infrared AI Recognition Camera

An array camera achieves the imaging effect of a large lens by using multiple small lenses. Its principle is similar to that of array telescopes and insect compound eyes. Compared to traditional cameras, array cameras have a wider field of view, produce larger images, and are smaller in size.
