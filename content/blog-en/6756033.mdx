Shift your mindset: The ability to build things yourself is crucial. No matter how rudimentary, it's an attempt, an endeavor to reach the pinnacle.

Rather than merely modifying someone else's code.

Building upon someone else's foundation offers the benefit of clear results, but the drawback is slower personal growth.

If you build one yourself, though it might be crude, it possesses a unique charm and offers a different kind of experience and satisfaction, giving you a clear understanding of your journey.
