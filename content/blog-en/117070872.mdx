## OMAPL138 Power/HDMI/Camera/UART/LCD Schematics Sharing

The OMAPL138 is a System-on-Chip (SoC) designed by Texas Instruments, featuring a dual-core ARM Cortex-A8 processor, along with various peripherals such as HDMI, camera, UART, and LCD interfaces. In this article, we will explore the schematics of the OMAPL138, focusing on its power, HDMI, camera, UART, and LCD interfaces.

### Schematics Overview

The OMAPL138 schematics can be broken down into several key components:

* Power management: The OMAPL138 features a power management unit (PMU) that regulates power to various peripherals and the processor core.
* HDMI interface: The OMAPL138 supports HDMI 1.3a with a maximum resolution of 1080p at 60Hz.
* Camera interface: The OMAPL138 features a camera interface that supports up to 12-bit RAW image capture.
* UART interface: The OMAPL138 has four UART interfaces, each supporting up to 3 Mbps data transfer rate.
* LCD interface: The OMAPL138 supports LCD interfaces with a maximum resolution of 1080x1920 pixels.
