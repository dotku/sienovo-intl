### 8-Slot USB CompactDAQ Chassis

The cDAQ-9178 is a bus-powered CompactDAQ USB chassis designed specifically for small, portable sensor measurement systems. The chassis allows for easy connection to sensors and electrical measurements via plug-and-play USB. It also controls timing, synchronization, and data transfer between C Series I/O modules and an external host. The chassis can be paired with various combinations of C Series I/O modules to achieve mixed measurements of analog I/O, digital I/O, and counter/timers. The cDAQ-9178 also features four 32-bit general-purpose counter/timers. You can access these counters, as well as shared clocks and triggers, via two built-in BNC connectors.
