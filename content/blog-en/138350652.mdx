The high-speed global shutter camera features a 1-inch global shutter sensor, supporting H.264/H.265 encoding. It achieves up to 50 frames per second (fps) at 8-megapixel resolution, and up to 120 fps in 1080P mode. Powered by the Hi3519AV100 processor, it integrates a 2 TOPS AI computing NPU, enabling extensive AI processing. The camera can capture high-definition, high-frame-rate video of fast-moving objects (up to 180 km/h) with clear imaging, no motion blur, and no distortion. It is primarily used in highway license plate capture and recognition, vehicle violation detection, and sports analysis such as racing and ball games.
