Smart Control Unit for Vending Machines

The vending machine control unit launched by Xinmai is an embedded integrated product that combines control, communication, and display functions for vending machines. Using this control unit can help you quickly build a complete solution for vending machines.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/108369612/01_645ee12fbe8bc31aa8d4fb27843d91ea.png)

Quick Setup, No Waiting

With Xinmai's smart control unit, communication, control, and display for vending machines are all taken care of, shortening the overall R&D time and bringing products to market without delay!

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/108369612/02_110e92e6036f4caae45a219fe24f0ab2.png)

Powerful Core, Performance Guaranteed!

Equipped with TI AM5728 DSP+ARM multi-core high-performance processor, with a main frequency up to 1.5GHz, it easily handles Android systems and high-end processing demands.

Rich Interfaces, Easy Connectivity!

Serial ports, network, audio, USB, 4G, WIFI, MIC, HDMI, and other common interfaces for new retail equipment are all available. Strictly selected IC components and interface protection designs ensure more stable peripheral operation!

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/108369612/03_dc860044cb0fd0b670940a441164f122.png)

HD Display, A New Vision!

The smart control unit is equipped with an HDMI display interface, supporting 1080P resolution. The CPU comes with hardware codec and Open GL 3D graphics acceleration, providing a superb multimedia and human-computer interaction experience!

Stable System, Worry-Free Use!

Supports Android 5.1.1 system. With Xinmai's ten years of embedded and industry technical accumulation, the system kernel and underlying drivers are well-protected, ensuring rock-solid product operation!

Module development and customization based on Xinmai XM5728-IDK-V3.

Features:

*   Based on TI AM5728 floating-point dual DSP C66x + dual ARM Cortex-A15 industrial control and high-performance audio/video processor;
*   Multi-core heterogeneous CPU, integrating dual-core Cortex-A15, dual-core C66x floating-point DSP, dual-core PRU-ICSS, dual-core Cortex-M4 IPU, dual-core GPU and other processing units, supporting OpenCL, OpenMP, SysLink IPC multi-core development;
*   Powerful video encoding and decoding capabilities, supporting 1 channel 1080P60 or 2 channels 720P60 or 4 channels 720P30 hardware video encoding/decoding, and H.265 software video decoding;
*   Supports up to 1 channel 1080P60 full HD video input and 1 channel LCD + 1 channel HDMI 1.4a output;
*   Dual-core PRU-ICSS industrial real-time control subsystem, supporting EtherCAT, EtherNet/IP, PROFIBUS and other industrial protocols;
*   High-performance GPU, dual-core SGX544 3D accelerator and GC320 2D graphics acceleration engine, supporting OpenGL ES2.0;
*   Rich peripheral interfaces, integrating dual Gigabit Ethernet, PCIe, GPMC, USB 2.0, UART, SPI, QSPI, SATA 2.0, I2C, DCAN and other industrial control buses and interfaces, supporting high-speed USB 3.0 interface;
*   The development board leads out a V-PORT video interface, allowing flexible access to video input modules;
*   Extremely small size, only 86.5mm*60.5mm;
*   Industrial-grade precision B2B connector, 0.5mm pitch, stable, easy to plug and unplug, anti-reverse insertion, critical large data interfaces use high-speed connectors to ensure signal integrity.
