Board Overview
The FMC_XM134 is a 4-channel 3.2 GSPS (or configurable to 2-channel 6.4 GSPS) 12-bit ADC acquisition FMC+ daughter card module. This board adheres to the FMC+ standard and complies with the VITA57.4 specification. It can be coupled as an ideal I/O module to the FPGA front-end. After digitization, RF analog signals are transmitted to the FPGA via a 16-lane JESD204B interface. The board supports a maximum of 2 channels of 6.4 GSPS RF direct sampling, with a -3dB analog input bandwidth of up to 8 GHz. Signals with instantaneous bandwidths below 3 GHz can all be digitized within the first Nyquist zone.

The board supports an on-board programmable sampling clock and an external reference clock. Multiple boards can also perform synchronized acquisition via trigger (input/output) signals. The board's 4 analog signal inputs are connected via 50Ω characteristic impedance SSMC RF connectors, using AC transformer coupling. The board can be widely used in radar signal processing, electronic warfare, and other applications.

Technical Specifications
- Performance Indicators:
  - Supports 4 channels, 12-bit, 3.2 GSPS sampling rate;
  - Configurable to 2 channels, 12-bit, 6.4 GSPS sampling;
  - ADC Analog Input Bandwidth: 8 GHz (-3dB);
  - ADC Full-Scale Input Voltage: 0.8 Vpp;
  - ADC Noise Floor: Single-channel mode -154.6 dBFS/Hz;
  - ADC Noise Floor: Dual-channel mode -151.8 dBFS/Hz;
  - Supports on-chip Digital Down-Converter (DDC) function in dual-channel mode;
  - Digital Interface: 16-lane JESD204B interface, line rate 12.8 Gbps;
- FMC Interface Specifications:
  - Standard FMC+ daughter card, compliant with VITA57.4 specification;
  - Board supports 1 EEPROM;
  - FMC Connector Model: ASP-184330-01, HPC interface;
  - Board powered by +12V, typical power consumption 6W;
- Clock Distribution:
  - Supports external clock mode;
  - On-board 1 high-precision clock chip HMC7044;
- Other Features:
  - Supports external trigger;
  - On-board status indicator lights;
- Physical and Electrical Characteristics
  - Board Dimensions: 84.1x69mm
  - Board Power Supply: 3Amax@+12V (±5%)
  - Cooling Method: Natural air cooling or metal conduction cooling
- Environmental Characteristics
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C;
  - Storage Temperature: -55°C to +125°C;
  - Operating Humidity: 5%~95%, non-condensing
