Power Data Concentrator

ARM Solution Overview: The power data concentrator, along with remote smart meters and data collectors, forms a remote meter reading, control, and management system. The concentrator is a data aggregation device for a meter reading area, used to collect and store data from water, electricity, gas, and heat meters. The concentrator has both an upstream and a downstream channel. The upstream channel can use GPRS wireless, telephone line, or wired LAN to achieve remote communication with the management center. The downstream channel uses low-power wireless, RS485, or pulse methods to collect, process, and store data from the collectors under its jurisdiction. The AM5728 core board can easily implement all the necessary functions for a concentrator.

Solution Implementation:

Module development and customization based on Xinmai XM5728-IDK-V3.

Features:

*   Based on TI AM5728 floating-point dual DSP C66x + dual ARM Cortex-A15 industrial control and high-performance audio/video processor;
*   Multi-core heterogeneous CPU, integrating dual-core Cortex-A15, dual-core C66x floating-point DSP, dual-core PRU-ICSS, dual-core Cortex-M4 IPU, dual-core GPU and other processing units, supporting OpenCL, OpenMP, SysLink IPC multi-core development;
*   Powerful video encoding and decoding capabilities, supporting 1 channel 1080P60 or 2 channels 720P60 or 4 channels 720P30 hardware video encoding/decoding, and H.265 software video decoding;
*   Supports up to 1 channel 1080P60 full HD video input and 1 channel LCD + 1 channel HDMI 1.4a output;
*   Dual-core PRU-ICSS industrial real-time control subsystem, supporting industrial protocols such as EtherCAT, EtherNet/IP, PROFIBUS;
*   High-performance GPU, dual-core SGX544 3D accelerator and GC320 2D graphics acceleration engine, supporting OpenGL ES2.0;
*   Rich peripheral interfaces, integrating dual Gigabit Ethernet, PCIe, GPMC, USB 2.0, UART, SPI, QSPI, SATA 2.0, I2C, DCAN and other industrial control buses and interfaces, supporting high-speed USB 3.0 interface;
*   The development board leads out a V-PORT video interface, allowing flexible connection to video input modules;
*   Extremely compact size, only 86.5mm*60.5mm;
*   Industrial-grade precision B2B connector, 0.5mm pitch, stable, easy to plug and unplug, anti-reverse insertion, high-speed connectors used for critical large data interfaces to ensure signal integrity.
