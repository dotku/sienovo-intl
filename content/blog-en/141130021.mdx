I. Medical Diagnostic Devices

With the increasing abundance of biochemical reagents, immunodiagnostic reagents, and molecular diagnostic reagents, domestic medical diagnostic devices have seen significant market demand. The technological development of diagnostic devices has evolved from microcontroller-based processing to Cortex-A53 processing, with a gradual increase in parallel processing channels, leading to a noticeable improvement in both detection speed and efficiency.

II. Immunodiagnostics: The Most Diverse Category

Immunodiagnostic reagents constitute the largest category among all diagnostic reagents. They can be categorized by application into infectious diseases, endocrinology, oncology, drug monitoring, and blood typing. They can also be classified by method, such as chemiluminescence, colloidal gold, EIA, and so on.
