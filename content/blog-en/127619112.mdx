RK3568/3288/3399 + HarmonyOS is widely used in Information Technology Application Innovation (Xinchuang) industries such as industrial tablets and military-grade medical equipment, supporting software and hardware customization!

It not only features a domestically developed operating system and stylish design but also offers rich interfaces and powerful core functions, complemented by a 10.1-inch LCD display.

Application scenarios include: smart manufacturing, smart finance, smart transportation, self-service terminals, and more.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/127619112/01_d1bd5e1ccbe4f900ba9e8dc75250042d.png)

The XM3568A motherboard adopts the Rockchip RK3568 quad-core 64-bit Cortex-A55 processor, with a main frequency up to 2.0GHz, offering significantly improved performance. It utilizes advanced 22nm process technology, characterized by low power consumption and high performance.
It features a dual-core architecture GPU, high-performance VPU, and NPU. It supports OpenGL ES3.2 /2.0 /1.1, Vulkan1.1; 4K 60fps H.265 /H.264/VP9 video decoding and 1080P 100fps H.265/H.264 video encoding.
 

Rich expansion interfaces: It features 2 USB3.0, 8 USB2.0, MiPi DSI, eDP, I2C, UART, ADC, GPIO, and other interfaces, which can be used for external device control and expansion.
It supports upgrading to Android and Linux operating systems and can boot the system via USB drives, EMMC, etc. The diverse system support makes entertainment, office work, programming learning, and creative development easy and flexible.

Application Cases:

The HarmonyOS AIoT-5G Training Kit and Experiment Platform is based on Huawei's typical IoT technical architecture, supported by 5G communication technology, with terminals adapted to the HarmonyOS operating system. It supports multi-scenario applications such as smart manufacturing, smart cities, enterprise intelligence, and intelligent connected vehicles.
