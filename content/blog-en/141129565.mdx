Fully Automatic Fluorescence Immunoassay Analyzer: Fluorescence immunoassay technology offers advantages such as strong specificity, high sensitivity, and good practicality. Therefore, it is used to measure very low concentrations of bioactive compounds, such as proteins (enzymes, receptors, antibodies), hormones (steroids, thyroid hormones, phthalate hormones), drugs, and microorganisms, etc.

Fully Automatic Fluorescence Immunoassay Analyzer

IMX6ULL Core Board

Fully automatic fluorescence immunoassay analyzers are used for in vitro diagnostics in hospitals, primarily for testing human serum, plasma, whole blood, and urine samples.

The IMX6ULL core board adopts an industrial-grade solution specifically tailored for low-power, low-cost, and compact applications. It is ideal for energy-saving and compact applications such as HMI, control systems, and IoT. With Linux system support, you can choose a cost-optimized core board configuration based on actual project requirements to meet various application and cost demands.
