(2) LCD Driver Porting

——————————

1. Statically Load LCD Driver
  　　Place the written LCD driver program lcd.c into the arm/linux/drivers/char directory. Modify the arm/linux/drivers/char/config.in file, adding the line: Bool LCD driver support CONFIG_LCD. Modify the arm/linux/drivers/char/Makefile file, adding the line: obj-$(CONFIG_LCD)+=lcd.o.
  　　This way, when you run `make xconfig` again, you will have the option to compile the LCD driver into the kernel. The same method can also be applied to other devices.
