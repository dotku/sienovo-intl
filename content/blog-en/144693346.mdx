2-Channel Voltage 50 MS/s USB 3.0 Data Acquisition Card

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144693346/01_6a4dd1db28614dd59bfa4ebd931e9783.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144693346/02_b3d2d59f7f214d72af996894e378f1d5.png)

This is a 16-bit high-speed USB 3.0 data acquisition card, designed with an XC7A100T-2FGG484I FPGA. It features a total of 2 channels, with each channel supporting a maximum sampling rate of 50 MS/s.
