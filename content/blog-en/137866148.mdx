##### **Agricultural Vehicle Industry Applications**

**Background Introduction**
The primary function of current in-vehicle computers for agricultural vehicles is to achieve precise positioning and navigation. More advanced systems aim to enable autonomous driving for agricultural vehicles and communicate with relevant sensors (e.g., soil depth sensors, fuel level sensors, etc.) to realize more automated and intelligent functions.

**Solution Requirements**
● Industrial-grade fanless embedded vehicle PC
● Equipped with wireless communication capabilities and precise GPS positioning
● Accommodates unstable on-board battery voltage of various agricultural vehicles
● Requires connection to various sensor devices via I/O interfaces

**Solution**
To meet the demands of agricultural vehicles, Xinmai has designed a series of rugged in-vehicle computers. These units feature a robust, fanless design to withstand harsh outdoor environments and offer a user-friendly interface for easy driver operation. Furthermore, their rich I/O interfaces enable communication between the in-vehicle computer and autonomous driving actuators, differential positioning modules, and various sensors, thereby fulfilling the customer's required functions. In addition, we also provide supporting power modules, mounting brackets, and various antennas, making it more convenient for customers to install the computer on agricultural vehicles and implement various predefined functions.

##### **Steel Mill Overhead Crane Industry Applications**

**Background Introduction**
With continuous societal development, overhead cranes play a crucial role as the primary material handling equipment in steelmaking and continuous casting production. Their operational processes, including changes in position, weight, and process flow, are closely linked.
For steelmaking and continuous casting areas, the most important basic data are hot metal charging volume, scrap steel charging volume, tapping weight, and casting weight. The overhead crane logistics system uses computers to collect, analyze, and process signals from the overhead crane scales, ensuring data authenticity and overhead crane positioning monitoring.

**Solution Requirements**
● Industrial-grade touch display all-in-one vehicle PC
● Equipped with wireless communication capabilities and high-strength vibration resistance
● Requires connection to various sensor devices via I/O interfaces

**Solution**
Xinmai's vehicle PCs are specifically designed for the harsh operating environments of steel mill overhead cranes. They feature an all-weather protection rating, solid-state drives, and a high-strength shock-absorbing structural design to prevent system failures during crane operation. They provide various I/O interfaces such as COM, LAN, DI/DO, etc., for connecting external monitoring modules, real-time data acquisition, and monitoring the crane's operating status. Integrated built-in 4G/WiFi communication capabilities enable timely contact with the dispatch center, allowing managers to obtain information promptly and achieve maximum operational efficiency.

**Solution Advantages**
● Solves the problem of large weighing structural errors caused by inaccurate weighing from ground track scales due to environmental factors, and improves data acquisition accuracy.
