Driven by the wave of Industry 4.0, traditional manufacturing industries face numerous challenges such as rising raw material and labor costs, disorganized shop floor management, complex order structures, difficulty in improving production efficiency, and inability to monitor production anomalies in real time. These issues make it impossible to meet the basic requirements of digital factories, let alone align with Industry 4.0 standards.

MES (Manufacturing Execution System) is an information management system designed for the shop-floor execution layer of manufacturing enterprises. MES provides enterprises with a comprehensive set of management modules, including manufacturing data management, production planning and scheduling, production dispatching, inventory management, quality management, human resource management, work center/equipment management, tooling management, procurement management, cost management, project dashboard management, production process control, bottom-level data integration and analysis, and upper-level data integration and decomposition. This creates a solid, reliable, comprehensive, and practical collaborative manufacturing management platform.

To meet these demands, the Kontron 15.6-inch industrial all-in-one tablet has emerged as an ideal solution, with the following product features:

Key Requirements and Features:

1. IP65-rated front panel on the industrial tablet, dustproof and waterproof design, supporting both embedded and desktop mounting.

2. Low-power 6th/7th generation Intel CPU with fanless design and large-area heat dissipation fins.

3. High-brightness display equipped with a highly sensitive industrial touchscreen, with a touch lifespan of up to 35 million touches.

4. Built-in auto power-on function, ensuring long-term stable operation of the operating system.

5. All data transmission interfaces use industrial-grade locking connectors, strictly complying with industrial standards.

6. Rich I/O interfaces on the MES workstation enable connections to printers, barcode scanners, and other peripheral devices.

7. Low overall power consumption for extended energy efficiency and environmental sustainability.

8. Strong anti-interference and filtering capabilities, ensuring long-term stable operation and 24/7 uninterrupted performance even in harsh environments.

9. Equipped with 6 RS232 serial ports, dual Ethernet ports, HDMI + VGA dual display outputs, and 6 USB ports for versatile application support.
