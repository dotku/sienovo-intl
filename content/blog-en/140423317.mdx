A 2-port 10G Fibre Channel adapter based on PCI Express bus architecture. This product is a half-height, half-length PCIe card, suitable for mainstream half-height server chassis or micro-workstations.
The board uses a Xilinx 28nm Kintex-7 series FPGA as the main processor, features one 64-bit DDR3 SDRAM as high-speed cache, and the front panel has two SFP+ optical transceivers, providing 2x 10G Fibre Channel or Ethernet data transceiver capabilities. The board uses air cooling and has temperature monitoring functionality. It can be widely applied in scenarios such as optical data acquisition and high-performance computing (HPC).

#### Key Features

*   Standard PCI Express half-height, half-length card, suitable for half-height chassis;
*   Complies with PCI Express Gen2.0 specification, optional x1, x4, or x8 modes, theoretical bandwidth up to 40Gbps;
*   Supports 2-port 10 Gigabit Fibre Channel network interfaces, maximum line rate of 10Gbps/lane, theoretical transmission bandwidth up to 2GByte/s, supports multiple high-speed protocols such as Aurora and RapidIO;
*   Onboard one 64-bit 2GByte DDR3 SDRAM memory, capable of high-speed data caching at an 800MHz clock rate, theoretical bandwidth up to 12.8GByte/s, DDR3 SDRAM read/write efficiency up to 90%;
*   Onboard 8-bit DIP switch, allowing selection of board operating modes via the DIP switch;
*   The board features 4-channel opto-isolated digital discrete inputs and 4-channel opto-isolated digital discrete outputs;
*   Onboard high-precision active temperature-compensated crystal oscillator (TCXO), temperature stability ±0.28ppm, aging rate ±1ppm;
*   Onboard one 32MByte SPI Flash for storing small amounts of parameter information;
*   Onboard one 128MByte BPI Nor Flash for FPGA loading;

#### Interface Features

*   Front panel supports 2 SFP+ Cages (maximum support 10Gbps/lane);
*   Front panel supports 2 LED indicators;
