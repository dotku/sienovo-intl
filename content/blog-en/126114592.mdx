RK3568 + HarmonyOS Industrial Tablet and Digital Signage Solution Design.

Equipped with RK3568 quad-core 64-bit processor

8GB RAM to meet demanding requirements

Supports Android, Linux, OpenHarmony

RS232/485, USB2.0, USB3.0, 2*GPIO

Dual Gigabit Ethernet ports

RK3568/3288/3399 + HarmonyOS is widely applied in industrial tablets, military, medical, and other domestic IT innovation industries, supporting software and hardware customization!

Not only does it feature a domestic operating system and a sleek design, but it also offers rich interfaces, powerful core functions, and comes with a 10.1-inch LCD display.

Application scenarios include: Smart Manufacturing, Smart Finance, Smart Transportation, Self-service Terminals, etc.

 ![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/126114592/01_d1bd5e1ccbe4f900ba9e8dc75250042d.png)

The XM3568A motherboard adopts the Rockchip RK3568 quad-core 64-bit Cortex-A55 processor, with a main frequency of up to 2.0GHz, offering significantly improved performance. It utilizes advanced 22nm process technology, characterized by low power consumption and high performance.
It features a dual-core architecture GPU, high-performance VPU, and NPU. It supports OpenGL ES3.2 /2.0 /1.1, Vulkan1.1; 4K 60fps H.265 /H.264/VP9 video decoding and 1080P 100fps H.265/H.264 video encoding.
 

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/126114592/02_c4681bb57bdc828ec73e38d248e5eae1.png)

Rich expansion interfaces: It features 2x USB3.0, 8x USB2.0, MiPi DSI, eDP, I2C, UART, ADC, GPIO, and other interfaces, which can be used for external device control and expansion.
It supports upgrading Android and Linux operating systems, and can boot the system via USB drives, EMMC, and other methods. The rich and diverse system support makes entertainment, office work, programming learning, and creative development easy and flexible.
