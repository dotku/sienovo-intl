**Project Background**

China's road network is vast, with a total mileage exceeding 5.35 million kilometers, and its expressway mileage ranks among the highest in the world. Facing the continuous growth of infrastructure stock, highway maintenance and management has entered a new era of an integrated approach to construction, management, and maintenance. As maintenance expenditures increase year by year, improving maintenance efficiency and reducing management costs have become urgent issues.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139387967/01_df0f8b88a8f92f591ee86ee80cbb48c7.jpeg)

**Challenges Faced**

1. High-Frequency Inspections and Labor Costs: Daily inspection work is arduous, leading to high labor costs.

2. Data Collection and Management: Manual data collection and recording are highly subjective, inefficient, and pose safety risks to personnel.

3. Limitations of Annual Inspections: While reliable, annual inspections are costly and infrequent, failing to meet real-time demands.

**Policy Directives**

The "14th Five-Year Plan for Highway Maintenance and Management Development Outline" emphasizes promoting facility digitalization and enhancing the digital level of maintenance management, especially AI-based automated inspections and IoT-driven maintenance project quality management.

**Solution**

For such application scenarios, Xinmai Technology, in collaboration with a software company, jointly developed a lightweight smart highway inspection solution. The inspection equipment and system collect road surface image data via vehicle-mounted cameras; utilize edge computers for AI image analysis to efficiently and accurately identify road safety risks and defects; upload images via object storage HTTP(s) protocol interfaces; upload metadata via MQTT protocol; simultaneously, combining 5G/4G communication and high-precision positioning technology, data is uploaded to the cloud in real-time, providing comprehensive and real-time assessment and display of road conditions. In cases of poor 4G/5G network conditions, the system caches data and resumes transmission later. The device has a built-in media server, allowing mobile apps to access and play real-time inference videos via RTSP protocol.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139387967/05_8dd00c775987aa9588b36ec7e223f7ed.png)

**Technical Advantages**

1. **High Efficiency**: AI intelligent analysis improves inspection efficiency by 10 times;

2. **High Accuracy**: Detection rate > 91.6%, false positive rate < 5.4%, area calculation error < 8.6%, ensuring data accuracy;

3. **Real-time Response**: Real-time detection, real-time reporting, second-level response;

4. **High Reliability**: Provides mechanisms such as caching and resumable transmission, ensuring secure and stable data transmission regardless of network environment limitations;

5. **Remote Controllability**: Built-in VPN and mirrored deployment enable remote upgrades and maintenance.

6. **Rich Algorithm Models**: Supports integrated management of multi-scenario, multi-version AI algorithms, including but not limited to: road debris detection, road water accumulation and icing detection, slope rockfall monitoring, roadside weather observation, and road asset inventory.

**Customer Benefits**

1. **Comprehensive Road Condition Overview**: Real-time monitoring and assessment of road conditions through daily inspections.

2. **Leap in Inspection Efficiency**: Significantly improves work efficiency, reduces labor costs, and mitigates safety hazards.

3. **Real-time Risk Warning**: Real-time detection of road risks through vehicle-mounted edge intelligent analysis, allowing for proactive preventive measures.

4. **Scientific Decision Support**: Provides scientific decision-making basis for maintenance management based on accumulated daily effective data, reducing maintenance costs.

**Implementation Results**

The lightweight smart highway inspection solution has been successfully implemented in 12 provinces and cities nationwide, gaining widespread recognition from customers.
