‌**The Power Communication Management Unit Solution**‌ is a design and application scheme for communication management units in power systems, aiming to enhance the communication efficiency and stability of power systems. The Communication Management Unit (also known as DPU) features multiple downstream communication interfaces and one or more upstream network interfaces, acting as a front-end processor. It is used to collect and consolidate communication data from all intelligent monitoring/protection devices within a substation and transmit it in real-time to the upper-level master station system, fulfilling functions such as tele-signaling and tele-measurement. Simultaneously, it can receive commands issued by backend machines or DCS and forward them to intelligent series units within the substation to achieve tele-control and tele-adjustment functions‌12.

#### Typical Application Scenarios

Communication management units are widely used in power demand-side management, industrial energy management, building energy management, photovoltaic monitoring, power operation and maintenance, and other systems. It serves as a data hub between various levels of intelligent devices and the upper-level monitoring center, ensuring real-time data transmission and remote control‌12. For example, in substations and dispatching centers, the communication management unit controls downstream RTU devices through a control platform to collect information such as tele-signals and tele-measurements, and feeds messages back to the dispatching center. After processing and analyzing the messages, administrators execute commands to achieve the goal of remote output of dispatching commands‌2.

‌**Key Features of the Communication Management Unit include**‌:

*   ‌**Data Acquisition and Processing**‌: Controls downstream RTU devices through a control platform to achieve the collection and feedback of information such as tele-signals, tele-measurements, and tele-control‌2.
*   ‌**Station Monitoring**‌: Includes common SCADA functions such as station data acquisition and processing, operation monitoring and alarm recording, human-machine interaction and system maintenance management, calculation and statistics, historical data recording, and Sequence of Events (SOE) recording‌2.
*   ‌**Control and Regulation Functions**‌: Allows control operations such as opening/closing, engaging/disengaging for controllable devices, and adjusting controls such as raising/lowering, and setpoint configuration for adjustable devices, via the keyboard on the screen display‌2.
*   ‌**Fault Recording and Five-Prevention Interlock**‌: Features fault recording functionality, enhancing automatic safety and preventing misoperations‌2.

‌**Technical Parameters and Standards**‌: The communication management unit is compatible with various communication protocols, such as Modbus, IEC101, IEC103, IEC104, DLT645, etc., ensuring reliable operation in demanding industrial environments like power and energy. Furthermore, the product is equipped with network security protection mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access and attacks, ensuring the security of the communication system‌3.
