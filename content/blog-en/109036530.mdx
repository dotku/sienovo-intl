## **Edge AI Gateway Accelerates Air Compressor Applications**

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/109036530/01_8ed6339a79ac3cfb39b7e402af6b96a4.png)

The number of devices connected to the internet is growing daily. A recent report by Strategy Analytics predicts that 38.6 billion devices will be connected to the IoT by 2025. The rapid development of the IoT wave has led to the generation of massive amounts of data, and edge computing gateways are quickly becoming a critical component for device management, data collection, and transmission. To address this, Advantech has launched an edge computing gateway based on the TI Sitara AM5728 Cortex-A15 processor.

As a barometer of China's economic development, the air compressor industry widely applies air compressors in metallurgy, electric power, petroleum, and other industries for blast furnace blowing, pulverized coal conveying for blast furnaces and boilers, and ventilation for mines and power plant cooling towers. As the industry moves towards systematization and energy efficiency, there is an urgent need to improve air compressor production management and reduce maintenance costs through device networking and remote monitoring.

Air compressor manufacturers want a solution that can acquire real-time equipment operating data (temperature, pressure, vibration, and operating hours) and perform compressor analysis, life assessment, and predictive maintenance. Since air compressors are often installed in harsh environments lacking Ethernet infrastructure, **the suitable edge computing gateway must meet the following requirements: wide voltage input for unstable power supplies; a wide operating temperature range to withstand harsh environments; and wireless expansion capabilities to simplify network deployment. Additionally, cloud-based services are needed for easy monitoring and multi-user access control.**

With the XM5728-GW-Edge edge computing gateway, customers can improve air compressor production management and reduce maintenance costs through diverse interfaces, flexible installation, device networking, and remote monitoring.

**Industrial-grade Palm-sized Edge Computing Gateway Withstands Harsh Environments**

The Xinmai XM5728-GW-Edge edge computing gateway is equipped with a TI Sitara AM5728 Cortex-A15 processor, providing high computing efficiency, low power consumption, and excellent cost-effectiveness. It also features multiple I/O interfaces, including 2 Gigabit Ethernet, 2 RS-232/422/485, and 6 GPIOs, meeting the overall requirements for an embedded edge computing gateway. It also has a wide power input and operating temperature range, protecting the system from damage due to unstable power supplies and ensuring stable operation in industrial field environments.

**Efficient Deployment, Cloud-based Management of Real-time Device Status**

In terms of software, it supports intelligent device maintenance management software, which not only enables effective and reliable remote monitoring but also tracks device operating status and supports wireless network software updates, thereby improving operation and maintenance efficiency.
