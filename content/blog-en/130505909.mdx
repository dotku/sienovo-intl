An 8K smart camera with graphene-based heat dissipation has been designed. It uses the Rockchip RK3588 as the main control unit and a Sony IMX435 as the sensor. Images captured by the sensor are sent to an 8K display via HDMI 2.1, achieving end-to-end 8K presentation. To ensure stable camera performance, this design employs graphene for heat dissipation.
