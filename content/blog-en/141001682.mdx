**Specification Sheet for Domestically Produced Phytium D2000 Motherboard**

1.  ****Product Overview****

The XM-F611 is a discrete graphics pluggable OPS PC motherboard independently developed and designed by our company, compliant with the Intel OPS (Open Pluggable Specification) standard. It features a Phytium Tengrui D2000 octa-core processor and a domestically produced discrete graphics card, paired with domestic Galaxy Kylin or UOS operating systems. It offers functions such as synchronous display, broadcasting, and 4K@60Hz ultra-high-definition display, providing customers with higher display rates, shorter latency, and better display effects.

This product offers advantages such as easy installation, easy maintenance, and easy upgrades. This OPS PC host is widely used in multi-functional electronic conferencing systems, large screen splicing, facial recognition, digital signage, interactive flat panel displays for education, multimedia advertising machines, interactive electronic whiteboards, security monitoring, and other industries.
