#### 32TOPS Edge Intelligent Gateway Launched, Empowering Digital Transformation Across Multiple Industries, Reducing O&M Costs and Increasing Efficiency, Powered by the Flagship RK3588 Chip

The XM-RK3588 Edge Intelligent Gateway, powered by the Rockchip RK3588 chip, offers computing power expandable to 32TOPS, **making it suitable for digital transformation in fields such as electric power and energy, smart transportation, smart cities, smart security, smart healthcare, and industrial internet.**

Currently, Xinmai has launched a series of edge intelligent gateway products based on Rockchip RK3568, RK3566, RV1126, and other platforms. These products cover various computing power requirements and cater to high-end, mid-range, and entry-level performance needs with dual or multi-network ports. They have been deployed and applied in areas such as intelligent O&M, digital cities, smart security, smart factories, and smart transportation. The highlights and application cases of several edge intelligent gateway products will be introduced in detail below.

**I. Edge Intelligent Gateway Application Cases: Empowering Digital Transformation Across Multiple Industries**

In edge computing applications, by applying new technologies such as IoT, big data, and artificial intelligence through video structuring to general security fields like autonomous driving, industrial inspection, unmanned patrol, and intelligent O&M, and by adapting AI algorithms to specific application scenarios and intelligently upgrading existing monitoring equipment, rapid deployment and implementation are enabled. This maximizes the analysis of existing data to obtain precise data for management decisions.

Based on Rockchip's multi-chip platforms, edge intelligent gateways have now been deployed and applied in multiple industries.
