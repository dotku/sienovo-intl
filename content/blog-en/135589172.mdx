PXIe, 208-channel AI (16-bit, 3.8 MS/s), 2-channel AO, 24-channel DIO, PXI Multifunction I/O Module
The PXIe‑6375 provides analog I/O, digital I/O, and four 32-bit counter/timers for applications such as PWM, encoders, frequency measurement, and event counting. This device leverages a high-throughput PCI Express bus and multicore-optimized drivers and application software to deliver high-performance capabilities. Onboard NI‑STC3 timing and synchronization technology provides advanced timing features, including independent analog and digital timing engines and retriggerable measurement tasks. The PXIe‑6375 is suitable for a wide range of applications, from basic data logging to control and test automation. The included NI‑DAQmx driver and configuration utility simplify configuration and measurements.
