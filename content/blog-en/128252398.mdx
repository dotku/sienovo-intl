## XM-5131 Wall-mounted Fanless Industrial PC

The XM-5131 is a wall-mounted fanless industrial PC based on Intel 6th/7th generation Core i-series platform CPUs, featuring expandable memory slots, 1× mSATA, 3× Gigabit Ethernet ports, 6× COM ports, 4× USB 3.0, and 4× USB 2.0. This product is suitable for wall-mounted applications such as industrial robot controllers and machine vision controllers.
