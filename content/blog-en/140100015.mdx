Application Scenarios

With the advancement of semiconductor technology, the demand for higher chip yield rates is increasing. However, the manufacturing process for integrated circuit chips is complex, and many defects often arise during their production. Therefore, defect detection is an essential process in integrated circuit manufacturing.
