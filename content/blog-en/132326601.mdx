The self-developed EtherCAT bus 4-axis open-loop stepper driver can connect to TwinCAT, Omron PLCs, Inovance PLCs, CODESYS, SOEM master, and IgH master.
Each motor axis supports a maximum drive current of 4A and can drive 42/57/86 series motors. It features 12 digital inputs and 8 digital outputs.
The EtherCAT bus supports a 125us Distributed Clock (DC) synchronization cycle. It supports multiple dynamic PDOs, dynamic Object Dictionary configuration, saving of ID station number settings, and CSP, CSV, and homing modes.
This is a low-cost EtherCAT bus driver solution.
