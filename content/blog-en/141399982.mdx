### 25 kS/s, 24-bit, 8-Channel PXI Strain/Bridge Input Module

The PXIe-4330 is a synchronous input module that provides integrated data acquisition and signal conditioning for bridge-based sensors. With its higher accuracy, high data throughput, and synchronous capabilities, the PXIe-4330 is an ideal choice for high-density measurement systems.

To eliminate noise, each channel of the PXIe-4330 provides anti-aliasing and digital filters. Each channel also features an independently programmable excitation voltage. In addition, the PXIe-4330 offers remote sensing, internal bridge completion, and shunt calibration options for each channel.
