1.  1.  Product Overview

The XM-AIBOX-8 AI Smart Edge Analysis All-in-One Machine is a high-performance, low-power edge computing product. Equipped with the domestic TPU chip BM1684, it boasts an INT8 computing power of up to 10.6 TOPS, can simultaneously process 8 channels of HD video, and supports hardware decoding for 8 channels of 1080P HD video and encoding for 1 channel.

This product highly integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms based on computer vision and deep learning networks, as well as a comprehensive video intelligent management platform. The AI intelligent algorithms cover various scenarios such as industrial parks, communities, construction sites, and campuses, and can be combined and configured on demand according to the specific scenario. The comprehensive video intelligent management platform supports front-end device management, real-time video preview, alarm push, forensic capture, online algorithm loading and optimization, data situational analysis large screen display, and more. The device is easy to operate, plug-and-play, and offers rich northbound API interfaces to empower upper-layer business application platforms.

1.  1.  Product Features

**Ultra-High Performance Computing and Codec Capabilities**

*   Supports peak INT8 computing power of up to 10.6 TOPS or high-precision FP32 computing power of 1.3 TFLOPS;
*   Supports full-process handling of up to 8 channels of 1080P HD video;
*   Supports hardware decoding for up to 8 channels of H.264/H.265 1080P@25FPS video;
*   Supports hardware encoding for up to 1 channel of H.264/H.2
