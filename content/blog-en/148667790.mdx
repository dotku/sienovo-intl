The following outlines the key technical features and solutions of the fully domestic, ultra-compact RK3576 core board and its supported **RK3576+FPGA collaborative scheme**:

* * *

#### **I. Core Board Hardware Specifications and Domestic Production Characteristics**

1.  **Ultra-Compact Design**
    
    *   Dimensions are only **40×60mm** (B2B connector package), and the stamp-hole package (IDO-SOM7609-S1) offers even more compact dimensions, meeting high-density integration requirements.
    *   Integrates RK3576J (industrial-grade) / RK3576 (commercial-grade) chip, PMIC power management, LPDDR4/4X memory, and eMMC/UFS storage, achieving full-function integration on a single board.
2.  **100% Domestic Production**
    
    *   All components (chips, storage, power management, etc.) adopt domestic solutions and have passed industrial-grade reliability tests such as electromagnetic compatibility and high/low temperature shock (-40℃~85℃).
