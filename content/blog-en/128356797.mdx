Cyclone V-based USB3.0/GIGE Industrial Camera Module Design Solution

This camera module can support up to 2-megapixel image sensors, providing high-performance ISP functionality and supporting USB3.0 or GIGE Vision interfaces. The first camera module utilizes an On-Semi PYTHON 480A color sensor (0.5-megapixel).
 

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/128356797/01_0bd8820b73847e67e3226cecf3759965.png)

Specifications

**Main Chips**

*   5CEFA2M13C8N
*   PYTHON480A
*   EN5329, EP53A8, EV1320
*   MT41J256M16HA-125IT
*   MT25QL256ABB8E12-0SIT
*   CYUSB3014-BZXI

**Performance**
⦁    800\*600 resolution, support up to 2M pixels
⦁    USB3.0 or GIGE Vision
