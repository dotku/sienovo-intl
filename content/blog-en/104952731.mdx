The TI AM5728 platform supports various industrial communication protocols including EtherCAT, EtherNet/IP, PROFIBUS, CAN bus, RS485, and RS232, enabling products to easily implement bus control and industrial networking. With dual C66x DSP cores, it can run more complex and higher-precision motion control interpolation algorithms. Additionally, by connecting an FPGA via the GPMC bus interface, the platform can further extend its capabilities in industrial control and image processing.
