### 8 AI Channels (14-bit, 2.5 MS/s/ch), 8 DIO Channels, PXI Multifunction I/O Module

The PXI-6133 is a simultaneously sampling, multifunction data acquisition device. It provides analog input, digital I/O, two 24-bit counters, and digital triggering. The PXI-6133 is suitable for a wide range of applications, including IF digitization, transient recording, ISDN, ADSL, and POTS manufacturing testing in the telecommunications industry, ultrasound and sonar testing, and high-energy physics. ![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139121915/01_c7f4b1f7ae6e3516ccb0ffb01c09b7c7.png)
