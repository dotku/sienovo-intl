16-Channel (16-bit) Vibration PCIe Acquisition Card

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677316/01_7e388218288d495aaf4aa51fcfae090d.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677316/02_dae4569ef65a43c19146795760ae1030.png)

Utilizing an XC7A100T-2FGG484I FPGA, this PCIe acquisition card is specifically designed for industrial PCs. As an auxiliary card, it can be inserted into a PCIe slot of an industrial PC, transferring acquired data to the CPU via the PCIe interface. Application-layer software can be directly developed on the CPU. It is primarily used in applications that require combination with an industrial PC or a desktop PC, where high sampling resolution is not critical and cost is a sensitive factor.

Each acquisition card integrates 16 channels. Further channel expansion depends on the available PCIe slots in the industrial PC or desktop PC. The total number of channels = Number of PCIe slots * 16.
