As a critical channel for urban material exchange and the "lifeline" responsible for transporting essential resources, transportation infrastructure carries heavy responsibilities including passenger mobility, freight logistics, urban development, connectivity, and collaborative governance. As an indispensable component of new infrastructure, Sinomax leverages cutting-edge AI technology to enable efficient traffic management—specifically, enforcement of over-limit regulations (over-length, over-weight, over-speed) and violation control (illegal parking, road space encroachment). By integrating mature video structuring models for vehicle analysis, we deliver real-time, dynamic, comprehensive traffic management solutions, establishing a unified "single view" of smart city transportation to enhance productivity, maintain stability, improve efficiency, and increase public well-being.
