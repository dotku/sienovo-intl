Industrial Computers Based on the RK3588 Processor

We have developed diverse industrial control motherboard solutions and complete industrial PC solutions based on the Rockchip RK3588 processor. They perform excellently in terms of visual processing, AI computing power, storage capacity, expandability, and reliability, which can help realize the multi-domain application of ultra-high-definition video technology.

Key technical features are as follows:

∙ Supports RK3588 (4\*A76@2.4GHz+4\*A55@1.8GHz) octa-core processor, with a built-in NPU offering 6Tops of computing power, supporting INT4/INT8/INT16 mixed-precision operations.

∙ Supports onboard 4~16GB LPDDR4/LPDDR4X memory, 1\*SATA3.0 high-speed storage, and 1\*eMMC (up to 128GB), plus 1\*TF card slot for further storage expansion.

∙ Integrated Mali-G610 GPU, compatible with OpenGL ES1.1/2.0/3.2, OpenCL 2.2, and Vulkan 1.2 graphics APIs, significantly enhancing display performance.

∙ Supports multiple display outputs: 1\*dual-channel LVDS, 2\*HDMI TX, 1\*HDMI RX, 1\*DP TX, 2\*MIPI TX/RX. Supports 8K@60fps H.265/VP9 video decoding and 8K@30fps H.265/H.264 video encoding, enabling easy multi-screen display.

∙ Supports 2\*LAN, onboard Wi-Fi/BT (supporting Wi-Fi 6, BT 5), 1\*M.2 B KEY for 5G network module access, and 1\*Mini-PCIe for 4G network module expansion, meeting wired/wireless network communication needs.

∙ Supports 4\*RS-232, 2\*RS-485, 1\*DEBUG for higher-speed short-distance/long-distance data transmission.

∙ Supports 4\*USB3.0, 4\*USB2.0 for multi-device networking.

∙ Supports 1\*Headphone, 1\*MIC-IN, 2\*5W AMP audio output.

∙ Supports 2\*CAN, 2\*MIPI DSI/CSI, 1\*I2C, 1\*POWER, 1\*SYS indicator light, 1\*Return Key, 1\*RST Key, 1\*JFP for various peripheral connections.

∙ Developed watchdog function, compatible with Android and Linux operating systems.

∙ Passive cooling design for lower power consumption and meeting the requirements for 0℃～60℃ wide-temperature operation.

∙ Dimensions: 189.5mm x 125mm, highly integrated functionality, easy to deploy.
