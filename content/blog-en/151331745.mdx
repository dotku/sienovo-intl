**Powerful Core, Stable Support**

Equipped with **Intel® 11th Gen Tiger Lake Core Processors**, adopting a System-on-Chip (SoC) design, providing powerful data processing capabilities.

**Enhanced Multi-tasking Efficiency**

**Dual-channel DDR4 memory architecture** supports capacity expansion via SODIMM slots, significantly enhancing the fluidity of multi-tasking and large application operation, easily handling complex computing tasks such as industrial robot path planning, real-time monitoring of automation systems, or energy storage power station data analysis.

**Diverse Expansion Ports**

Integrates **4 USB 3.0/2.0** ports for high-speed peripheral access; provides **6 RS485 serial ports** for seamless connection to traditional industrial equipment such as PLCs, sensors, and inverters; **8 DI and 8 DO** channels provide strong support for status monitoring and control command execution.

**Flexible Storage Applications**

Provides 1 standard 7-pin SATA-III interface and 1 full-length Mini-PCIe slot supporting mSATA SSDs (maximum transfer rate 3Gb/s), meeting various storage capacity and speed requirements.
