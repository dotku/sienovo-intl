The Loongson cloud terminal is a domestically developed, autonomous, and controllable solution based on the high-performance quad-core Loongson 3A4000 processor platform. Equipped with Kunlun domestic firmware, it supports domestic operating systems such as UOS and Kylin. Designed to meet the requirements of secure domestic information processing, it enables full localization and control from hardware and operating system to applications, making it a flagship desktop cloud solution for China's information security.
