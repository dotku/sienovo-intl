### Beckhoff Linear Transport System

Beckhoff's XTS magnetic-driven flexible transport system is a highly mature, high-performance drive solution where magnetically driven movers travel along a path formed by fully integrated motor modules. An industrial PC equipped with TwinCAT automation software can independently control movers with varying motion characteristics. The benefits this system offers to users are numerous.
