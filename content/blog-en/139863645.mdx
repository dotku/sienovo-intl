**Medical Endoscopy and Surgical Robotics Solutions**

In applications for medical endoscopy and surgical robotics solutions, it demonstrates its unique advantages. It supports the acquisition, processing, and 8K high-definition video image display, and also provides efficient 3D image processing capabilities. Its advanced synchronization technology ensures left-eye and right-eye video data synchronization accuracy down to 1 line, with end-to-end latency controlled within 50 milliseconds, significantly enhancing real-time performance and response speed. These features provide doctors with clearer, more stereoscopic, and more precise visual information, thereby improving the accuracy and safety of minimally invasive surgery.

**Industrial Inspection Solutions**

In the field of industrial automation, it also demonstrates its powerful performance. It can achieve efficient object recognition and performance computation in areas such as semiconductor inspection, electronics manufacturing, photovoltaic energy, lithium batteries, and assembly, improving the efficiency and accuracy of production quality inspection. Particularly when using the YOLOX object recognition algorithm, it achieves an mAP of up to 0.9 and a recognition speed of 50 FPS, greatly enhancing visual processing capabilities in industrial automation processes.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139863645/03_82c4c77f3c40d586049d73bbef907609.png)

Its multi-channel video processing capability, thanks to its 8-channel 12G-SDI bidirectional interfaces and 4-channel HDMI 2.1 input/output, enables its wide application in various fields such as **medical imaging, industrial automation, and smart cities & security**. Whether for research institutions or high-tech enterprises, it is an ideal choice for product prototyping and testing, providing strong support for imaging requirements across various industries.
