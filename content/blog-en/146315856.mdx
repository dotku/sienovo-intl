Board Introduction:

This board is a 3U Digital Input (DI) acquisition board, primarily designed for acquiring and processing standard DC110V digital switch signals.

Performance Specifications:

Power Supply: DC5V, DC3.3V, DC±15V, DC±24V

Self-test Function: Supported

MCU: MC9S08DZ60

Communication Interfaces (Backplane Side): CAN x2, I2C x 1

CPLD: EPM570T-144I3

Number of Channels: 16 channels, nominal high level 110V, low level 0V

Dimensions: 220mm\*100mm\*1.6mm

Weight: 0.165kg

Operating Temperature: -25℃~70℃
