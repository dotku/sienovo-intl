The XM-5145 industrial switch is a Layer 2 managed switch specifically designed and developed for harsh environments such as rail transit, marine, and vehicle-mounted applications. The product design complies with rail transit industry certifications.
The product uses M12 connectors to ensure stable network communication in vibrating environments. The entire unit adopts a fanless passive cooling design, ensuring long-term stable operation in working environments from -40°C to 75°C.
Unique Bypass design ensures network continuity even in the event of unexpected power loss.

## XM-5130 Vehicle-mounted M12 Managed Switch

Overview: The XM-5130 is a Layer 2 managed Ethernet switch. Its front panel provides 13 100M auto-adaptive Ethernet ports and 4 inter-vehicle Ethernet ports with link aggregation and bypass functions, all provided via M12 connectors. This product is suitable for harsh operating environments with vibration, extreme temperatures, high humidity, significant power fluctuations, and complex electromagnetic interference. The design strictly adheres to EMC principles, enabling it to meet the demanding on-board operating conditions of railway industrial trains, and can be extended to other critical mission systems in harsh environments.
