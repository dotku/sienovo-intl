1 Product Information

The edge computing gateway, based on the T-Head TH1520 chip, supports the OpenHarmony small system. It is a crucial hub connecting IoT devices and cloud platforms, applicable for data collection and forwarding in scenarios such as urban infrastructure, smart factories, smart buildings, business outlets, and operation service centers.

2 Product Highlights

● Powerful Edge Computing Capabilities

Quad-core Xuantie C910 CPU with a maximum frequency of 2.5GHz, 64-bit LPDDR4X-3733, and 4 TOPs empower the gateway's edge computing capabilities. It enables IoT edge real-time response, fast connectivity, intelligent applications, and effectively reduces cloud computing workload.

● Reliable Network Connectivity with Multiple Protocols

Multiple internet access options: Supports Fast Ethernet, Wi-Fi, and 3G/LTE CAT4/LTE CAT1 networks. Compatible with a wide range of mainstream protocols, including: Zigbee 3.0, LoRaWAN, Matter, BLE 5.0, Modbus.

● Comprehensive Security Protection

Data transmission encryption technology, network security firewall, supports Zone Virtualization compatible with the Global Platform TEE software ecosystem.

● Dual Domestic, Independent and Controllable

Utilizing the T-Head TH1520 RISC-V chip and OpenHarmony operating system, achieving independent and controllable hardware and software.

3 Application Scenarios

The edge gateway is used for data collection and forwarding in the following scenarios:

• Urban Infrastructure: Connecting temperature and humidity sensors, traffic flow radar, video surveillance, and other devices to cloud applications.
• Smart Factories: Connecting equipment sensors, PLCs, security cameras, and other devices to cloud applications.
• Smart Buildings: Environmental monitoring equipment, energy consumption monitoring equipment, lighting control, ventilation, and other devices and applications.
• Business Outlets, Operation Service Centers
