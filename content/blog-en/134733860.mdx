According to statistics, there are approximately 450,000 residential communities nationwide. Demands such as monitoring objects falling from height, managing illegal dumping, personnel management, fire and smoke detection, illegal occupation of public space, pedestrian flow detection, and vehicle type detection are closely related to every participant in creating community safety. According to publicly available court data, there are over 1,000 cases annually related to objects falling from height. Incidents of objects falling from height pose significant dangers and are increasing year by year, sounding an alarm for "overhead" safety. Deploying Xinmai's smart community management solution can effectively supervise behaviors such as objects falling from height, illegal dumping personnel management, fire and smoke detection, vehicle recognition, and delivery personnel recognition. The recognition accuracy rate reaches over 85%, playing a positive and beneficial role in providing pre-emptive warnings and post-incident accountability, thereby ensuring the "overhead" safety of residents.
