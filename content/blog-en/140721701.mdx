#### Hardware

*   Phytium FT-2000/4 Quad-core Processor
*   Onboard surface-mount DDR4 8GB memory; supports memory expansion up to 16GB
*   2 VGA interfaces or VGA+LVDS interfaces, supporting independent dual display
*   Standard HD audio interface
*   14 serial ports (COM2 and COM3 support optional RS232/RS485/RS422)
*   2 10/100/1000M Ethernet interfaces
*   2 USB 2.0, 4 USB 3.0; 16-channel IO
*   PS/2 keyboard and mouse
*   Supports storage such as SATA DOM, SATA SSD

#### Software

Supports various mainstream domestic operating systems, including Ubuntu, SylixOS, Galaxy Kylin, and other mainstream operating systems.

#### Other

*   Wide voltage input range: 9V~36V
*   Extended operating temperature range: -25℃ to +70℃
*   Product dimensions: 260mm (W) \* 200mm (D) \* 112.5mm (H)
