## VPX 3U CAN Communication Board

■ PCIe x1 expansion with 8-channel CAN interface

■ 8 independent isolated CAN interfaces, each CAN channel assigned 1 interrupt

■ Configurable to 125kbps, 250kbps, 500kbps, 1Mbps; rated operating frequency is configurable; no termination resistors on the device side

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138313159/01_020426e56806bb94628e4bada9024143.png)

**PCIe x1 Expansion Digital I/O Interface**

16-channel digital input: Opto-isolated, high-level threshold voltage 18V, low-level threshold voltage 6V, external signal drive capability no less than 30mA

16-channel digital output: High-level voltage is power supply voltage (20V-32V), low-level voltage range 0V-1V, single channel contact capacity 1A

16-channel digital output, with monitoring capability
