##### **MES Workstation Industrial Applications**

**Project Overview**
The MES workstation is located in the middle layer of the entire system, responsible for integrating all factory systems and forming a data platform. The upper layer is ERP, while the lower layer connects to PLCs, data acquisition, and display equipment, etc. MES automatically receives ERP business orders, schedules production, and issues production instructions to operators or lower-level equipment. Xinmai Technology specializes in MES application scenarios and has designed and produced a series of industrial panel PCs for MES workstations.

**Solution Requirements**
● Unfixed on-site workstations, complex locations, and harsh installation conditions.
● A wide variety of information collection equipment, requiring the workstation to have multiple I/O interfaces.
● The factory operates 24 hours a day, so the workstation must support uninterrupted operation.

**Solution**
DPC series industrial panel PCs optimize the entire production process, from order placement to product completion, through information transmission. When real-time events occur, users can react and report promptly, guiding and processing them with current accurate data. This reduces non-value-added activities within the enterprise, effectively guides the factory's production operations, thereby improving the factory's on-time delivery capability, enhancing material flow performance, and increasing production return on investment.
