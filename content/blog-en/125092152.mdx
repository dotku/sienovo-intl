As the Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) becomes more widespread and powerful, RK3568-based edge computing gateways have emerged. Edge computing effectively reduces the load on cloud servers, significantly decreases bandwidth usage, and also provides convenient conditions for localized regional autonomy.

Edge computing industrial gateways perfectly leverage the combined advantages of "edge" and "endpoint." Through 4G/5G communication and the internet, they integrate AI, Industrial IoT, and edge computing to achieve features such as cloud-edge collaboration, edge-endpoint linkage, compute power offloading, and regional autonomy. They can be widely applied in fields such as Industrial IoT, smart transportation, power IoT, environmental protection and fire safety, and smart agriculture.

## Solution Introduction

RK3568 Platform

Main functional requirements for the control board: Display interfaces (LVDS high-definition screen, HDMI large screen), 5 serial ports for connecting various sensors, PLCs, inverters, etc., multiple GPIOs, 6 USB ports, 2 CAN ports, 2 Ethernet ports, WIFI, and 4G for multiple network access modes to achieve remote control and management. It comes with a 1 TOPS NPU, facilitating AI and various algorithm acceleration.

**Product Features:**

1. Supports Ubuntu OS;

2. Features one 10/100/1000Mbps RJ45 Ethernet port;
3. Supports 2.4GHz WiFi connectivity;
4. Features one HDMI port;
5. Supports OpenGL ES 1.1/2.0/3.0, OpenVG 1.1, OpenCL, DirectX 11;
6. Supports 4K, H.265 hardware decoding with 10-bit color depth, HDMI 2.0;
7. Supports 1080P multi-format video decoding and 1080P video encoding, supports H.264, VP8, and MVC image enhancement processing;
8. Features a hardware security system, supports HDCP 2.X, supports ATECC608A chip hardware encryption;
9. Supports OpenCV machine vision library, supports TensorFlow;
10. Supports connection to IoT cloud platforms (encrypted ciphertext communication based on SHA256, PRF, HMAC-SHA256, HKDF, ECDSA, ECDH, AES algorithms);

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/125092152/04_299b92780bb96f564a34765041547680.jpeg)

Rich Interface Features

Equipped with high-speed interfaces such as PCIe 3.0, SATA 3.0, CAN-FD, and [USB](http://www.eechina.com/keyword/USB "USB") 3.0, 2 Gigabit Ethernet ports and up to 10 UA\[color=inherit !important\]RT interfaces. It simultaneously supports communication methods such as wired network, 4G/5G, and dual-band WiFi, meeting the data acquisition and communication functions of conventional industrial gateways.

Lightweight Artificial Intelligence

With a 2.0GHz clock speed and an NPU offering 1 TOPS of computing power, it enables local lightweight artificial intelligence to perform intelligent analysis of collected industrial equipment operational data and make corresponding adjustments, thereby improving the operational management efficiency of industrial equipment.

Powerful Image Processing Capability

It supports cameras for image acquisition and analyzes and judges images through algorithms to achieve functions such as personnel identification, intelligent fire monitoring, and remote inspection.

As a core component of smart gateways, this gateway has undergone rigorous environmental temperature tests, pressure tests, and long-term stability operation tests to ensure its stable and reliable operation in complex industrial environments, effectively reducing the overall machine failure rate and maintenance costs.
