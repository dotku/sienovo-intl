## S2500 Tengyun Processor Motherboard

The motherboard conforms to the VPX 6U standard form factor and provides interfaces including DVI, VGA, USB, Ethernet, and serial ports.

*    Processor: Tengyun S2500  
*    Chipset: ZX-200  
*    Memory: Quad-channel DDR4 with domestically produced memory chips, up to 64GB capacity  
*    Solid-state storage: Supports mSATA SSDs with capacities of 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, and 1TB  
*    Interfaces: 6 USB ports, 4 Ethernet ports  
*    Dimensions: 233.35mm (L) × 160mm (H), board thickness 2mm  
*    Operating environment: -40°C to +65°C (industrial grade), -20°C to +55°C (commercial grade)  
*    Firmware: Phytium UEFI  
*    Operating system: Compatible with Kylin OS (Server Edition) 4.0.2
