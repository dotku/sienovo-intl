The Xinmai XM-3568-01 motherboard features a Rockchip RK3568 quad-core Cortex-A55 processor with a main frequency of up to 2.0GHz, offering significantly improved performance. It supports up to 8GB of memory capacity with a frequency of up to 1600MHz, and full-link ECC for safer and more reliable data. It is equipped with dual gigabit adaptive RJ45 Ethernet ports and extends to 4 additional I211 Ethernet controllers, making it suitable for gigabit routers, soft-routers, firewalls, and other applications.

Supports HarmonyOS + Android 11 + Linux.
