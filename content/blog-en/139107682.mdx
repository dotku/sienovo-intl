### Revitalizing Rural Smart Agriculture - Building Smart Irrigation Systems

Project Background

The smart water-saving irrigation solution is applied in this Wangcheng Smart Water-Saving Irrigation Demonstration Project, collecting irrigation data from 16 areas near Wangcheng District to achieve visualized irrigation management.

Product Advantages

1. Provides HANESRT series products for water-saving irrigation projects, collecting irrigation water source data.

2. The equipment can collect water volume and geological conditions, thereby controlling the amount of irrigation water.

3. The DUTMCK platform provides a visualized H5 configuration interface, allowing users to schedule data as needed and generate intuitive data tables.

### Online Monitoring of Energy Consumption Data - Supporting Energy Industry Energy Saving and Emission Reduction

Project Background

To achieve the integration of Tianjin-Beijing-Hebei, the Shijiazhuang Energy Saving and Emission Reduction Project targets 146 industrial enterprises in Shijiazhuang with an annual energy consumption of over 5,000 tons of standard coal (216 sets of equipment). It involves the design, construction, debugging, acceptance, and subsequent maintenance services for a primary energy consumption data collection and a partial secondary energy consumption data collection IoT system and network transmission system.

Product Advantages

1. Provides HANESRT series products for the Shijiazhuang Energy Saving and Emission Reduction Project, collecting and transmitting environmental data.

2. Provides the DUTMCK platform (IoT Cloud Platform) for the Shijiazhuang Energy Saving and Emission Reduction Project, where collected environmental data is analyzed on our platform in sub-second time, generating data analysis reports required by customers.

### Yushan Industrial Park - Building a Full-Closed-Loop Green Production and Manufacturing Base

Jiangxi Yushan Industrial Park was established in July 2002, located in Yushan County, the eastern gateway of Jiangxi. With a total planned area of 30 square kilometers, it is one of the first 18 key industrial parks in Jiangxi Province. In 2006, it was approved by the provincial government as a provincial-level industrial park, named Jiangxi Yushan Industrial Park. Currently, a total area of 11.8 square kilometers has been developed, forming a "one park, three zones" structure, namely Jinshan Core Area, Wencheng Industrial Park, and Yanrui New Building Materials Base. It has initially formed three leading industries: non-ferrous metals, electromechanical auto parts, and new building materials, with food processing and light industry textiles also beginning to take shape. The park shows good development momentum. Currently, there are 276 enterprises in the park, with 164 enterprises in operation, including 105 above-scale enterprises, employing over 20,500 people. In 2014, the park achieved a main business revenue of 25.11 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 16.8%; and realized profits and taxes of 3.35 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 12.04%. In early 2015, Yushan County Industrial Park was upgraded to "Yushan Economic Development Zone".
