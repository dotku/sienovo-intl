## Digital Signage

Smart networked digital signage allows multimedia content such as audio, video, images, documents, and web pages to be freely laid out and published as programs to endpoint devices via the internet. Different endpoints can be centrally managed and uniformly updated. Depending on whether the application scenario is centralized or distributed, either LAN-based management or cloud server management can be adopted.

## Vending Machines

With the rise of IoT, big data, and artificial intelligence, smart devices are now deeply integrated into people's daily lives. Intelligent vending machines serve as a key hub connecting online consumption with offline experiences. Combined with customized operation models and big data collection and analysis, they enable traditional retail vendors in niche markets to easily transform their business roles.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/134228063/02_b13ab32af247f95d51c9dbf40105d978.png)

## Point-of-Sale (POS) Terminals

As a hardware device capable of aggregating multiple payment methods, the smart POS terminal, when equipped with the Android operating system, further meets diverse needs such as membership marketing, data analytics, and intelligent management. It will become a critical gateway for store digitalization and smart upgrades.
