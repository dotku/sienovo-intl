In recent years, frequent mining accidents have exposed numerous issues across traditional mining operations. With key coal-producing provinces across China successively issuing relevant policy documents, the pace of intelligent mine construction has accelerated. Upgrading conveyor belt transmission systems is a critical component of mine intelligence and a key direction for cost reduction and efficiency improvement.

△ Policies on intelligent mining from various provinces

Conveyor belt transmission systems face several industry-wide challenges. For example, low automation levels, inspection blind spots, and prolonged worker exposure to high-dust environments increase both safety and occupational health risks. To address these issues, a **"Smart Mining All-in-One Machine" has been developed based on SOPHON computing products, establishing a "Conveyor Belt Intelligent Video Monitoring System." This system has been deployed in Shanxi Aluminum’s intelligent transformation project, significantly advancing its journey toward intelligence, automation, and unmanned operations**.

01丨 Efficient and Accurate Algorithm Recognition

To enhance the safety, stability, and controllability of conveyor belt systems in mining operations, over 10 AI vision algorithms have been developed:

1

**Conveyor Belt Operational Status Monitoring**

Including belt blockage, material leakage, tearing, misalignment, empty running (uniform material), slippage, and foreign objects

2

**Health Monitoring of Belt Drive Equipment**

Including motors, gearboxes, couplings or hydraulic couplers, drive drums, idlers, and belt frames

3

**Personnel and Environmental Safety Monitoring**

Including open flames and smoke, safety helmet wearing, and personnel intrusion beyond boundaries

02丨 Flexible and Rapid Deployment with All-in-One Machine

**SOPHON edge computing products offer high computational power, low power consumption, and compact size, with strong environmental adaptability, making them ideal for complex edge scenarios.** The algorithm-integrated all-in-one machine built on SOPHON computing products enables fast deployment and flexible handling of high-concurrency application scenarios, delivering powerful edge computing capabilities for diverse smart applications.

△ SOPHON computing products

By leveraging self-developed AI vision algorithms and SOPHON high-density servers, the "Smart Mining All-in-One Machine" and the "Conveyor Belt Intelligent Video Monitoring System" have been co-built. The system enables remote backend monitoring, allowing real-time oversight of the entire conveyor belt system, timely judgment and response to alarm-triggering anomalies, and achieving round-the-clock real-time monitoring with centralized command and control.

03丨 Deployment and Application at Shanxi Aluminum

In Shanxi Aluminum’s intelligent transformation project, **a series of AI vision algorithms for conveyor belt monitoring are deployed on SOPHON high-density servers SE6/8, covering nearly 100 cameras across the plant’s conveyor belt areas.** The system monitors the operational status of over 10 conveyor belts in the raw material workshop and the health status of associated drive equipment, performing real-time algorithmic analysis via edge computing.

Furthermore, it demonstrates the capability for full-chain algorithm development on heterogeneous platforms and the ability to deploy and infer algorithms across different computing platforms.

  **Shanxi Aluminum**  

Shanxi Aluminum Co., Ltd., a subsidiary of State Power Investment Corporation (SPIC), is a key enterprise in SPIC’s alumina production and operation. The company's main business is alumina, with an annual production capacity of 2.9 million tons. It operates its own thermal power plant and dedicated railway line, and currently manages four self-owned mines with bauxite geological reserves of approximately 100 million tons.
