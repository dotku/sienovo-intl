Government Agencies

**Solution Background:**

In the context of the national strategy for IT application innovation and development, government service halls need to deeply advance "Internet + Government Services" based on national independent technological capabilities. This involves accelerating the construction of a national integrated online government service platform, further implementing the innovation-driven development strategy, enhancing government network security assurance capabilities and informatization construction levels, promoting the deep application and transformative development of government services, fostering the development of strategic emerging industries, and utilizing secure and controllable information technology to strengthen cybersecurity and informatized government construction.

**Solution:**

Government affairs are crucial to the national economy and people's livelihoods, and both the government and the public have very high requirements for the security and data privacy of smart government products and services. Combining the application requirements of all-in-one government service machines, **Zhisuan has developed an independently controllable solution based on a 3.5-inch multi-USB, multi-serial port domestic industrial control motherboard, powered by the Phytium D2000 processor. Zhisuan supports domestic Kunlun firmware, domestic operating systems like UOS and Kylin, and features rich I/O interfaces, capable of meeting the expansion needs of all-in-one government service machines in various user scenarios.** It supports high-frequency government services from multiple departments such as human resources and social security, civil affairs, housing provident fund, public security, and taxation. This can significantly improve government efficiency and strengthen data protection, delivering secure, reliable, and high-performance data processing for government and enterprise users, preventing data theft, and safeguarding government information security.

*   **Achieve domestic autonomy for government equipment, providing a solid and trustworthy foundation for the construction of Digital China;**
*   **Strengthen data processing capabilities, enrich interfaces, and meet the optional module requirements for different government service windows;**
*   **Low latency enables coordinated multi-device linkage and enhances real-time communication capabilities between smart devices and business systems.**

Education Industry

**Solution Background:**

The development of new-generation information technologies such as 5G, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence has ushered in a new wave of reform and innovation in educational informatization. With the increasingly widespread application of computers across campuses, traditional PC-based teaching methods present numerous inconveniences. For example, the diverse brands and models of classroom terminals make unified management impossible, and any issues directly impact teaching quality. Timely updates to teaching systems and software upgrades are required, posing significant challenges for school management and maintenance. Students arbitrarily installing various software and copying data can easily lead to the leakage of internal teaching materials.

**Solution:**

Teaching is no longer limited to the traditional PC model and is gradually moving towards the "cloud" fast lane. Cloud desktop solutions provide a smarter way to achieve modern teaching. The security of teaching resources is the basic guarantee for achieving efficient classrooms. **The education cloud desktop solution combines Zhisuan cloud terminals with a desktop cloud management platform. The cloud terminals are based on domestically produced high-performance 8-core Phytium D2000 processors, independent X100 graphics cards, and an independently controllable domestic solution, achieving adaptation of chips, operating systems, and applications.** It supports applications in scenarios such as teacher offices, multimedia classroom upgrades, training room construction, and libraries/e-reading rooms. Zhisuan cloud desktop terminal solutions can reduce teaching operation and maintenance costs, improve management efficiency, and provide numerous conveniences for teachers and students, significantly enhancing work and learning efficiency.

*   **Effortless and Efficient:** Achieves unified management and centralized updates, simplifying daily maintenance for administrators and improving efficiency. Additionally, for tasks involving frequent switching between scenarios like teaching and exams, efficiency is boosted by more than 10 times compared to the PC model.
*   **Unified Standards, Secure Teaching:** Data is centrally stored in the data center, and all teacher office and teaching-related operations are conducted under a unified, secure cloud desktop, which is more conducive to ensuring the security and reliability of teaching materials.
*   **Cost Reduction, Efficiency Improvement, Green Energy Saving:** Cloud terminal desktop deployment cycles are shortened by over 80%, and operation and maintenance costs are reduced by over 40%. Furthermore, the entire machine has low power consumption, is pollution-free, noise-free, and can save over 64% in electricity consumption.

Financial Industry:

Solution Background:

Amidst the wave of accelerating financial market infrastructure and building domestic independent innovation, the banking industry is continuously promoting the intelligent upgrade of offline branches. Current smart branches integrate manual services and self-service banking, which also places demands on the security and reliability of financial equipment. With the successive advancement of independent innovation in the financial industry, the demand for domestic smart branches will become even more prominent. Based on industry demands, Zhisuan and Phytium have jointly provided financial equipment products based on Phytium chips for financial industry customers.

Solution:

Zhisuan's two domestically produced all-in-one machines, based on the Phytium D2000 processor and domestic operating systems, can be applied to various financial self-service equipment, such as ATMs, counter terminals, and all-in-one counter machines. With up to 10 serial ports and 14 USB interfaces, various peripherals can be connected via USB and serial ports, and communicate with backend data centers via network ports. This enables functions such as ID card readers, fingerprint recognition, magnetic stripe readers/writers, and IC bank card processing, effectively improving the intelligence level of branches, simplifying business processing procedures, enhancing business processing efficiency, and reducing business processing costs.

*   **Achieve Independent Upgrade:** After self-service equipment undergoes independent upgrades, it achieves the goals of preventing supply chain disruption and backdoors, making a significant contribution to ensuring national financial security.
*   **Reduce Bank Operating Costs:** According to incomplete statistics, the annual volume of financial services provided by one self-service device far exceeds that provided by a single teller in a year, while its cost is only 1/20 of a teller's salary.
*   **Reduce Counter Service Pressure:** Completing a withdrawal or deposit transaction on a self-service device takes only 1/10 of the time required for manual operation at a counter.
*   **Reduce Human Risk:** The equipment does not generate any banking operational risks arising from factors such as ethics or desires.
