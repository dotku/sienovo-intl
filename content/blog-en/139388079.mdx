While VR technology has revolutionized various aspects of daily life, such as entertainment, healthcare, architecture, education, and product design, creating VR content remains a challenging hurdle.

At the Jetson TX2 launch event held by Nvidia in San Francisco, it was demonstrated how the Jetson TX2 can accelerate AI computing, graphics, and computer vision operations, and power workflows for VR content creation.

The Artec Leo, a handheld scanner developed by Artec 3D using the Jetson TX1, can perform real-time 3D capture, fusion, modeling, and visualization on its own display, and can also stream to mobile phones and tablets.

The Artec Leo is powered by the ultra-fast, credit card-sized Jetson TX1 platform, scanning 8 million points per second, integrating color and texture data into 3D depth information, to create 3D models with 0.1 mm accuracy in real-time.

Artyom Yukhin, President and CEO of Artec, said: "The Artec Leo makes 3D scanning as simple as shooting video. The Jetson TX1 is small yet powerful and easy to integrate. Using it, we can quickly create lifelike models, significantly simplifying the workflow for CG artists, designers, engineers, and other VR professionals."

1.  1.  Product Overview

The XM-AIBOX-32 Smart Edge Analysis All-in-One Machine is a high-performance, low-power edge computing product. Equipped with the BM1684X main chip, it delivers up to 32 TOPS of INT8 computing power, up to 16 TFLOPS of FP16/BF16 computing power, and up to 2 TFLOPS of FP32 computing power. It can process 32 channels of high-definition video simultaneously, supporting hardware decoding for 32 channels of 1080P HD video and encoding for 12 channels.

This product highly integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms based on computer vision and deep learning networks, as well as a comprehensive video intelligent management platform. The AI intelligent algorithms cover various scenarios such as parks, communities, construction sites, and campuses, and can be combined and configured on demand for specific scenarios. The comprehensive video intelligent management platform supports front-end device management, real-time video preview, alarm push, forensic capture, online algorithm loading and optimization, and large-screen data trend analysis display. The device is easy to operate and plug-and-play, and features rich northbound API interfaces to empower upper-layer business application platforms.
