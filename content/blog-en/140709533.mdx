**Significant Increase in Demand for Medical Imaging Equipment**

Medical imaging refers to high-end medical devices that obtain images of the human body or specific tissues and organs within the body using X-rays, ultrasound, or electromagnetic fields, assisting doctors in more accurate and efficient clinical diagnosis and subsequent treatment planning. With socio-economic development, the improvement of the healthcare system, and increased public health awareness, the demand for medical care is growing. Coupled with advancements in new-generation information technology and the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic, the popularity of medical imaging equipment has continuously risen in recent years, and its market size has expanded.

In the broader context of "AI+" healthcare integration and development, the overall development trend for medical imaging equipment is towards faster, more convenient, clearer, safer, and smarter solutions. Regardless of the type of medical imaging equipment, its basic working principle involves control by a computer system. It acquires medical image data by integrating scanning gantry systems, data acquisition systems, operator consoles, and image display systems, enabling functions such as intelligent analysis and high-definition imaging.

Therefore, when developing products, most medical imaging equipment manufacturers today have higher requirements for the quality, data processing, and graphics processing performance of the core computer system hardware. Embedded computer products built on 10th Gen Intel Core processors can adapt to intensive AI-accelerated inference computing applications, possess excellent computing power and graphics processing performance, feature rich I/O interface designs, and support various medical imaging equipment applications.

Key technical features are as follows:

CPU: Supports Intel 10th Gen Core i3/i5/i7 LGA1200 65W processors and Intel H420E/Q470E chipsets.

Memory: Supports DDR4 2400/2666/2933MHz, 2*SO-DIMM dual-channel memory slots, and up to 64GB memory.

Storage: Supports 2*SATA3.0, 1*M.2 2280 KEY M interface (Q470E supports PCIe X4 SSDs, H420E supports SATA SSDs) for increased storage space.

Graphics Performance: Integrated Intel UHD Graphics 630, supports multiple display interfaces including 1*HDMI, 1*DP, 1*VGA, with a maximum resolution of 4K.

Networking: MITX-6135 supports 2*LAN, 4*USB2.0, 6*USB3.0, 2*COM, and 1*M.2 2230 KEY-E interface for WLAN/BT expansion (Q470E supports PCIe/USB signals).

EPC-3130M-A10 supports 6*LAN, 4*USB2.0, 6*USB3.0, 6*COM, and 1*M.2 2230 KEY-E interface for WLAN/BT expansion (Q470E supports PCIe/USB signals).

I/O Capabilities: MITX-6135 supports interfaces such as 1*PCIe 16x slot, 1*TPM, 1*PS2 KBMS, 8*GPIO, supports JLPC for serial port expansion (with AFC-385C serial port expansion), and supports PCIe5678 for 4-lane PCIe x1 signal expansion.

EPC-3130M-A10 supports 1*CPUFAN (with speed control), 1*PS2 KBMS keyboard/mouse, and 1*ON/OFF button.

Usability: Supports Windows 10, Windows 11, and Linux operating systems.

Reliability: Supports watchdog functionality and stable operation under various conditions, including wide temperature ranges and complex electromagnetic environments.
