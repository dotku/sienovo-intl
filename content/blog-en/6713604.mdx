I want to write a novel, and have favorite authors.

Seeking happiness, expanding, acting, just like a job interview, you'll always find the right fit.

Travel, the embrace of nature. Shenzhen Bay, rafting, water park, Fenghuang Ancient Town – surely I must go somewhere.

It's been too long since I've been home, I miss my parents.

Friends, eloquent, witty, easy to talk to (that's right, easy to talk to).

  

\=================

Friends, Easy to Talk To

\=================

This way, on one hand, it can enrich life, and on the other hand, it makes playing ball with others more interesting and allows for greater relaxation when going out.

Also, it provides convenience for writing novels and for deep reflection on life.

It's also good for providing a moment of ease when interacting with superiors at work.

Therefore, it's extremely important.

  

Although decadent, I always have ideas.

Creativity, novelty, making people feel it's fun to be with you, and learning when necessary.

  

\==================

Friends, Novels

\==================

Friends, fill life with sunshine and happiness.

Friends, make difficulties seem small.

Friends, nourish like rain and dew.

Friends, help thoughts grow.

Friends, like mountains and rivers.

Friends, are childhood playmates.

Friends, are the society you know.

Friends, like women.

  

\==================

Harmonious Life

\==================

This kneaded life!

I need a feeling that is harmonious, appropriate, at a moderate pace, and with the right flavor.

No rushing, no anxiety, no sleeplessness, no restless sleep.
