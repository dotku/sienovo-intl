**Key Product Features:**

- Easy and simple installation; the device can be directly adhered to the car's front windshield using 3M adhesive.
- Supports real-time preview via IE, mobile phones, tablets, and PC client software.
- Built-in 2-channel analog HD, with each channel supporting up to 2-megapixel resolution. Expandable with an additional 2 channels of 1080P analog HD camera input.
- Supports GPS, Wi-Fi, 3G/4G, Bluetooth, and other modules.
- The device features a robust anti-tamper and dustproof design, ensuring the TF card, SIM card, antenna, and connection ports are protected.
- Built-in G-SENSOR module automatically uploads alarms for abnormal vibration, rollover, collision, and sudden braking.
- Built-in supercapacitor allows the device to continue operating in the event of abnormal power loss.
