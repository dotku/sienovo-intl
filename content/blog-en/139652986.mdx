Core Board Introduction

The FMQL20SM core board is a fully domestically produced industrial core board. It is designed based on Fudan Micro FMQL20S400M heterogeneous multi-core SoC processor, which features a quad-core ARM Cortex-A7 (PS side) and FPGA programmable logic resources (PL side). The PS side clock speed is up to 1GHz. All components of the core board, such as the CPU, RAM, ROM, power supply, crystal oscillator, and connectors, adopt domestically produced industrial-grade solutions, achieving a 100% localization rate. A localization rate certification report can also be provided.

The core board exposes interfaces such as Gigabit Ethernet, SDIO, USB, CAN, UART, SPI, and I2C via domestically produced industrial-grade B2B connectors. PL side programs can be loaded via the PS side, and both the PS side and PL side can be developed independently. The core board has undergone professional PCB layout and high/low temperature testing and verification, ensuring stable and reliable quality to meet the requirements of various industrial application environments.

When users perform secondary development with the core board, they only need to focus on upper-layer applications, enabling rapid product solution verification, reducing development difficulty, and shortening R&D cycles, thereby lowering overall costs and seizing market opportunities.

In addition, we also provide complete machine customization services, including baseboard design, EMC testing, structural design, program and algorithm porting, and other services.
