China is the world's largest consumer market, and the retail industry holds tremendous growth potential. Internet giants Alibaba and Tencent are accelerating and expanding their investments in this space. The Sinmak Smart Retail solution covers a wide range of sectors including FMCG (fast-moving consumer goods), apparel, food and beverage, hospitality, home and beauty, consumer electronics, new retail operators, apparel chains, hypermarkets/supermarkets, department stores/shopping malls, FMCG brands, and restaurant chains. By leveraging foot traffic analytics, the solution generates precise customer profiles and insights into consumer behavior, enabling targeted advertising, customer flow management, improved conversion rates, and increased average transaction value—delivering a data-driven, precision marketing business model.
