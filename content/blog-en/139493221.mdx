Modern healthcare systems, hospitals, and service providers are deploying new tools and building exciting new care models to better serve patients. These strategies focus on Clinical Decision Support (CDS), helping to provide timely, filtered, patient-specific information for clinicians to enhance care. In recent years, this pursuit has seen an increasing number of medical devices introduced into healthcare environments. These devices include tablets, wearables, health monitors, and Artificial Intelligence (AI) imaging systems. Wearable devices can keep clinicians informed of vital patient signs, such as heart rate and blood pressure, alerting healthcare providers before problems arise. Health monitors can collect patient data and trigger corresponding actions based on the results, thereby assisting with remote care—for example, monitoring blood glucose levels and sending information to a companion device (such as an insulin pump) to administer insulin.

AI imaging models can detect potential issues in X-rays and prioritize these images for radiologists or internists to review. Consequently, healthcare systems and health service providers must decide how to manage and fully leverage this unprecedented volume of data. Considering bandwidth overhead, access rights, and privacy, decisions must be made on which data to send to the cloud and which to manage locally. Edge computing brings data processing, analysis, and storage closer to the source where data is generated—for example, a hospital's local servers or a patient's mobile device at home. Edge computing, as a complement to cloud computing, allows IT decision-makers to choose the appropriate computing location for workloads. This strategy can help healthcare systems optimize data collection, storage, and analysis—for a typical hospital, the annual data volume already reaches 50 PB.

1.  1.  Product Overview

The XM-AIBOX-16 Smart Edge Analysis All-in-One Machine is a high-performance, low-power edge computing product. Equipped with the domestic TPU chip BM1684, it boasts INT8 computing power up to 17.6 TOPS, can simultaneously process 16 channels of high-definition video, and supports hardware decoding of 32 channels of 1080P HD video and 2 channels of encoding.

This product highly integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms based on computer vision and deep learning networks, as well as an intelligent video comprehensive management platform. The AI intelligent algorithms cover various scenarios such as parks, communities, construction sites, and campuses, and can be combined and configured on demand according to the scenario. The intelligent video comprehensive management platform supports front-end device management, real-time video preview, alarm push, forensic snapshot, online algorithm loading and optimization, data situational analysis large-screen display, etc. The device is easy to operate, plug-and-play, and features rich northbound API interfaces, enabling integration with upper-layer business application platforms.
