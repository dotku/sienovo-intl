The XM series is a data acquisition instrument designed to meet essential requirements such as "simple operation, portability, and versatility for various measurement needs." With an ultra-compact, lightweight palm-sized design, it supports eight types of measurement modules and enables multi-channel measurements of up to 576 channels. Additionally, it features a wiring reduction system that significantly cuts down wiring time. Users don't need to worry about wiring duration, wiring errors, or restoration work in the event of cable disconnection. The system also includes helpful setup assistance functions and intuitive icons, enabling even beginners to operate it "effortlessly." Furthermore, power can be selected from AC, DC, or battery options, with a high-capacity lithium-ion battery supporting data acquisition for up to 800 minutes. Data transmission via a wireless LAN module minimizes cabling constraints at measurement sites.

### Product Features

#### Capable of measuring temperature, voltage, current, strain, acceleration, pulse, CAN signals, and more

Designed for data verification, analysis, and reporting. Therefore, the faster the measurement tasks are completed, the better. To achieve rapid measurements, the process must be simple and flexible. The NR-X series software allows easy configuration even without referring to a manual or prior knowledge of the operation.
