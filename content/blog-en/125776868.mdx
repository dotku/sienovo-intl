Xinmai Technology's AGX Xavier GMSL carrier board is an embedded AI computing product with × GMSL video input interfaces, capable of empowering numerous end devices with AI computing power, thereby effectively lowering the barrier to entry for AI product deployment. Xavier can provide up to 32 TOPS of computing power, which can well meet the computing demands for image, LiDAR point cloud, and other data in scenarios such as low-speed autonomous driving and industrial machine vision.

In autonomous driving technology, environmental perception is key, and neural network algorithms are at its core. With the widespread adoption of autonomous driving technology, traditional general-purpose processors can no longer adequately meet the demands. Thus, processors specifically designed for autonomous driving emerged. Amidst the wave of AI and autonomous driving, Nvidia has also actively transformed, shifting its business focus from graphics processing to AI computing and autonomous driving.
