HD Video Streaming Motherboard

HD Video Live Streaming

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/142386132/05_1e9110738b6bc6daea11dd0f6bda1287.png)

The SC66-IM66 V1.0 Android mainboard is based on the SDM660 platform, featuring the SDM660 custom 64-bit ARM v8.0 application processor (Kryo260 CPU) with a KryoGold quad-core high-performance processor @ 2.2 GHz, 1 MB L2, and a KryoSilver quad-core low-power processor @ 1.843 GHz, 1 MB L2, running Android 9/10. It is independently developed, offers stable performance, industrial quality, and a rich interface design.

Widely applied in AI facial recognition, HD video transmission, AI robots, commercial display equipment, industrial tablets, interactive all-in-one machines, smart self-service terminals, and more.

Designed specifically for live streaming applications, it reserves various multimedia interfaces to meet the multi-functional demands of the live streaming industry. Dual HDMI IN supports multi-camera live streaming and external USB devices such as sound cards, microphones, headphones, DSLR cameras, gimbal cameras, and other live streaming equipment.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/142386132/06_1e9d754e7b2af45d6d1234ad488cd736.png)

It features 4 USB ports, 4 serial ports, 6 I/O ports, and other communication interface expansions, compatible with mainstream industry peripherals. It supports dual MIPI output, allowing for connection with dual USB cameras for AI facial recognition applications. It comes with a built-in 4G communication module and BT 4.2.
