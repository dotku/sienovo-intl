Analog and Digital I/O, 667 MHz Dual-Core CPU, 512 MB DRAM, 512 MB Storage Capacity, Zynq-7020 FPGA CompactRIO Single-Board Controller
The sbRIO-9637 is an embedded controller that integrates a real-time processor running NI Linux Real-Time, a user-reconfigurable FPGA, and I/O on a single printed circuit board (PCB). The sbRIO-9637 can be easily embedded in high-volume OEM applications that require flexibility, reliability, and high performance. The controller features Gigabit Ethernet, CAN, USB, serial, and SDHC ports, and its operating temperature range can withstand the most demanding environments. The sbRIO-9637 features 16 16-bit analog inputs, 4 16-bit analog outputs, and 28 3.3 V digital lines.
