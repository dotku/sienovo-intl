The Xinming XM-3568-01 motherboard features the Rockchip RK3568 quad-core Cortex-A55 processor, with a maximum clock frequency of up to 2.0GHz, delivering significantly enhanced performance. It supports up to 8GB of memory with a frequency of up to 1600MHz and full-chain ECC support for improved data security and reliability. The board integrates dual gigabit adaptive RJ45 Ethernet ports and expands four additional Ethernet controllers (I211), suitable for applications such as gigabit routers, soft routers, and firewalls.

Supports HarmonyOS, Android 11, and Linux.
