### 16-Channel, 60 V, 8 Solid-State Relays, 8 Inputs, Channel-to-Channel Isolated Digital I/O Device

The USB-6525 is a bus-powered industrial USB I/O device that enables automation even for the most demanding applications. Its solid-state relay outputs are ideal for controlling pumps, valves, motors, and various other industrial actuators. The USB-6525 features channel-to-channel isolated digital inputs for reading data from industrial digital sensors, and 8 solid-state relay outputs with a switching voltage of 60 VDC/30 Vrms and a maximum switching current of 500 mA per channel. With programmable power-on states, you can configure the initial output states in software to ensure glitch-free, safe operation when connected to industrial actuators.
