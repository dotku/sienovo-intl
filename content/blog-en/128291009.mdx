Overview: The XM-5130 is a Layer 2 managed Ethernet switch. Its front panel provides 13 100M auto-sensing Ethernet ports and 4 inter-vehicle Ethernet ports with Link Aggregation and bypass functionality. The interfaces are provided via M12 connectors. This product is suitable for harsh operating environments with significant vibration, temperature and humidity fluctuations, large power supply variations, and complex electromagnetic interference. Strict EMC principles were followed during the design process, enabling it to meet the demanding operating conditions of railway industrial on-board train applications, and it can be extended to other critical mission systems in harsh environments.
