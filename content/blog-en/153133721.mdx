**Solution Overview**

Sienovo's Industrial High Dynamic Range Vision Perception Solution features an ultra-wide dynamic range, adapts to complex lighting conditions, integrates deep learning algorithms, supports object tracking and scene understanding, and is compatible with mainstream AI platforms, providing an efficient and precise solution for industrial vision.

**Solution Advantages**

■ High Dynamic Range Imaging: Features an ultra-wide dynamic range, adapting to complex and variable industrial lighting environments, ensuring stable imaging under various conditions such as strong light and low light.
■ Deep Learning Integration: Integrates deep learning algorithms, supporting dynamic object tracking, 3D scene reconstruction, and spatial semantic understanding to achieve intelligent vision capabilities.
■ USB Interface Convenience: Utilizes a USB interface, offering plug-and-play functionality, facilitating integration with mainstream AI computing platforms and systems, and reducing deployment complexity.
■ High Compatibility: Compatible with various operating systems and software platforms, meeting the needs of different industrial application scenarios.
■ Industrial-Grade Protection: Features an industrial-grade protection design, capable of withstanding harsh industrial conditions such as vibration and temperature fluctuations, ensuring long-term stable operation of the equipment.

The USB3.0 high dynamic range industrial camera is centered on high-performance imaging, designed specifically for complex lighting and harsh environments, combining high-definition image quality with stable transmission. With excellent low-light performance and a wide dynamic range, it is suitable for agricultural machinery, industrial inspection, smart security, and rail transit scenarios, and with flexible optical configurations, it meets high-precision vision requirements across multiple fields.

■ 3.0μm large pixel size, enhancing photosensitivity in low-light environments;

■ Integrated ISP chip for image processing, optimizing real-time imaging effects;

■ USB3.0 interface, supports high-speed data transmission, ensuring smooth 30fps full frame rate output;

■ Agricultural Machinery: Used in modern agriculture for navigation and monitoring of agricultural robots.

■ Industrial Automation and Quality Inspection: In production lines and automated inspection systems, used for high-precision image acquisition and quality inspection, capable of rapidly transmitting and identifying image data.
