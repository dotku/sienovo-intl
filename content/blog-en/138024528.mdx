##### **Tire Curing Machine Solution**

**Project Background**
The curing process is the final step in tire production. To enhance the tire's wear resistance and rolling resistance, tires undergo a curing process to increase their strength. The effective control of the three key elements of curing directly impacts the quality of the final product. Traditional control systems, which involve numerous buttons, switches, and indicator lights, require manual operation. Modern intelligent control systems can automatically adjust curing process parameters to achieve optimal curing quality, improve production efficiency, and enable fully automated management of the curing operation.

**Solution Requirements**
● The control host needs to be high-performance, capable of quickly and accurately processing various data.
● The on-site environment is harsh, requiring high-level protection features.

**Solution**
The DPC Industrial Panel PC, acting as the host computer, can perform all control actions, monitor the curing process and equipment status, and receive data such as temperature and pressure acquired by the PLC. Users can adjust recipe parameters using configuration software to achieve intelligent management and control of tire curing equipment, thereby realizing automated production.
 

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138024528/02_4c8206d10a7020b5db4906b82d33bb0.jpeg)

  
**Solution Advantages**
● Touch-based human-machine interface (HMI), integrated design, robust and durable.
● Wide temperature and humidity range and high protection rating design, adapting to harsh on-site environments with high temperature, high humidity, and excessive dust.
● Improves control accuracy, strictly controls curing process conditions, saves energy, and enhances production efficiency.

**Industry Applications**
● Smart Factory
● Smart Logistics
● Equipment Automation
