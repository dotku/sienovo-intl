The RK3568 chip is a mid-to-high-end general-purpose SoC, manufactured with a 22nm process technology, integrating a quad-core A55 processor and a Mali G52 GPU. It has a built-in independent NPU with 0.8 TOPS computing power. The chip features rich peripheral interfaces, supporting display peripherals such as HDMI, eDP, MIPI, and LVDS. Through interfaces like GPIO, RS485, RS232, and ADC, it can connect to PLC programmable controllers, photoelectric conversion module sensors, and more. In industrial applications, the RK3568 is primarily used as the processor for products such as IoT gateways, NVR storage, industrial panel PCs, industrial inspection devices, and industrial control boxes. It is widely applied in scenarios like industrial inspection, process control, data acquisition, control computers, and centralized IPC management.

●**Quad-core 64-bit Processor:**

●**Up to 8GB Memory:**
Supports up to 8GB DDR memory capacity, meeting the requirements of product application scenarios that demand large memory.

●**Integrated GPU/VPU/NPU Co-processors:**
Dual-core architecture GPU, high-performance VPU, and NPU. Supports OpenGL ES3.2 /2.0 /1.1, Vulkan1.1; 4K 60fps H.265 /H.264/VP9 video decoding and 1080P 100fps H.265/H.264 video encoding.

●**Rich Interfaces, Supporting Various Industrial Peripherals:**
Features interfaces such as 2x USB3.0, 8x USB2.0, MiPi DSI, eDP, I2C, UART, ADC, and GPIO. These can be used for external device control and expansion.
