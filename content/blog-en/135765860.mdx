Digital I/O, RMC, 1.33 GHz Dual-Core CPU, 1 GB DRAM, 4 GB Storage, Artix-7 75T FPGA, CompactRIO Single-Board Controller
The sbRIO-9603 is an embedded controller that integrates a real-time processor, user-reconfigurable FPGA, and digital I/O on a single printed circuit board (PCB). The sbRIO-9603 can be embedded in high-volume OEM applications that require flexibility, reliability, and high performance. The controller features two Ethernet connectors, an RS232 serial port, a CAN port, and an RMC connector for connecting two C Series I/O modules and 96 3.3 V digital I/O lines.
