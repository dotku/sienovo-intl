1. Pay attention to the version: There are versions for FriendlyARM and smdk2416.

2. Make sure to install the DNW driver (use the folder search method to locate driver files).

3. After installation, check "Device Manager" to see if the related device appears under categories such as "Universal Serial Bus devices".
