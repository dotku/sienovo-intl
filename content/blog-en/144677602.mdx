8-channel Vibration/Voltage Edge Computing Acquisition Board

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677602/01_3e21b1de7b8a4c22bb23a59533164f8.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677602/02_5242bf86eb1b430f8f2a17a77d6ff42b.png)

It adopts an EP4CE40F23I7 + TMS320C6748 architecture. Key features include the addition of a DSP for data edge computing, enhanced analog channel conditioning (e.g., zero-point calibration, gain switching, analog filter control, modulation/demodulation), and the addition of a 1-channel DAC output. This board is primarily suited for applications that do not require a high number of channels but demand high sampling resolution and accuracy, are sensitive to noise and cost, and require certain edge computing capabilities.
