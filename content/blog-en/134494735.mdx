This 8K network set-top box features the RK3588 chip platform and runs a clean Android 12 operating system, supporting expansion to Ubuntu and Debian systems. Primarily targeting overseas markets, this set-top box comes with dual HDMI output ports, one HDMI input port, an integrated dual-band WiFi 6 wireless module, Gigabit Ethernet, and USB interfaces. The product can be customized according to customer requirements and supports OEM and ODM orders.
