Introduction

In recent years, technologies such as IoT, cloud computing, machine learning, and cybersecurity have continuously driven the development of Industry 4.0. The "cloud-edge-device" architecture is gradually replacing the "cloud-pipe-device" architecture, and edge computing has become a key element for transformation in many fields in the new era. Taking smart manufacturing as an example, unlike CDN edge computing technology serving the internet, industrial sites also require more powerful and intelligent hyper-edge computers to directly serve data sources. It can operate in a closed local area network environment, directly providing intelligent computing services for OT (Operational Technology) devices. Through configurations of various specifications and multiple expandable hardware interfaces, it fully meets the demands for computing power and data storage in various industrial computing scenarios. Through local high-speed Ethernet or 5G private networks, it meets the network communication requirements for low latency, high bandwidth, and high reliability in industrial environments. By running industrial applications and AI algorithm models on hyper-edge computers, most business computations are completed within the local network environment, significantly improving business response speed, reducing internet traffic and bandwidth requirements, and minimizing data leakage risks, thereby increasing enterprise production efficiency while lowering overall production and operational costs. Therefore, Winghong Information's SuperSpirit 1 was born, with powerful performance and a leading intelligent edge computing operating system, to drive rapid digital transformation and widespread adoption of smart manufacturing for enterprises.
