1.  IP Modification Method

Modify the corresponding bits in the `lwipopts.h` file as follows:

#define STATIC\_IP\_ADDRESS 0xA9FE0305

Where 0xA9FE0302 is hexadecimal, corresponding to IP: 169.254.3.5. The meaning of the corresponding bits is:

****A9****:169

****FE****:254

**0****3**:3

**05**:5

Please modify as needed.
