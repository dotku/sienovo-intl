*   Railway Power Supply Monitoring

#### Smart Onboard Solutions

Smart onboard train solutions encompass a full range of products for locomotives and carriages, featuring high reliability, high scalability, and fully redundant systems. They comply with global standards and certifications and are field-proven. Smart onboard solutions can be rapidly deployed and integrated with existing systems.

**Applications:**

*   Train Control and Management System (TCMS)
*   Vehicle Monitoring System
*   Pantograph Monitoring System
*   Onboard Signaling System (ATS)
*   Passenger Information System (PIS)
*   Onboard Surveillance System
*   Onboard Infotainment and Wireless Systems

Wayside Solutions
Wayside solutions optimize wayside railway operational efficiency and safety. Integrating advanced AI vision computing technology into rugged designs, they are capable of withstanding high temperatures, high humidity, and high vibration. They perform well even in high-altitude areas and harsh environments.

Applications:
Power SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) system
Computer-based Interlocking System
Wayside Train Signaling System (ATS)
Centralized Traffic Control (CTC) system
Train-to-Ground Wireless Communication System

#### Rail Transit Solutions

Rail transit solutions provide various products for railway stations and train platforms. They feature high resistance to electromagnetic interference, water resistance, and vibration resistance, and are adaptable to various harsh environments. Supporting various communication protocols and lightweight programming languages, rail transit operators can easily adjust system settings and software programs according to different situations.

**Applications:**

*   Automatic Fare Collection (AFC) system
*   Building Automation System (BAS)
*   Fire Alarm System (FAS)
*   Integrated Supervisory Control System (ISCS)
*   Smart O&M (Operations and Maintenance) Platform
*   Smart O&M System for Rail Transit Depots
