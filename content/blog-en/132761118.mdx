Product Features:

Rich communication interfaces to meet control and communication requirements in various scenarios. Quad-core industrial-grade processor, offering high performance, low power consumption, and high reliability. Fanless design with a compact enclosure.

Equipped with SylixOS, a large-scale real-time operating system with 100% independently developed kernel, supporting POSIX interface specifications. It possesses fully independent and controllable intellectual property rights, meeting localization requirements, and features high real-time performance, high reliability, and high security. It provides the convenient and mature programming development suite RealEvo-IDE.

Supports CODESYS, enabling user secondary development.

Supports protocol stacks such as EtherCAT, CANOpen.

Supports QT, providing a convenient graphical development environment.

Supports hard real-time capabilities, with task switching in microseconds and interrupt response in nanoseconds.

Product Applications:

Wide range of applications, including industrial control: robot controllers, CNC controllers, motion controllers, AGV controllers, Soft PLCs, HMI integrated control systems; and power industry: relay protection devices, distribution terminals, and other fields. Users can quickly complete verification based on this platform, and also rapidly design products by combining this hardware platform (core board) with self-developed baseboards.

Reduces R&D costs, as users do not need to concern themselves with underlying platform development, allowing them to allocate more resources to application layer development and enhance product competitiveness.

Provides professional technical support and secondary development services from the original manufacturer.
