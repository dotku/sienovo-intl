##### **Applications in Electronic Kanban Systems**

The core of intelligent electronic kanban systems lies in achieving full visualization and automated management in factories, ultimately improving efficiency, reducing costs, and enhancing product quality. The hardware of an electronic kanban system mainly consists of two components: a miniature industrial computer and a display terminal (flat-screen TV or LCD).

**Requirements**  
● Support for high-definition large-screen display  
● Support for wired and wireless networking  
● Compact size for easy installation

**Solution**  
● EBC-1122 miniature industrial computer + flat-screen TV, supporting large-screen display  
● Supports both wired network and Wi-Fi communication

**Advantages**  
● Fanless, low-power industrial design ensures long-term stable operation  
● Supports VESA and wall-mount installation  
● Palm-sized ultra-compact form factor for convenient on-site installation

**Machine Vision Applications in Automotive Production Lines**

**Background**  
As a highly automated high-tech industry, automotive manufacturing has successfully integrated many advanced automation technologies into various production processes. Numerous stages in vehicle manufacturing now operate without human intervention, necessitating a reliable inspection technology to verify the correctness of each assembly step and the quality of assembled components. Machine vision technology, with its unique technical advantages, has become the preferred solution for automated inspection systems.

**Requirements**  
● High-performance CPU  
● Light source controller supporting 4 channels  
● Multiple Ethernet ports and isolated GPIO  
● Equipped with PCI/PCIe expansion slots

**Solution**  
The VBC series, designed specifically for machine vision applications, supports desktop-grade high-performance CPUs and integrates a 4-channel light source controller, 16-channel isolated GPIO, and supports external expansion of PoE and motion control cards. This provides an integrated hardware solution for automation in automotive production lines, effectively ensuring the stability of the entire machine vision system.
