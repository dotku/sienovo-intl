Train Control and Management System (TCMS) ![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/146248829/02_841b4ad75f304b42b681dc5af6792a12.png)

The Low-Vacuum Tube Maglev Hyperloop Train (referred to as "Hyperloop Train"), a major national strategic project, has seen the establishment of the Hyperloop Train Laboratory by China Aerospace Science and Industry Corporation (CASIC) Third Academy and other entities.

      The Hyperloop Train utilizes superconducting maglev technology to decouple from the ground, eliminating frictional resistance. The pipeline, which maintains a near-vacuum environment, significantly reduces air resistance, thereby achieving "near-ground flight" at speeds exceeding 1000 km/h.
