**Solution Overview**

The In-vehicle Terminal Application System for Key Passenger and Hazardous Goods Vehicles addresses road traffic safety issues currently faced by such vehicles during operation. It utilizes an intelligent supervision system that integrates government regulatory platforms, enterprise intelligent supervision centers, in-vehicle terminals, and communication networks. Through the interconnection of these components, it achieves business management, data interaction, and sharing, providing powerful supervision and monitoring means for the "three excesses" (over-speeding, overloading, and excessive fatigue driving) during the transportation process of key passenger and hazardous goods vehicles.

**Background and Challenges**

With the continuous development of the national economy, road transport has always been a vital component of the transportation system. Currently, China faces a severe road traffic safety situation, with road traffic accidents and major accidents occurring frequently every year. Accidents caused by passenger vehicles overloading, over-speeding, illegal operations, etc., account for a significant proportion. Consequently, traffic management departments have intensified law enforcement efforts and invested more energy in governance. Road passenger transport safety production concerns the survival and development of transport enterprises, the life and health of employees and the public, and the happiness of people's families and social harmony and stability. It is a crucial factor for construction and transport enterprises to grow stronger. Further strengthening safety production management, maximizing the control of various safety hazards, and preventing all types of accidents is an undeniable responsibility for enterprises today.

This solution introduces an in-vehicle terminal application system for key passenger and hazardous goods vehicles, which, through a GPS monitoring system, comprehensively manages various integrated information aspects for all company vehicles, including archive management, positioning and monitoring, and real-time dispatch. It effectively curbs issues such as vehicle over-speeding, detours, and slow emergency response. By strengthening road transport safety management and dynamic supervision of transport vehicles through the in-vehicle terminal system, it ensures that "key passenger and hazardous goods vehicles" can be monitored in real-time by enterprises and government platforms. This includes monitoring illegal behaviors such as over-speeding and fatigue driving by transport vehicle drivers, serving as an effective means to achieve scientific and safe development in road transport.

**Solution**

This solution uses Hikvision Video Surveillance Platform (iVMS-8600) as its carrier to achieve interconnection between various components such as government regulatory platforms, enterprise intelligent supervision centers, in-vehicle terminals, and communication networks. The integrated in-vehicle terminal system consists of a driving recorder and video all-in-one machine, a multi-mode navigation satellite positioning system, emergency alarm equipment, an in-vehicle information display screen, a voice intercom, an LED information display screen, a driver qualification certificate authentication module, and in-vehicle cameras. Combined with the GPS monitoring platform, it enables business management, data interaction, and sharing.

**Solution Highlights**

**1) High Capacity**

The system is designed with massive capacity, capable of supporting a large platform with millions of units. With excellent server and client design, the system can stably handle an extremely large number of vehicles and clients simultaneously online for extended periods. The designed capacity can reach millions.

**2) Good Compatibility**

Compatible with various databases, browsers, and terminal devices. It supports different types of databases (SQL Server 2000, Oracle), all current types of browsers, and terminals from different manufacturers.

**3) Stable Architecture**

Stable architecture, modular design, adaptable to systems of different scales, and easy to expand. The server-side architecture is modular and loosely coupled. For smaller operational scales, it can be centrally deployed. For larger operational scales, it can be distributed, reusing existing hardware resources and eliminating waste in hardware investment. At the same time, the server-side architecture fully considers load balancing; even if the operational scale expands indefinitely, it can be handled by simply adding hardware devices, without needing to adjust the software system architecture.

**4) Extensive System Expansion Capabilities**

The system has independent intellectual property rights, rich extensibility, and supports secondary development based on customer requirements. With independent intellectual property rights, its modular design facilitates user expansion, with reserved expansion modules for convenient secondary development.

**5) Highly Targeted Business Functionality**

Possesses extensive industry experience, covering various sectors such as official vehicles, hazardous materials vehicles, passenger transport, logistics, taxis, engineering vehicles, and ships. A strong understanding of the GPS industry assists users in completing daily monitoring operations in a simple and easy-to-use manner. All necessary functions for GPS monitoring are already provided within the system platform.

**6) Comprehensive Reporting**

Excellent backend data processing and report design, offering various horizontal and vertical analysis reports.

**7) System Security**

Automatically monitors system operation status, provides timely insights into system health, and enables rapid troubleshooting. A dedicated server is deployed to monitor the platform services 24/7 for normal operation.

**8) Comprehensive Electronic Maps**

Offers rich electronic maps for enhanced user experience and supports map integration from mainstream providers.
