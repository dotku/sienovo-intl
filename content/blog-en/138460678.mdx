## Loongson Dual-Socket Motherboard

■ Loongson 7A1000 chipset
■ 32GB ECC memory
■ 1 VGA output
■ Supports iKVM
■ 2 front USB 2.0 ports, 4 rear USB 2.0 ports
■ 4 Gigabit Ethernet ports
■ Supports Kylin OS
■ Operating temperature: -40℃～+65℃
■ Storage temperature: －55ºC～+70ºC

## 3AV6UT Loongson Trusted Motherboard

■ VPX 6U form factor, Open VPX specification

■ Domestic Loongson 3A3000 quad-core high-performance processor, 1.2GHz clock speed

■ 7A1000 chipset

■ Dual-channel DDR3 ECC 72-bit memory;

■ Standard configuration with domestic DDR3 memory chips, total capacity 16GB;

■ Onboard mSATA MLC SSD, standard capacity 512GB (optional 128GB, 256GB, 1TB)
