##### **Fleet Management Embedded In-Vehicle Industry Applications**

Fleet management solutions can effectively address the challenges of managing a large number of vehicles, and when combined with a dispatch system, they ensure more precise and effective execution of commands. They provide real-time monitoring of vehicle status, routes, and locations, guiding drivers for safe and orderly operation, and effectively reducing insurance costs, accident rates, and wear and tear on tires and components.

Solution Requirements
● Industrial-grade fanless compact rugged design
● Powerful network management capabilities
● Rich I/O interfaces for effective vehicle status detection
● Robust vibration resistance
● Wide voltage input

**Solution**
Introducing a basic, general-purpose modular in-vehicle computer, featuring a compact fanless structural design. It is equipped with an Intel Celeron platform high-performance, low-power processor and pre-installed with Windows Embedded Standard or Professional operating system, fully leveraging Microsoft Windows data processing capabilities in mobile environments. It optimizes application and network management capabilities while maintaining flexibility for multi-tasking applications. The VC-1000 can integrate modules such as 802.11a/b/g/n/ac, Bluetooth V4.0, full-network public LTE or private LTE, GPS/GNSS/Beidou, and CAN2.0B. Its power management features an 8~36V wide-range DC input with a power ignition delay on/off function, making it suitable for various fleet management application scenarios such as logistics fleets, port container trucks, and public transportation management. Meeting STD-MIL-810G vibration and shock resistance standards, it ensures reliable operation even in the harshest environments.
