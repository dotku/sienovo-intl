**Introduction to RK3588 AI Edge Computing Motherboard Specification**

### ******About This Document******

This document details the AI edge computing motherboard based on the Rockchip RK3588 chip, including its form factor, dimensions, technical specifications, and detailed hardware interface design reference instructions, enabling customers to quickly apply the RK3588 edge computing motherboard in smart terminal devices in AI fields such as industrial internet, smart cities, smart security, smart transportation, and smart healthcare.

1.  1.  ******Product Overview******

This product is an AI edge computing motherboard based on the Rockchip RK3588 architecture. The RK3588 is a new generation domestic flagship high-performance 64-bit octa-core processor, fabricated with 8nm process technology. It features high computing power, low power consumption, powerful multimedia capabilities, and rich data interfaces. It is equipped with an octa-core CPU (quad-core A76 + quad-core A55) and an ARM G610MP4 GPU, with a built-in NPU offering 6 TOPs computing power. It supports 8K@60fps video decoding and 8K@30fps encoding. It offers a rich set of peripheral interfaces and excellent expandability.
