Phytium FT1500A-16 6U VPX High-Performance Dense Computing Blade

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138380388/01_19eec8f1910be3a790b1df1872c60ab8.png)

A high-performance 6U VPX single-board display computer compliant with the OpenVPX VITA65 standard. The product adopts the mainstream GPGPU architecture, integrating an FT1500A-4 quad-core processor from Tianjin Phytium and a high-performance embedded GPU, the AMD® Radeon® E8860. The E8860 delivers industry-leading 3D graphics performance and ultra-high multi-display technology, supporting 2K/4K high-resolution displays. It is ideal for embedded applications requiring exceptional multi-display experiences and advanced visual effects. Powered by a high-performance domestic ARM processor, the design is mechanically compatible with industrial control and ruggedized systems, enabling it to replace x86-based display and control subsystems or integrated display-processing units in ground-based, airborne, and shipborne radar systems, while fully supporting existing radar applications. On the software side, the development team has already assisted a customer in completing driver development for the E8860, along with foundational graphics libraries and graphical environments, achieving substantial technical progress.

Featuring the mainstream GPGPU architecture, this blade is suitable for high-performance computing (HPC) centers, parallel computing, computing clusters, signal processing, and display control applications.
