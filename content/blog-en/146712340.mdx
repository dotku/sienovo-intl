### ******3, VPX Signal Definition******

Definition Principles:

1.  Complies with the 601 standard;
2.  Based on the 601 standard, uses vacant pins to add frequently used interface signals;
3.  For infrequently or rarely used signals, they can be left unconnected when not in use, and connected according to the signal definition when needed.
