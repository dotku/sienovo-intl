*   ****Core Hardware Configuration****

****1. Controller Chip****‌

*   Uses domestic TL63104 controller chip, supports PCIe transfer rates of 2.5GT/s, 5GT/s, 8GT/s, 16GT/s, 32GT/s, and supports 96+8 lanes. Supports 6 x16 groups and 1 x8 group, with each group supporting 1 to 8 ports. x16 groups support x16, x8, x4, x2 ports; x8 groups support x8, x4, x2, x1 ports.
*   Basic Mode: Extends PCIe interfaces and supports multi-level expansion, as shown in the figure below.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/148793340/01_4393249fe01f4dcd99c1f3d8686a2768.png)

*   NT Mode: Interconnects multiple PCIe domains, supporting NTB and DMA features, as shown in the figure below.![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/148793340/02_13fb9a5eb3c4ee1927812c7fc12df48.png)

*   ‌P2P Mode: Supports data transfer between any DPs, effectively increasing transfer bandwidth and reducing transfer latency.
*   Supports non-blocking crossbar architecture.
*   Integrates SM2/SM3/SM4 encryption engines to ensure data transmission security, meeting domestic replacement requirements.
*   Supports standard hot-plug and surprise hot-plug.![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/148793340/03_b89a0088693d4eb48e5ca923ab0e6fa2.png)

****2. Expansion Interfaces****‌

*   Upstream Bus Interface: PCIe Gen5 x16, gold finger connector.
*   Downstream Interfaces and Connectors:

*   1x PCIe Gen5 x16, connector model 10146070-113Z0LF, external gold finger connector.
*   4x PCIe Gen5 x16, connector model G97V24332HR, external cable.
*   2x PCIe Gen5 x4, connector model G97V21352HR, external cable.

*   ****‌Performance****

*   Supports 32GT/s single-lane speed, with bidirectional bandwidth up to 128GB/s (x16 aggregation).
