Solar Integrated Storage and Control Unit

Technical Specifications:

MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) technology with efficiency up to ≥99.5%. Utilizes high-quality components to ensure stable and precise control, unaffected by environmental factors. Supports local and remote control, power cycle/reboot, and a five-segment timing mode. Withstands harsh environments, with an operating temperature range of -20°C to 70°C. The battery pack features multiple protections including overcharge, PV and battery reverse connection protection, and output overcurrent and short-circuit protection. GPS positioning for anti-loss, with real-time navigation via cloud platform map mode to locate the device. Applicable scenarios include engineering projects for roads, scenic spots, fish ponds, aquaculture bases, and agricultural bases. Smart operation, supporting remote monitoring via mini-programs and PCs for parameter settings and controller reboot. RS485 communication bus and standard Modbus communication protocol, meeting communication needs for various applications.
