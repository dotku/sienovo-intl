Introduction

Humanity faces unprecedented challenges and potential food crises due to the impacts of climate change and population growth. To better utilize limited resources and increase crop yields, leveraging the power of modern technology has become increasingly important. Smart agriculture not only helps improve harvests but also conserves human resources and reduces the harmful effects of pesticides and chemical fertilizers.

Application Requirements

A client of Shinmax Technology has been facing severe water shortages and insufficient human resources. Although there is strong market demand for safe organic food, unfortunately, due to the nature of its production, it suffers from severe pest problems.

Therefore, the industry believes that a modern, intelligent, and more efficient farming method is crucial for its long-term survival.

System Solution

To meet these needs, Shinmax Technology has launched a series of smart agriculture solutions using high-performance embedded RISC systems. For this client, Shinmax Technology deployed a solution including an XM5728-GW, an Arm-based box with wireless IoT nodes, combined with remote management software services to manage temperature/humidity optimization, remote farm management, automatic irrigation, pest control, and energy control. The fully integrated solution helps overcome water shortages through better resource management and allows organic food producers to operate more efficiently and profitably.

Solution Advantages

Integrated solution from smart gateways and sensor nodes - utilizing Linux and DeviceOn built-in software for connectivity and remote management.

Reliable wireless sensor network solution that is easy to implement.
