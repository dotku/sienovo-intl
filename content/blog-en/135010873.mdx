## Edge Computing Box

Rockchip RV1126 | 2.0 TOPS INT8/INT16

● Integrated NPU with computing power up to 2.0 TOPS@INT8/INT16.

● Supports H.264/H.265/MJPEG video encoding and decoding; supports multi-level video quality configuration and encoding complexity settings.

● Encoding and decoding performance:

    3840 x 2160@30 fps + 1080p@30 fps encoding

    3840 x 2160@30 encoding + 3840 x 2160@30 fps decoding

● Maximum decoding resolution: 4096x2304@30fps.

● Supports a series of deep learning frameworks including TensorFlow, MXNet, PyTorch, Caffe, meeting diverse developer requirements.

● Rich peripheral interfaces: Ethernet, HDMI in/out, RS232, RS485, Line in/out, Wi-Fi, TF, etc.

The edge computing box is a deep intelligent edge computing device designed by Xinmai Technology based on the RV1126. The core RV1126 adopts a highly integrated precision architecture, featuring a quad-core ARM Cortex-A7@1.5GHz and RISC-V architecture. The entire device features lightweight design, high performance, low power consumption, and easy integration, making it widely applicable in facial recognition, intelligent security, behavior analysis, and other fields.
