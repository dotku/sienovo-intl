Application Scenarios

With the development of high-tech industries and national defense modernization in China, there is an increasing demand for large optoelectronic devices in fields such as aviation and aerospace, micro-devices in micro-electromechanical and optoelectronic information fields, and the processing of complex parts. All these applications necessitate the use of precision, or even ultra-precision, machining and inspection equipment.

##### Customer Requirements

High precision, high efficiency
