**Qingniao Fire Protection Co., Ltd.** was established in June 2001 and was listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange in August 2019, becoming the first enterprise in China's fire alarm industry to be listed on the A-share market. The company consistently focuses on fire safety and IoT fields, with its main business involving the R&D, production, and sales of "one-stop" fire safety system products. The company's products cover the entire chain of fire alarm and firefighting: early warning → alarm → fire prevention → evacuation and escape → extinguishing, providing comprehensive protection services for people's lives and property. The company takes safeguarding people's lives and property as its mission, builds its product system with completely independent intellectual property rights, achieves excellent results with a comprehensive market channel network, and wins customers through standardized and rigorous professional services. Qingniao Fire Protection has been actively expanding into overseas markets, establishing production bases, R&D, and marketing centers in North America, Europe, and other regions, to expand its international market with products that comply with international standards such as UL, ULC, and FCC. Today, Qingniao Fire Protection ranks among the top in the national industry in terms of brand awareness, corporate strength, and product capabilities, and has developed into an outstanding representative of China's firefighting industry moving towards internationalization.

As an advanced technology integrating multiple disciplines, artificial intelligence is driving continuous increases in society's demands for the technical level and application of firefighting equipment. Traditional firefighting equipment based on existing technologies can no longer meet current societal demands for intelligent, timely, and convenient smart operation of fire rescue equipment. Currently, in the ongoing development of smart cities, a crucial aspect is the construction of smart firefighting systems. Smart firefighting construction provides fundamental safety guarantees for urban public security. Perception technologies, communication network interoperability, and smart applications of urban basic functions involved in smart firefighting construction are also core and key elements of smart city development. Based on the above analysis, it can be seen that smart firefighting, as a scientific technology, possesses characteristics of systematicity and complexity.

The development and construction of smart firefighting technology primarily relies on intelligent fire management and control systems powered by advanced AI, such as advanced IoT technology, data communication technology, and information integration technology. Currently, smart firefighting technology has been widely applied and promoted in intelligent fire supervision and practical fire emergency rescue operations.
