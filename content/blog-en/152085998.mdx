A communication management unit specifically designed for the power industry, equipped with a domestic CPU. It offers a wealth of communication interfaces, including multiple network ports and serial ports, meeting the requirements for comprehensive substation monitoring. Compatible with domestic operating systems such as Kylin and Neokylin, it has passed high-level EMC tests and is designed to meet the stringent on-site usage requirements of power and energy environments.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/152085998/01_a4bdc525e9884f26a1aea644d4037ad7.jpeg)

Based on Phytium 4-core/8-core processors, this highly localized ARM communication management platform offers 8 Gigabit Ethernet ports and 8 isolated serial ports.

*   Phytium FT-2000/4-core or D2000/8-core processor, main frequency 2.3~2.6GHz
*   8 Gigabit Ethernet ports, 8 full-featured isolated serial ports
*   HDMI+VGA dual display interfaces
*   3 USB2.0, 2 USB3.0
*   Supports 2 sets of SATA hard drive storage
*   Supports 2 channels of 100~240V AC/DC power input
*   Supports CAN communication (replaces 4-channel/8-channel COM)
*   Fanless design for the entire unit
*   Ultra-high localization rate for the entire unit![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/152085998/02_ba98a5dc28fc4b3db1eaebe90bc7bbd8.png)
*   The ECU-461 is an embedded communication management platform based on the domestic Phytium FT-2000/4 (compatible with D2000/8) processor. It features 8 onboard Gigabit Ethernet ports and 8 isolated serial ports, with support for replacing 4 or 8 serial ports with CAN communication. An internal PCI-E bus interface is reserved, allowing for customized expansion of additional LAN or COM ports, as well as wireless communication options.
*   ECU-461
