The Trevista CI Dome combines Cognex's patented computational imaging algorithms with structured diffuse dome illumination to deliver unparalleled topographical image quality, providing innovative solutions for inspecting both glossy and matte surfaces. This helps to: **reduce inspection time, lower reject rates and costs, and detect more defects faster**.

_**1**_

**Shape from Shading (SfS) technology** captures raw intensity images and separates shape information from grayscale information to provide high-quality topographical images.

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139316449/02_197621a8cb525500ef9bc996f9d11e53.jpeg)

_**2**_

Compared to other CI systems, the Trevista CI Dome can capture synthetic topographical images **2x faster**, improving efficiency and throughput and reducing inspection time.

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139316449/03_2eb589745f1eafd14c105552f03f6442.jpeg)

_**3**_

**Dome diffuse illumination** captures surface gradients, AI tools classify defect severity, and the Trevista CI Dome provides highly detailed surface visualization, **reducing reject rates and costs**.
