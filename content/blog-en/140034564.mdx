Binocular stereo vision is based on the principle of parallax, acquiring images of an object from different positions using imaging devices, matching the positional offsets of corresponding points to obtain disparity data, and then calculating the object's spatial three-dimensional information. We offer a high-image-quality binocular stereo camera, featuring advantages such as high resolution, low power consumption, and long range, making it suitable for various applications like robotic lawnmowers, robot vacuums, and AGVs.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/140034564/01_5aa0f47a26d44bfaa374ae617bf56c19.png)

This camera module is a depth camera that performs 3D imaging based on active binocular structured light. It consists of two IR image sensors, one RGB image sensor, one infrared speckle projector, and one IMU, making it suitable for applications such as obstacle avoidance and navigation in robots, AGVs, AMRs, and drones.

Resolution 1280H*800V

Frame Rate Up to 1280*800@30fps

Operating Temperature -20-50℃

Dimensions 90.75mm*25mm*30mm

FOV H84°*V59°±3°

*   3D imaging based on structured light
    
*   Depth measurement up to 25m
