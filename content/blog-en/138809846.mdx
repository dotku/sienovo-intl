The Hotel Front Desk Self-Service Kiosk Solution is based on self-service terminals, enabling guests to complete check-in/check-out with simple operations. This significantly improves hotel operational efficiency, enhances guest experience, and reduces labor costs.

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138809846/01_dc9f1e10c694bd11982593b7e431238a.jpeg)

This solution addresses the following pain points of traditional front desk check-in methods:

1. High traffic and delayed reception
2. Long guest queuing times
3. Insufficient staffing during hotel peak hours
4. Guest privacy protection concerns

#### 

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138809846/02_b0da331dbb4fe5ba0885cdf894755405.png)

**Project Business Requirements**

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138809846/03_dff096d0150fe897541cb3dde0c7eec8.png)

#### 

#### 

1. **Touchscreen Operation:** Supports touchscreen operation, allowing guests to tap on the screen to self-check-in, check-out, extend their stay, print invoices, manage membership, and perform other operations.

2. **ID Card Recognition:** Supports ID card recognition, allowing guests to place their ID card directly on the terminal. The system will automatically read the ID card information and fill in the check-in registration form, avoiding the tediousness and errors of manual data entry.

3. **Facial Recognition:** Supports facial recognition. During registration, the terminal will require guests to perform facial recognition and compare the recognition results with their ID card information to ensure identity verification.

4. **Payment Function:** To facilitate guests paying room fees, the self-service terminal can integrate payment functions, supporting various payment methods such as bank cards, Alipay, and WeChat Pay. Guests can complete payment directly on the terminal after registration.

5. **Check-in Credential Printing:** After completing registration and payment, the self-service terminal can automatically print check-in credentials, such as room cards and invoices. Guests can then directly access their rooms with these credentials.

**Core Motherboard Solution Advantages**

This solution offers optional configurations based on actual hotel needs, including the IoT-3288E powered by the RK3288 platform and the AIoT-3568A powered by the RK3568 platform. Both motherboards offer significant advantages in performance and configurations, fully meeting the various functional requirements of terminal devices.

*   **IoT-3288E**
    

**Motherboard Advantages:**

The IoT-3288E's advantage lies in its rich expansion interfaces, allowing connection to various external devices and offering extremely strong expandability.

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138809846/05_b3400e49c178ccfbd9cea60d3decf6ff.png)

**Motherboard Configuration**

Core Configuration

CPU: RK3288 quad-core processor, main frequency up to 1.8GHz

Operating System: Android 7.1/11.0

Memory: Standard 2GB (4GB optional)

Storage: Standard 16GB (8GB/32GB/64GB optional)

Display Interfaces

HDMI Output: 1 port, supports 1080P@60Hz, 4Kx2K@60Hz output

LVDS Output: 1 single/dual channel, can directly drive 50/60Hz LCD screens

eDP Output: Can directly drive eDP interface LCD screens of various resolutions

Expansion Interfaces

USB: 6 x USB 2.0

Serial Ports: 1 x TTL, 4 x RS232, 1 x Bluetooth multiplexed serial port, 11 x GPIO, 1 x ADC, 1 x IIC, button interface

Storage Card: Supports TF card

Network Support

Wireless Network: Built-in WiFi; Bluetooth 4.2

Wired Network: Supports 10/100M adaptive Ethernet

Mobile Network: Supports 4G internet via PCI-E interface

*   **AIoT-3568A**
    

**Motherboard Advantages:**

The AIoT-3568A is based on the RK3568 quad-core processor, possessing powerful core processing capabilities, with a built-in independent NPU capable of providing 1 TOPS of computing power.

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138809846/06_79ae4624a0267d933d476fab26a13257.png)

**Motherboard Configuration**

Core Configuration

CPU: RK3568 quad-core processor, main frequency up to 2.0GHz, 1 TOPS computing power

Operating System: Android 11.0 (supports Linux/OpenHarmony)

Memory: Standard 2GB (4GB/8GB optional)

Storage: Standard 16GB (32GB/64GB/128GB optional)

Display Interfaces

HDMI Output: 1 port, standard Type-A female connector, supports up to 4Kx2K@60Hz resolution

LVDS Output: 1 port, supports single/dual 6-8bit, can directly drive 50/60Hz LCD screens, maximum LVDS screen resolution 1920*1080

eDP Output: 1 port, can directly drive eDP interface LCD screens of various resolutions

Expansion Interfaces

USB: 1 x USB 3.0, 5 x USB 2.0

Serial Ports: 2 x RS232, 2 x TTL, 1 x RS485

I2C Interface: 1 port, can connect I2C interface TP or peripherals

CAN: 1 port

Storage Card: TF card

Network Support

Wireless Network: Built-in WiFi; Bluetooth 4.2

Wired Network: Supports 10/100/1000M adaptive Ethernet

Mobile Network:

1. Supports 4G internet via PCI-E interface
2. Supports 5G internet via M.2 5G interface (optional)
