Energy Storage and Environmental Monitoring Controllers are primarily applied in various power/new energy scenarios such as power equipment monitoring, photovoltaics, energy storage, charging piles, wind power generation systems, microgrid scenarios, and virtual power plants.![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/150555556/02_1bb8b4cc43034d20a1543dedd8eaac10.png)

Features

1.  Multi-interface Support: The device is equipped with 4 external RJ45 network ports, featuring switch functionality and supporting network communication. Additionally, it provides multiple DI inputs, DO dry contact outputs, AI inputs, and AO outputs to meet the connection requirements of various devices.

2.  Strong Communication Capability: This controller has a built-in 4G communication module, with an optional 5G module.

3.  Multiple Storage Methods: To ensure data security and reliability, this controller supports multiple storage methods, including SD card storage, EEPROM storage, and FLASH storage, ensuring long-term data preservation and fast retrieval.

4.  Strong Extensibility: This controller can be externally connected with LoRa, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, 433MHz, and 2.4G modules. It supports the latest **Huawei NearLink communication** and **HarmonyOS**, making it the optimal product for localized and specialized application scenarios.

Advantages

1.  Real-time Monitoring and Control: Through this controller, users can monitor the operational status of devices in real-time and remotely adjust their working status. This real-time monitoring and control enhances device operational efficiency and accuracy, reducing the risk of failures.

2.  Customization Support: A professional R&D team can provide customized functional and appearance designs, tailored to customer requirements. This flexibility allows the controller to adapt to the needs of different industries and specific scenarios, offering tailored solutions.

Application Scenarios

Power/New Energy: Can be applied in power equipment monitoring, solar power generation systems, wind power generation systems, PV energy storage charging piles, microgrid scenarios, etc., to achieve remote monitoring and control of energy equipment and improve energy utilization efficiency.

Summary

The Energy Storage and Environmental Monitoring Controller, with its rich features, flexible customization support, and wide application in multiple scenarios, provides the new energy industry with an efficient and intelligent device management solution.
