A precision Source Measure Unit (SMU) can perform four-quadrant operation, precisely outputting voltage or current while simultaneously measuring voltage, current, and resistance. It integrates the functions of a 6½-digit Digital Multimeter (DMM), a 5½-digit precision voltage source, current source, electronic load, and pulse generator. Its rich features, compact size, and standard test interfaces make it ideal for integration into test fixtures. The Source Measure Unit platform is primarily used for IVR testing and measurement of semiconductors, sensors, modules, and similar components. It is custom-designed for production line testing, providing efficient and cost-effective test and measurement solutions for production line automation in ICT (In-Circuit Test) and FCT (Functional Test).
