The PCR gene amplification instrument, also known as a PCR nucleic acid amplification instrument or polymerase chain reaction nucleic acid amplification instrument, is used for applications such as determining genetic disease patterns in samples, paternity testing, gene replication, and the diagnosis of infectious diseases.

The core board features a connectorized design. This industrial-grade core board is a tailor-made solution specifically for low-power, low-cost, and compact applications, making it ideal for energy-saving and compact uses such as HDMI, control systems, and IoT. It provides Linux system support, allowing you to select cost-optimized core board configurations based on your actual project needs to meet various application and cost requirements.
