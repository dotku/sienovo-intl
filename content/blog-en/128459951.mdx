**Specifications**

**Parameters**

**Product**

FT-2000/4, COM Express® Compact Type 6

**Processor**

FT-2000/4 integrates 4 FTC663 processor cores, main frequency 2.2, 2.6, 3.0GHz, L2 cache 4MB, L3 cache 4MB

**BIOS**

Uboot/UEFI/Custom firmware

**Memory**

Onboard 16GB domestic DDR4 memory

**Ethernet Interface**

1 Gigabit Ethernet port, 10/100/1000M adaptive network signal (FeiTeng CPU embedded network controller)

**Serial Ports**

2 TTL serial ports, derived from FeiTeng FT2000/4, where COM0 is the debug serial port and COM1 is the functional serial port

**Storage**

Onboard 64GB SSD

**USB Interface**

4 USB3.0 ports, compatible with USB2.0;
4 USB2.0 ports, with OC function

**SATA**

4 SATA III 6Gbps ports, compatible with SATA I 1.5Gbps and SATA II 3Gbps transmission (SATA3 is connected to the onboard SSD by default)

**PCIe Interface**

PCIe lane0~3: 1 PCIe2.0 x4 (from bridge chip);
PCIe lane4: 1 PCIe2.0 x1;
PCIe lane5~6: 2 PCIe3.0 x1;
PEG lane0~15: 2 PCIe3.0 x8, configurable as 1 PCIe3.0 x16.

**Other Interfaces**

2 I2C interfaces; 4 GPO, 4 GPI; 1 SPI master interface; supports RTC

**Power**

Input: DC 8V~14V, typical 12V; Power mode, supports ATX or AT power-on modes

**Dimensions**

125mm×95mm (L×W)

**Weight**

＜300g

**Power Consumption**

TYP: ≤22W (System loaded, idle state)
MAX: ≤35W (Full load state)

**Operating Temperature**

0℃-50℃, -20℃-+60℃, -40℃-+85℃ (Optional)

**Storage Temperature**

-40℃-+85℃

**Humidity**

5% to 95%, non-condensing

**Features**

Small size, low power consumption, high performance.
Diverse functional interfaces, uses high-density connectors for excellent shock resistance.
Low cost, strong expandability, customizable baseboards according to user needs.
Flexible product, easy to maintain, long lifecycle.

**Software Support**

Galaxy Kylin (Kylin)
UnionTech (UOS)
E-Hui (SylixOS)
