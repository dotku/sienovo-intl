Anesthesia Depth Monitor

Biochemical analyzers, often simply called biochemical instruments, are devices that use the principle of photoelectric colorimetry to measure specific chemical components in body fluids. Due to their fast measurement speed, high accuracy, and small reagent consumption, they are now widely used in hospitals of all levels, epidemic prevention stations, and family planning service centers. Their use can significantly improve the efficiency and benefits of routine biochemical testing.

A certain company adopted an Allwinner core board design. With the enhancement of ARM performance, the control section switched to using an ARM main control board. It runs on a Linux system, is powered by a lithium battery, is compact and portable, and is used for rapid microbial detection in medical systems.
