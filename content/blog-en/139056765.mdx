Smart cash registers help physical stores upgrade their services, effectively improving checkout efficiency and increasing revenue. As a result, they are widely used in industries such as retail, catering, beauty, specialty stores, gas stations, automotive services, and education & training. To better serve partners, we have launched several professional Android motherboards tailored for smart cash register applications. These motherboards offer advantages such as low power consumption and rich interface options, along with comprehensive support including system, software, and peripherals, making them highly popular in the market.

**XM-3566SF**

**Motherboard Positioning:** Mini form factor, primarily designed for single-screen POS terminals.

**Motherboard Dimensions:** 100\*80mm, board thickness: 1.6mm

![Image](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139056765/02_cd4decc3769af38dd813bff34ec43f48.jpeg)

**Key Features:**

① Equipped with RK3566 quad-core processor, up to 1.8GHz clock speed, running Android 11 OS, providing 1T computing power support.

② Supports single-display output via HDMI/LVDS/MIPI/eDP interfaces; compatible with various mainstream touchscreens including infrared, optical, capacitive, resistive, and touch film. Supports plug-and-play HID configuration for touchscreens, no driver or calibration required.

③ Supports 7 USB interfaces (3x 4-pin 1.25mm connectors, 2x dual-layer USB ports).

④ Supports 2x TTL, 2x RS232 (RJ11, RJ12 cash drawer compatible serial ports), 1x debug interface, 4x GPIO interfaces, and 1x TP interface.

⑤ Built-in printer power supply, enabling direct integration of printing functionality.

⑥ Supports MIPI camera, compatible with up to 10MP MIPI camera modules.

⑦ Ethernet design supports 10/100M adaptive Ethernet, 2.4G Wi-Fi (5G compatible), single antenna, and Bluetooth 4.0.
