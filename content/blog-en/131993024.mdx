With the rapid development of technologies such as the Internet of Things (IoT), big data, and artificial intelligence, edge computing has become a hot topic in the field of information technology. In the IoT domain, edge computing is widely applied in various fields such as smart transportation, smart security, and industrial applications. Therefore, industrial motherboard design solutions based on edge computing technology are also receiving increasing attention.

The RK3588 AI is an AI chip launched by Rockchip, primarily designed for edge computing. This chip integrates Rockchip's latest AI algorithms, enabling various AI applications such as speech recognition, natural language processing, and image recognition. Additionally, the RK3588 AI also supports automatic speech-to-vision conversion technology, allowing for gesture control without physical touch. Therefore, integrating the RK3588 AI chip into industrial motherboard designs can significantly enhance device intelligence and user experience.

This article will introduce the RK3588 AI edge computing smart transportation and smart security industrial motherboard design solution from the following aspects.
