8-Channel I/O (Input/Output)

High-Precision Data Acquisition Terminal Board

The hardware architecture utilizes a domestic AG16KF256 FPGA, externally expanded with 16MB of SDRAM to serve as a transmit buffer for the Gigabit Ethernet interface. Compared to similar hardware architectures featuring an ARM processor, while a pure FPGA architecture offers less edge computing capability than an ARM-based one, it boasts a maximum sampling rate of 256 kSps, making it highly suitable for high-sampling-rate, pure data acquisition applications.

The product also features 4 digital input channels and 4 digital output channels, allowing for control of external devices.
