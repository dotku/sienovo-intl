The HarmonyOS All-in-One Access Control Solution uses the HarmonyOS AIoT-3568X AI motherboard as its core platform, running the OpenHarmony operating system. Utilizing self-developed algorithms and domestic chips, it ensures manageability and controllability, and provides identity recognition and support for an ecosystem of other peripheral accessories.
