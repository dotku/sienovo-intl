### Railway TCMS System Integrated Display Unit

The DDU, also known as the Driver Display Unit, is used to show the driver the operating status of the signaling system and traction system, and is widely applied in metro and railway rolling stock.

This is a railway TCMS system integrated display unit, featuring an Intel@ ATOM™ N2600 processor. It is designed for operation in TX temperature environments ranging from -40 to +70ºC and meets the electromagnetic compatibility, shock, and vibration test requirements of the European railway application standard EN50155. It is a hardware display unit specifically designed for Railway Train Control and Management Systems (TCMS).

*   High-brightness, sunlight-readable touchscreen display with automatic brightness adjustment based on ambient light.
*   Intel@ ATOM™ N2600 dual-core processor, 2GB DDR3 memory.
*   Die-cast magnesium-aluminum alloy enclosure, front panel IP65, rear IP42.
*   2 x M12-D 10/100Mb Ethernet ports, upgradeable to 1000Mb.
*   DC16V ~ 160V or DC60-160V input, suitable for various customer power supply requirements.
*   PC104 slot for MVB, CANOpen, Profibus expansion cards.
*   2 x DB9 isolated serial ports: 1 x RS23, 1 x RS485.
*   Compliant with European Railway Standard EN 50155.
