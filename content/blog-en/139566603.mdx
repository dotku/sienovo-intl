### Oscilloscope Modules

• 32-bit Resolution

• 125 MSPS Sampling Rate

• Supports Single-Channel/Dual-Channel Module Selection

• Low-speed mode enables real-time power distribution and overall device power detection

• High-speed mode enables signal analysis and power-up timing measurements

##### **Application Scenarios**

• Capture and analyze waveform parameters such as period, amplitude, and anomalous signals

• Power ripple and noise analysis

• Signal template comparison

• Wireless charging (signal demodulation)
