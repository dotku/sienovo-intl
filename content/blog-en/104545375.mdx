The Xinmai Industrial Equipment IoT Cloud Platform is a highly customizable information platform developed based on Xinmai's core operational control products and integrated information technology. It is designed to adapt to the network interconnection of industrial equipment, provide value-added services to customers, create a new ecosystem for technical services, and bridge the last mile of industrial applications. This platform communicates with the cloud platform through the network communication function in the controller, offering a fully integrated and interconnected solution spanning device control, sensing layer, scheduling layer, and information management layer, allowing for rapid deployment without extensive development.

The Industrial Equipment IoT Cloud Platform is widely applied in fields such as smart manufacturing, digital factories, and "Internet+".

Manager Liang Quan, Head of SAP Greater China South Region Business Unit, stated that Xinmai Technology is a reliable partner in the field of digital factory engineering equipment.
