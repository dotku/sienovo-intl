C6678 +K7+AD9253/AD9783 8-Channel Signal Processing Board Introduction

Power Consumption: ≤ 35W

Weight: ≤ 150g

Dimensions: 105mm*180mm

Supply Voltage: 5V

All selected components are imported industrial-grade.

Analog-to-Digital Converter (ADC): 2 x AD9253 (125MHz sampling, total 8 SSMA coaxial channels)
(Compatible with AD9653, 16-bits)

Digital-to-Analog Converter (DAC): 1 x AD9783 (500MHz, 14-bits, 1 SSMA coaxial channel)

FPGA: 1 x XC7K325T-FFG900

Digital Signal Processor (DSP): 1 x TMS320C6678 (clock frequency 1GHz)

DDR3: 4 x 256Mx16bit

UART: 1 x TL16C754 (4 transmit/receive channels)

Clock Management Chip: AD9512 (receives external clock, 1 SSMA coaxial channel)

Gigabit Ethernet: Rectangular connector (J30J9) or RJ45 interface

Data Acquisition: Optical port (10Gbps)

Interface I/O: 21 groups of RS422/485 (J30J31),
2 transmit, 2 receive RS232 (J30J25),
3 transmit, 3 receive isolated RS422 (ADM26815, J30J15),
5 groups of TTL 3.3V (J30J25: 3 transmit, 2 receive, 3 SSMA channels)
