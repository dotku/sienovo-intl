### What is CompactDAQ?

CompactDAQ systems can acquire and deliver validated data that meets your testing needs, regardless of distance or environment. These customizable, portable solutions are composed of different data acquisition modules, which can achieve synchronized measurements over a network, helping you digitize data. They can also be deployed closer to sensors, thereby minimizing noise and simplifying field wiring.

Precise Sensor Measurements

NI offers over 70 models of high-quality C Series I/O modules with built-in sensor or signal conditioning capabilities for you to choose from. You can mix and match different I/O modules to build precise and repeatable systems that meet any measurement need.

![System Scalability](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/134282158/03_a3aeff18398d28e2093478b777f59b0b.png)

System Scalability

Use Ethernet-compatible CompactDAQ chassis to expand and deploy systems, achieving microsecond-level synchronized measurements across multiple chassis or in distributed environments.

![Compact and Rugged Design](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/134282158/04_f229883bb77ee1ecd4d39bdabd7f20a0.png)

Compact and Rugged Design

CompactDAQ's rugged features can withstand operating temperatures from -40°C to 70°C and shock up to 30 g, making them suitable not only for laboratory use but also for field operations. For extreme operating environments, you can use IP67-rated FieldDAQTM devices.

![Program or Not, the Choice is Yours](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/134282158/05_d1567985cfed9daec8807bee138fe663.png)

Program or Not, the Choice is Yours

You can use .NET, C#, Python, or LabVIEW to develop custom data logging applications, or use FlexLogger™ application software to quickly configure systems and record and view data without programming.
