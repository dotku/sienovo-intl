32-Channel (24-bit) High-Precision Data Acquisition Core Board

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677176/01_de4505a7daac4accab9f5cfac90e3f93.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677176/02_1f3b23f7694b4cefa89da46665ee44a6.png)

Featuring an EP4CE115F29I7 model FPGA, this is a high-precision, multi-channel dynamic signal acquisition core board. The core board is primarily divided into two modules: a communication board and an acquisition board, both controlled by an FPGA. Its main application is for dynamic acquisition and data transmission within the company's internal chassis, with power supplied by the chassis.
