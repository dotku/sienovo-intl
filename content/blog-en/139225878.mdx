Imaging and vision technology specialist has recently officially launched a series of camera products compatible with the NVIDIA DRIVE AGX Orin platform. These self-developed digital coaxial GMSL2 camera modules meet the high-quality imaging requirements of intelligent vehicles.

Currently, there are 11 camera models available for the NVIDIA DRIVE AGX Orin platform. These products utilize image sensors from ON Semiconductor, Sony, and OmniVision with resolutions ranging from 2 to 8 megapixels. They support YUV/RAW data output formats and feature high dynamic range (HDR) and LED flicker mitigation (LFM).

These camera products offer long-distance transmission, high data rates, and strong anti-interference capabilities, making them suitable for applications requiring long-range, low-latency, and high-quality data transmission. They have already been deployed in various vehicles, vehicle-infrastructure coordination systems, unmanned ground vehicles (UGVs), large-scale robots, and industrial robotic arms.

 **NVIDIA DRIVE AGX Orin Developer Kit**

NVIDIA DRIVE Orin™ is a centralized computing platform designed for intelligent vehicles, delivering massive computational performance (254 trillion operations per second) for highly automated and autonomous driving applications. Since its release, it has become a flagship SoC in the autonomous driving domain and serves as an ideal solution for powering autonomous driving functions, surround-view perception, digital instrument clusters, and AI-powered cockpits.

The DRIVE AGX Orin Developer Kit provides the hardware, software, and sample applications required for production-grade autonomous vehicle development. Built on production-ready, automotive-grade chips, the DRIVE AGX system is designed with safety as a priority and includes an open software framework. With the scalable DRIVE Orin product family, developers can build once and then scale and leverage their development investment across entire fleets—from L2+ systems to L5 full autonomous driving systems.

![Image](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139225878/02_33ab27452587e0ca40e5e41396c1001e.png)

As an elite partner of NVIDIA, leveraging software driver technologies on NVIDIA DRIVE AGX Orin, we have deeply integrated GMSL2 cameras with the DRIVE AGX Orin platform, enabling up to 16 GMSL2 cameras to be simultaneously processed using DRIVE AGX Orin’s hardware acceleration.

The GMSL2 camera module development kit based on NVIDIA DRIVE AGX Orin not only provides autonomous vehicle developers with a rich and mature selection of GMSL2 camera products but also offers camera drivers to support test environment setup. Additionally, a professional ISP team is available to provide ISP tuning services for the NVIDIA platform, enabling developers to use the development and evaluation kit more easily and efficiently.

![Image](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139225878/03_60f2d5bc1bae1975ec6b4e386388f511.jpeg)

Using this developer kit, developers can efficiently build a GMSL2 camera development platform and leverage the provided hardware, software, and sample applications to obtain abundant high-quality reference data and foundational example applications during evaluation and development—reducing development effort and risk, improving efficiency, and accelerating time-to-market.

Xinmai's successful launch of GMSL2 cameras compatible with the NVIDIA DRIVE AGX Orin platform delivers a high-quality, fast, and cost-effective imaging solution to customers in the intelligent driving industry, while also accelerating the real-world deployment of autonomous driving technologies. In the future, we will continue to uphold the principle of "technological leadership and efficient innovation," closely follow the development trends of the intelligent driving industry, persist in independent innovation, and contribute to the advancement of intelligent vehicles.
