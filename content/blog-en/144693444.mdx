2-Channel Voltage 80M Sampling (16-bit) PCIe Data Acquisition Card

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144693444/01_6b2c614f1a324655bc673596e0ecbd9d.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144693444/02_78161491f9e54f47a829195463e87d5b.png)

Equipped with an XC7A100T-2FGG484I FPGA, this is a high-speed PCIe acquisition card specifically designed for industrial PCs. It boasts a maximum sampling rate of 80M. As an add-on card, it can be inserted into a PCIe slot of an industrial PC, transmitting acquired data to the CPU via the PCIe interface. Application layer software can be developed directly on the CPU. It is primarily intended for applications that require integration with an industrial PC or a standard computer host, where high sampling resolution is not strictly required and cost is a relatively sensitive factor.

Each acquisition card integrates 16 channels. Subsequent channel expansion depends on the number of available PCIe slots in the industrial PC or standard computer host. Total channels = Number of PCIe slots * 16.
