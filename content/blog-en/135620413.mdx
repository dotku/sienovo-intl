Non-enclosed, 4-Channel, 51.2 kS/s/ch, ±5 V, C Series Sound and Vibration Input Module
The sbRIO‑9234 can measure signals from Integrated Electronic Piezoelectric (IEPE) and non-IEPE sensors, such as accelerometers, tachometers, and proximity probes. The sbRIO‑9234 is also compatible with smart TEDS sensors.\\n\\nThe sbRIO‑9234 offers a wide dynamic range and integrates software-selectable AC/DC coupling and IEPE signal conditioning. Multiple input channels can simultaneously measure signals. Each channel also features built-in anti-aliasing filters that automatically adjust to the sampling rate. When used with NI software, the module provides processing functions for condition monitoring, such as frequency analysis and order tracking. This non-enclosed module is designed for OEM applications.
