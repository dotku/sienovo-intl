## XM-FT2000 Phytium Motherboard

The XM-FT2000 motherboard is a CPCI 6U standard board, offering interfaces such as DVI, VGA, USB, network, serial port, and SATA.

*   Processor: Phytium FT2000/4 quad-core domestic processor, clock speed ≥2.2GHz
*   Chipset: Zhaoxin ZX-200 chipset
*   Memory: Dual-channel DDR4; DDR4 memory, 16GB capacity
*   Storage: Onboard mSATA MLC SSD, standard capacity 512GB (optional 128GB, 256GB, 1TB)
*   Interfaces: Supports USB, PCI, PCIe, SATA, Ethernet interfaces, etc.
*   Dimensions: 233.35mm (L) × 160mm (H), board thickness 2mm
*   Operating Environment: -40℃~+65℃ (Industrial grade), -20℃~+55℃ (Commercial grade)
*   Firmware: UEFI firmware
*   Operating System: Adapted for Galaxy Kylin Operating System (Desktop Edition) 4.0.2

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138307453/01_3298c35318fc8f284ba373d31e72e7aa.png)

The motherboard complies with the CPCI specification and adopts a 6U form factor. It integrates an FT2000/4 quad-core processor, ZX-200 chipset, MxM interface discrete graphics card, and WX1860AL Gigabit network card on board. It can provide various interfaces such as network, serial port, USB, VGA, and DVI, featuring high display performance and multiple display interfaces.
