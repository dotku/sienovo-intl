### 2 QSFP, 8 AI, 8 AO, 16 Low-Latency AO, 64 DIO, KU060 FPGA PXI FlexRIO Multifunction I/O Module

The PXIe-7890 enables you to address signal-level inverter Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL) applications. This module combines Analog Input (AI), low-latency Analog Output (AO), and Digital I/O (DIO) with a Kintex Ultrascale FPGA and high-speed serial ports to support multiple traction motor models. The PXIe-7890 can be used for the verification and validation of Electronic Control Unit (ECU) performance to replicate real-world simulations and achieve comprehensive test coverage.
