### PXIe‑6614, 5 V, 8-Channel, Precision Reference PXI Counter/Timer Module

The PXIe‑6614 is a high-precision counter/timer module that can perform encoder position measurements, event counting, period measurements, pulse width measurements, pulse generation, pulse train generation, and frequency measurements. The module features 8 counter/timers, digital debouncing filters, and TTL/CMOS-compatible digital I/O. The PXIe‑6614 offers the robust features required by industry, making it suitable for numerous applications such as automotive/aerospace, industrial/motion control, and manufacturing test. The module's built-in oven-controlled crystal oscillator (OCXO) helps the device perform very accurate and precise measurements over long periods.
