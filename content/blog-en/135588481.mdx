PXIe, 16-channel AI (16-bit, 3.5 MS/s/ch), 4-channel AO, 48-channel DIO, PXI Multifunction I/O Module
The PXIe‑6378 is a synchronously sampled multifunction DAQ device. This module provides analog I/O, digital I/O, four 32-bit counters, and analog and digital triggering. Onboard NI‑STC3 timing and synchronization technology provides advanced timing capabilities, including independent analog and digital timing engines and retriggerable measurement tasks. The PXIe‑6378 is suitable for numerous applications, such as IF digitization; transient recording; ISDN, ADSL, and POTS manufacturing tests in the telecommunications industry; ultrasonic and sonar testing; and high-energy physics. The included NI‑DAQmx driver and configuration utility simplify configuration and measurements.
