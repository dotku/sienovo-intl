### What is CompactDAQ?

CompactDAQ systems can acquire and deliver validated data to meet your testing needs, regardless of distance or environment. These customizable, portable solutions consist of various data acquisition modules that enable synchronized measurements over a network. They help you digitize data and can be deployed closer to sensors, minimizing noise and simplifying field wiring.

CompactDAQ offers a cost-effective benchtop measurement approach. You can combine sensor-specific conditioned I/O modules with software optimized for DAQ applications.

**Ideal for:**

*   High-channel-count distributed DAQ applications 
    
*   Benchtop testing and measurement 
    
*   Mixed-sensor measurements

### Chassis

CompactDAQ chassis are developed for precise conditioned measurements, allowing you to perform various mixed-measurement data acquisition in a synchronized I/O system. These chassis offer connectivity options such as USB, Ethernet, or WiFi, and feature multiple slots to provide ample I/O for various applications. Combined with the right software, CompactDAQ systems help you configure how to acquire, analyze, display, and manage your measurement data.

### Acquire Mixed-Measurement Data

CompactDAQ chassis support over 60 measurement modules. Once inserted into the chassis, these modules can directly connect to sensors or signals and acquire synchronized measurement results. CompactDAQ chassis feature multiple timing engines, allowing separate hardware-timed I/O tasks to run at different rates within the same system.

### Deploy in Harsh Environments

With their rugged form factor, wider operating temperature range of -40°C to 70°C, 50g shock resistance, and 5g vibration resistance, CompactDAQ chassis can be deployed close to the device under test, shortening sensor cable lengths and reducing noise interference.

### Synchronized Distributed Systems

With chassis based on Time-Sensitive Networking (TSN) technology, you can achieve accurate long-distance synchronization and minimize wiring costs, making it easier to implement time-synchronized distributed measurements over Ethernet. You can also use integrated network switches to easily expand your system via simple daisy-chain connections.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/130503404/07_5f31ac1a1b5d91bf82af93e361b31acb.png)

### Controllers

CompactDAQ controllers are rugged, reliable, high-performance integrated controllers with industry-standard certifications. They can record data to onboard or removable SD storage while performing waveform acquisition and online software analysis. Controllers also offer a wide range of standard connectivity and expansion options, such as USB, Ethernet, CAN/LIN, and RS232 serial.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/130503404/08_7809328ca714079c666ca2b51b1234bb.png)

#### Data Acquisition Modules

The platform offers over 60 C Series I/O modules, supporting almost any type of sensor, allowing you to quickly customize your hardware for a perfect balance of size, cost, and performance. C Series modules are high-quality input and output modules that provide signal conditioning and analog-to-digital conversion for your CompactDAQ system. These hot-swappable modules can be directly inserted into the chassis, enabling you to easily customize a system tailored to your application's specific needs.
