### PXIe , 5 V, 8-Channel PXI Counter/Timer Module

The PXIe‑6612 can perform encoder position measurement, event counting, period measurement, pulse width measurement, pulse generation, pulse train generation, and frequency measurement. This module features 8 counter/timers, digital debouncing filters, and TTL/CMOS-compatible digital I/O. The PXIe‑6612 can simultaneously execute three high-speed DMA transfer tasks.

Shenzhen Xinmai provides NI locally produced cards and customization.
