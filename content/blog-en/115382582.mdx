**1 Overview of the Development Kit**

*   Based on the TI AM5728 floating-point dual DSP C66x + dual ARM Cortex-A15 industrial control and high-performance audio/video processor;
*   Heterogeneous multi-core CPU integrating dual-core Cortex-A15, dual-core C66x floating-point DSP, dual-core PRU-ICSS, dual-core Cortex-M4 IPU, and dual-core GPU, supporting multi-core development with OpenCL, OpenMP, and SysLink IPC;
*   Powerful video encoding and decoding capabilities, supporting 2K@30fps, 1×1080P@60fps, 2×720P@60fps, or 4×720P@30fps hardware video encoding/decoding, and H.265 software decoding;
*   Supports up to 1×1080P@60fps full HD video input, and 1×LCD + 1×HDMI 1.4a output;
*   Dual-core PRU-ICSS industrial real-time control subsystem supporting industrial protocols such as EtherCAT, EtherNet/IP, and PROFIBUS;
*   High-performance GPU with dual-core SGX544 3D accelerator and GC320 2D graphics engine, supporting OpenGL ES 2.0;
*   Rich peripheral interfaces, including dual Gigabit Ethernet, PCIe, GPMC, USB 2.0, UART, SPI, QSPI, SATA 2.0, I2C, DCAN, and other industrial control buses and interfaces, with support for high-speed USB 3.0;
*   Development board exposes V-PORT video interface for flexible connection to video input modules;
*   Ultra-compact size: only 86.5mm × 60.5mm;
*   Industrial-grade precision B2B connector with 0.5mm pitch, stable, easy to plug/unplug, reverse-insertion protection; high-speed connectors used for critical high-data-rate interfaces to ensure signal integrity.

The XM5728-IDK-V3 is a development board designed by Shenzhen Xinmingxin based on the TI AM5728 (dual floating-point DSP C66x + dual ARM Cortex-A15) XM5728-SOM system-on-module. It provides users with a testing platform for the XM5728-SOM, enabling rapid evaluation of the core module’s overall performance.

The XM5728-IDK-V3 baseboard uses a 4-layer PCB design with lead-free immersion gold finish. It not only provides customers with comprehensive AM5728 getting-started tutorials but also assists in baseboard development, offering long-term and full technical support to help customers accelerate secondary development and bring products to market quickly. A wide range of demo programs are provided, along with tutorials for DSP+ARM multi-core communication development, comprehensive technical support, and assistance in baseboard design, debugging, and DSP+ARM software development.
