(I) August Manifesto

———————

1. I will focus my energy on practical programming.
2. I will fully immerse myself in programming. Even simple execution or transcription, I am willing to do.
3. Leveraging my strengths in critical thinking, enjoyment of thought, comparison, and summarization, I will mentally run through program debugging, code execution, and ideas. (Non-linear execution, partial execution)
4. Copy, modify, innovate.
5. Draw flowcharts and write pseudocode in my mind (in various forms, the program will always become clear. Sometimes the program is code, sometimes a diagram, sometimes a mathematical expression). Another benefit: logical relationships become clearer, which also facilitates writing documentation and comments.
