This article discusses the AM5728 + Euresys industrial camera image processing case study.

#### I. Core Board Introduction

The XM-AM5728-KIT consists of the XM-SOM5728-CORE core board and the XM5728-IDK-V3 industrial baseboard.

The XM-SOM5728 core board integrates power management, DDR3, NAND FLASH, QSPI FLASH, EEPROM, and an SD card slot. It also extends the AM5728's HDMI, PCIe, SATA, USB, Ethernet, video, audio, industrial network, UART, SPI, I2C, CAN, and GPIO interfaces via connectors. Users can choose a standard Sienovo baseboard or design their own baseboard according to their needs to implement their specific functions.
