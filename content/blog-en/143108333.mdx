1.  1.  1.  **Hard Hat and Reflective Vest Detection Algorithm**
            1.  **Function Description**

Hard hat and reflective vest detection involves pre-setting a monitoring area in a surveillance scene. If personnel within this area are not wearing a hard hat or reflective vest, an alarm is triggered promptly. The detected target's resolution in a 1080p image should be no less than 30*30 pixels.

1.  1.  1.  1.  **Recommended Scenarios**

Scenario Requirements: Suitable for indoor and outdoor use, avoiding direct sunlight or backlight conditions; at night, only full-color images are supported. If the camera does not support full-color mode, please use white light for illumination.

Target Distance Requirements: Events beyond 3 meters can be detected from a standard 3-meter pole height.

Target Pixel Requirements: The detected target's resolution in a 1080P image should be greater than or equal to 30*30 pixels.

Recall Rate: 95%

1.  1.  1.  **Electric Vehicle Detection Algorithm**
            1.  **Function Description**

The electric vehicle detection algorithm, based on AI visual analysis technology, automatically identifies whether an electric vehicle enters the video image. If an electric vehicle is detected, an alarm is triggered on screen.

1.  1.  1.  1.  **Recommended Scenarios**

Scenario Requirements: Suitable for indoor and outdoor use, avoiding direct sunlight or backlight conditions; at night, only full-color images are supported. If the camera does not support full-color mode, please use white light for illumination.

Target Distance Requirements: Events beyond 3 meters can be detected from a standard 3-meter pole height.

Target Pixel Requirements: The detected target's resolution in a 1080P image should be greater than or equal to 30*30 pixels, with electric vehicle occlusion not exceeding 30%.

Supports detection of two-wheeled, three-wheeled, four-wheeled, and other electric vehicles.

Recall Rate: 90%
