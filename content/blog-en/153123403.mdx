**Solution Introduction**

The Robotics - Multi-Channel Vision Edge Computing Solution leverages the "Lijian" series cameras from Sienovo, with features such as high resolution, low latency, multiple interfaces, and wide dynamic range. This enables adaptation to various lighting conditions and scenarios, providing robots with multi-dimensional visual information. Based on a high-performance edge computing platform, it achieves real-time processing and analysis of multi-channel visual data, equipping robots with powerful visual perception and real-time decision-making capabilities. It can be widely applied in high-precision real-time perception fields such as intelligent monitoring, industrial automation, and logistics warehousing, providing robots with integrated "perception-decision-control" edge intelligence support.![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb8.r2.dev/blog/153123403/01_4d84fce512e84d9ebfe79f376e865d0e.png)
