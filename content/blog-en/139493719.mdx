Under the overall framework of a multi-layer closed-loop management system in the oil and gas industry, closely integrating with the development ideas and technological trends of industrial IoT platforms, and focusing on the actual needs of the oil and gas production business, a cloud-deployed big data platform software for the industrial sector has been developed. The platform is entirely based on a B/S (Browser/Server) architecture design, utilizing technologies such as IoT and cloud computing to provide one-stop services including device access, control system operation monitoring, and equipment asset management. It achieves unified data collection, storage, computation, display, and sharing, and builds systems for equipment monitoring, equipment management, and equipment operation and maintenance, centered around equipment operation and management, thereby supporting the construction and rapid deployment of IoT platform applications.

1.  1.  Product Overview

The XM-AIBOX-16 Smart Edge Analysis All-in-One Machine is a high-performance, low-power edge computing product. Equipped with the domestic TPU chip BM1684, it delivers up to 17.6 TOPS of INT8 computing power, capable of processing 16 channels of high-definition video simultaneously, and supporting hardware decoding of 32 channels of 1080P HD video and 2 channels of encoding.

This product highly integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms based on computer vision and deep learning networks, as well as a comprehensive intelligent video management platform. The AI intelligent algorithms cover various scenarios such as parks, communities, construction sites, and campuses, and can be combined on demand and configured per scenario. The comprehensive intelligent video management platform supports front-end device management, real-time video preview, alarm push, evidence capture, online algorithm loading and optimization, and large-screen display of data situation analysis. The device is easy to operate and plug-and-play, and also features rich northbound API interfaces to empower upper-layer business application platforms.
