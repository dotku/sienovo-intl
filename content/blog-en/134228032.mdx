## AI Vision Scale
The single-screen AI scale integrates an independent NPU, offering up to 0.8 TOPS of computing power, which significantly boosts AI processing efficiency. This enables fast and accurate identification of fresh produce, quick weighing, and label printing, thereby reducing operating costs for fresh produce stores. It also alleviates congestion from weighing queues during peak hours, improves weighing efficiency, and upgrades the overall shopping experience.
