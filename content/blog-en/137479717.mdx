**Application Scenarios: Communities, Campuses, Hotels, Shopping Malls, Restaurants, Hospitals, Ports, and Many Other Fields**

**I. Edge AI Box Product Introduction**

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/137479717/01_31b06eb50e1bd55fd4346fedd57cbcf6.png)

1. Based on a rich algorithm repository, algorithms can be flexibly configured for different scenarios and remotely updated and iterated in real-time.

2. Supports custom video channel algorithm execution plans.

3. Supports searching for alert event videos based on event name, time, and other criteria.

4. Supports unified storage and management of event-driven video data, saving space.

5. Supports intelligent video storage, intelligent processing, platform management, intelligent retrieval, alert management, and other functions.

6. Supports connecting to third-party cameras via ONVIF and RTSP protocols, and supports HDMI video output, etc.

**II. Features**

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/137479717/02_72458286ff140b8c07c255a345b08c1b.png)

1. **Integrated Centralized Management of People, Vehicles, Objects, and Environment** — Provides integrated supervision for people, vehicles, objects, and the environment within designated areas, enabling real-time reporting and viewing of various abnormal information to assist in rapid decision-making.

2. **Supports Integration with Various Platforms and Devices** — The data information management platform can be customized and developed for different scenarios, integrating with various devices and platforms.

3. **Direct Connection to Front-end Cameras for Easy Deployment** — Easily connects to various front-end cameras via the Edge AI Box, enabling intelligent storage, processing, alert linkage, and other functions.

4. **Supports Autonomous Algorithm Training for More Scenarios** — AI vision algorithm automated training provides a complete set of tools for autonomous algorithm optimization (including collection, labeling, training, deployment, etc.) to adapt to the algorithm needs of specific scenarios.

5. **Hundreds of Vision Algorithms, Flexible Calling** — A rich set of basic algorithms perfectly matches the needs of multi-scenario applications, such as **restaurants** (detection of chef uniforms/hats, small animals, area hygiene, etc.), **area security** (identification of smoke/fire, boundary intrusion, fighting, high-altitude littering, etc.), **area management** (identification of license plates/vehicle types, aisle obstruction, illegal parking, etc.), and **business analytics** (pedestrian flow statistics, trajectory analysis, VIP recognition, etc.).
