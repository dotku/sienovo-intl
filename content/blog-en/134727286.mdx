Due to the unique nature of the gas station industry, oil depots and stations typically store large quantities of refined fuel to meet sales demands, making personnel and property safety a top priority. Xinxing Smart Gas Station Solution focuses on station safety and digitalization, delivering an all-in-one intelligent safety solution for gas stations. The solution covers safety management in service areas and fuel unloading zones. By leveraging intelligent recognition and analysis technologies such as AI edge computing boxes and cameras, it enables real-time video content analysis to detect anomalies and predict risks. This provides the most efficient and direct method to enhance gas station safety. The system supports detection of fire and smoke in operational zones, mobile phone usage, unauthorized personnel intrusion, staff behavior compliance, on-duty/off-duty status, smoking, license plate recognition, and more—enabling proactive responses to potential threats. Its lightweight deployment design also facilitates fast and easy project implementation.
