With the rapid development of technology, AI is gradually permeating various fields, and traffic monitoring is no exception. In recent years, AI vision analysis system solutions have been increasingly applied in traffic monitoring, providing intelligent support for urban traffic management.

For intelligent traffic monitoring, AI system solutions primarily include the following core components:

I. Real-time Monitoring and Analysis

The AI vision analysis system adopts advanced image recognition and processing technology to monitor urban traffic conditions in real-time. The system captures information such as traffic flow, speed, and vehicle types in real-time through cameras installed at intersections and road segments, providing data support for traffic management.

II. Vehicle Information Recognition

The system utilizes deep learning algorithms to extract key vehicle information such as license plates, vehicle types, colors, and speeds, providing detailed data for traffic monitoring.

The following are several key advantages of the AI vision analysis system solution:

1. Efficiency and Accuracy: The AI vision analysis system features high efficiency and and accuracy, capable of processing large amounts of data in a short time, providing real-time data support for traffic monitoring.

2. Data Visualization: The system uses data visualization technology to display traffic monitoring data in forms such as charts and maps, allowing management personnel to quickly understand traffic conditions.

3. Flexibility: The AI vision analysis system can be flexibly adjusted according to actual needs, adapting to data analysis in different scenarios and time periods.

4. Data Security: The system protects data security through encryption algorithms, ensuring the authenticity and integrity of traffic monitoring data.

In summary, intelligent traffic monitoring, powered by AI vision analysis system solutions, provides intelligent support for urban traffic management, improving traffic monitoring efficiency and reducing labor costs. Through real-time monitoring and analysis, it provides scientific decision-making basis for traffic management departments, assisting in the optimization and improvement of urban traffic in China. In the future, AI vision analysis technology will play an increasingly important role in the field of traffic monitoring.

The in-vehicle MDVR (main unit) is a cost-effective and highly scalable device developed specifically for multi-channel video surveillance and remote monitoring needs in vehicles such as buses and trains. It is developed using a high-speed processor and an embedded Linux platform, combining the most advanced H.265 video compression/decompression technology in the IT field. Utilizing ****dual hard drives**** + SD card (mirror recording) as storage media, the MDVR can achieve 8 channels of analog HD video/audio input + 4 channels of digital HD IPC input for video/audio recording and vehicle driving information recording. It features ****dual 4G/5G modules**** and ****dual supercapacitors****. The MDVR product has a concise appearance, strong anti-vibration capabilities, flexible and convenient installation, powerful functions, and high reliability.

Features

*   Built-in HiSilicon high-performance image processing chip, H.265 encoding, high compression ratio, clear image;
*   Supports 8 channels of AHD megapixel HD/analog SD video/audio input + 4 channels of IPC HD input, 1 channel of video/audio synchronous output, 1 channel of VGA output, 1 channel of HDMI output;
*   Supports 12 channels of 1080P HD real-time local recording;
*   Professional in-vehicle power supply design with 9-36V DC wide voltage input;
*   Multiple protection circuits for undervoltage, short circuit, reverse connection, etc., suitable for various vehicle types;
*   Supports intelligent power management recognition, automatic shutdown at low power, and low power consumption when ignition is off;
*   Adopts a special file management mechanism to encrypt data, effectively protecting data security;
*   Proprietary hard drive bad sector detection technology, ensuring both recording continuity and extending hard drive life;
*   Built-in supercapacitor to prevent data loss and hard drive damage from abnormal power failures;
*   Supports dual hard drive + SD card storage, supports 4T*2 + 512G;
*   Supports GPS/BD positioning, high sensitivity, fast positioning;
*   Supports WIFI wireless download, supports 802.11b/g/n, frequency 5.8GHz/2.4GHz;
*   Built-in 3G/4G/5G module, supports LTE/HSUPA/HSDPA/WCDMA/EVDO;
*   Built-in AI: ADAS+DMS (or BSD)
