1.30 GHz dual-core CPU, 2 GB DRAM, 4 GB storage capacity, -20 °C to 55 °C, Kintex-7 70T FPGA, 4-slot CompactRIO controller
The cRIO-9040 is a rugged, customizable, high-performance embedded controller powered by an Intel Atom dual-core processor, offering NI-DAQmx support and an SD card slot for data logging, embedded monitoring, and control. The controller includes a Kintex-7 70T FPGA and LabVIEW FPGA Module support, enabling advanced control and coprocessing applications. The controller utilizes Time-Sensitive Networking (TSN) to provide precise and synchronized timing and deterministic communication, making it ideal for highly distributed measurements. This controller offers multiple connectivity ports, including Gigabit Ethernet, USB 3.1, USB 2.0, RS232, and RS485 ports. You can use the USB 3.1 port to add a local human-machine interface (HMI) as well as for programming, deployment, and debugging software, thereby simplifying application development.
