****1.1**** ****Product Introduction****

Driven by the rapid intelligent development needs of industries such as intelligent transportation and industrial internet, the XM-RK3588 edge processor was developed with OpenHarmony as its framework, embedding HarmonyOS, to create a highly intelligent, highly reliable, highly secure, and independently controllable edge processor.

图 1-1 边缘处理器
Figure 1-1 Edge Processor

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138169507/01_a0e3746b2076a0aee3841b4dad102b2b.png)

HarmonyOS enhances IoT interconnection and interaction capabilities, giving the edge processor the following advantages:

l Powerful support for connecting edge devices in complex scenarios, rapid deployment capability, and convenient replication capability.

l Localized data processing and storage, reducing network bandwidth pressure and customer operating costs.

l End-to-end data security management, with domestic OS + domestic chip, ensuring user data security.

l Rapid AI empowerment for existing devices, improving the processing capability for massive data.

****1.2**** ****Product Features****

The edge processor has the following features:

l High-reliability design, ensuring stable device operation

\-        Embedded watchdog technology, fault self-recovery, ensuring stable device operation.

\-        Multi-antenna design, enhancing wireless signals, ensuring stable network operation in various customer environments.

l Full-featured, quickly experiencing the convenience and speed of wireless communication

\-        Supports Bluetooth, DI, DO, RS485, RS232, Ethernet, HDMI, audio, and other input/output.

\-        Equipped with an 8-core high-performance processor, providing high-speed processing and real-time response edge computing capabilities.

\-        Supports remote full-system upgrades, saving human and material resources.
