### 102.4 kS/s, 100 dB, 0.8 Hz AC/DC Coupling, 5-Input Sound and Vibration Device

The USB-4432 is designed for sound and vibration applications. The input channels integrate Integrated Electronic Piezoelectric (IEPE) signal conditioning for accelerometers and microphones, and feature built-in anti-aliasing filters that automatically adjust to the sampling rate. The fifth input channel does not provide IEPE support.

102.4 kS/s, 100 dB, 0.8 Hz AC/DC coupling, 4-input/single-output sound and vibration device - The USB-4431 is designed for sound and vibration applications. The input channels integrate Integrated Electronic Piezoelectric (IEPE) signal conditioning for accelerometers and microphones. The four input channels of the USB-4431 can simultaneously digitize input signals. The analog output (AO) channel is ideal for excitation-response tests and can be synchronized with the AI channels.
