Fully Automatic Chemiluminescence Immunoassay Analyzer

The fully automatic chemiluminescence immunoassay analyzer can analyze whole blood, serum, and plasma samples from subjects, enabling the formulation of scientific treatment or management plans.

The IMX8P features a quad-core processor based on the Cortex-A53 architecture, clocked at up to 1.8GHz, and equipped with 2GB of standard memory. Its robust video processing capabilities, coupled with a high-version operating system, provide stunning visual effects and a fluid operational experience, greatly enhancing user satisfaction.
