1.  1.  Product Overview

The XM-AIBOX-16 Smart Edge Analysis All-in-One Machine is a high-performance, low-power edge computing product. Equipped with the domestic TPU chip BM1684, its INT8 computing power reaches up to 17.6 TOPS. It can process 16 channels of high-definition video simultaneously and supports hardware decoding of 32 channels of 1080P HD video and 2 channels of encoding.

This product highly integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms based on computer vision and deep learning networks, as well as a video intelligent comprehensive management platform. The AI intelligent algorithms cover various algorithms for scenarios such as parks, communities, construction sites, and campuses, which can be combined on demand and configured by scenario. The video intelligent comprehensive management platform supports front-end device management, real-time video preview, alarm push, forensic snapshot, online algorithm loading and optimization, data trend analysis large screen display, etc. The device is easy to operate, plug-and-play, and also features rich northbound API interfaces, enabling upper-layer business application platforms.

**Ultra-High Performance Computing and Codec Capabilities**

*   Supports peak INT8 computing power of up to 17.6 TOPS or high-precision FP32 computing power of 2.2 TFLOPS;

*   Supports hardware decoding of up to 32 channels of H.264/H.265 1080P@25FPS video;
*   Supports hardware encoding of up to 2 channels of H.264/H.265 1080P@25FPS video.
