1.  ****Product Introduction****

*   Centec CTC7132, built-in ARM-Cortex A53, clocked at 1.2GHz;
*   Supports 24 Gigabit Ethernet ports, 24 10 Gigabit optical ports (850nm multimode), 1 Gigabit management Ethernet port, 1 management serial port;
*   Supports 1 Fast Ethernet health management port: used for reporting device port status, voltage, current, temperature, and other information;
*   Internal connectors feature locking mechanisms, providing excellent reinforcement characteristics, stability, and reliability, suitable for use in harsh environments;

*   100% domestic production and autonomous control;

*   Supports Layer 3 switching functions.
