The Series 2nd Generation GMSL Image Acquisition Card (hereinafter referred to as **2nd Gen Image Acquisition Card**) is an independently developed FPGA-based high-speed image product. Compared to the 1st Gen card, the 2nd Gen Image Acquisition Card, due to the adoption of PCIe Gen3 technology, has seen a multiple increase in both speed and bandwidth. This image acquisition card can connect to GMSL cameras, offering advantages such as high frame rate, high speed, and low latency,

widely applied in unmanned vehicles, autonomous driving, autonomous machines, data acquisition, and other scenarios.

Industrial PC platform. Plug in the [Tengyun] Series GMSL Image Acquisition Card, and the acquisition card can connect to all vehicle cameras. Each card can support

8 GMSL camera inputs, supporting up to 8-megapixel 30fps. Simultaneously, it can connect to GPS or PTP for time synchronization. Timestamped data can be transmitted via the PCIe Gen3 interface with low latency and lossless transfer to the industrial PC's GPU for processing.
