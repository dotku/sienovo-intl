## NI 9235: A Domestic Alternative for High-Speed Synchronous Strain Measurements

The NI 9235 is an 8-channel C Series strain/bridge input module that enables high-speed synchronous measurements of dynamic strain on all channels. This feature is particularly crucial for applications that require comparing multiple channels at a specific moment, such as impact testing. In this article, we will explore the key features and technical specifications of the NI 9235, as well as its potential applications and advantages.

## Key Features and Technical Specifications

The NI 9235 features built-in voltage excitation for quarter-bridge sensors, making it a convenient and efficient solution for strain measurement applications. Additionally, it provides 60 VDC isolation and 1,000 Vrms transient isolation, which enhances safety and provides high common-mode noise rejection. The module's technical specifications include:

### Bridge Configuration

* 1/4 Bridge

### Analog Input Channels

* Maximum Differential Analog Input Channels: 8

### Environmental Protection

* Conformal Coating: No
* Encapsulation: Yes

### Voltage Range and Sample Rate

* Analog Input Voltage Range: -29.4 mV/V to 29.4 mV/V
* Maximum Sample Rate: 10 kS/s/channel

### Connection and Platform Compatibility

* Front Connection Type: Spring Terminals
* Supported Hardware Platform: C Series
* Bridge Resistance: 120 Ohms

## Applications and Advantages

The NI 9235 is particularly suitable for applications that require high-speed synchronous measurements, such as:

* Impact testing: The NI 9235 enables simultaneous measurement of dynamic strain on multiple channels, allowing for accurate comparison and analysis of strain data.
* Structural testing: The module's high sample rate and synchronous measurement capability make it an ideal solution for testing the structural integrity of materials and components.
* Research and development: The NI 9235's high-speed measurement capability and built-in voltage excitation make it a valuable tool for researchers and developers working on strain measurement applications.

In conclusion, the NI 9235 is a powerful and versatile 8-channel C Series strain/bridge input module that offers high-speed synchronous measurements, built-in voltage excitation, and enhanced safety features. Its technical specifications and applications make it an attractive solution for a wide range of industries and research fields.
