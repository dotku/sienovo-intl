### 10 kS/s/ch, 120 Ω Quarter-Bridge Strain Gage, 8-Channel C Series Strain/Bridge Input Module

The NI‑9235 can simultaneously measure dynamic strain on all channels, enabling high-speed synchronous measurements. This feature is crucial for applications that require comparing multiple channels at a specific moment, such as impact testing.\\n\\nThe NI‑9235 features built-in voltage excitation for quarter-bridge sensors. It also features 60 VDC isolation and 1,000 Vrms transient isolation, providing high common-mode noise rejection and enhanced safety.

Product Details

Bridge Configuration: 1/4 Bridge

Maximum Differential Analog Input Channels: 8

Conformal Coating: No

Analog Input Voltage Range: -29.4 mV/V to 29.4 mV/V

Encapsulation: Yes

Maximum Sample Rate: 10 kS/s/channel

Front Connection Type: Spring Terminals

Supported Hardware Platform: C Series

Bridge Resistance: 120 Ohms
