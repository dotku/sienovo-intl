As digitalization and intelligent technologies become increasingly integrated with work and daily life, there is growing focus on human-centric services. By leveraging digital tools and technology to meet diverse needs, the transformation of commercial buildings into smart buildings is accelerating, gradually forming a comprehensive smart building product matrix.

**Solution Highlights**

**01/Digital Signage – Brand and Information Display**

Corporate culture promotion, announcements, and notices

Centrally managed content via remote control

Integrated visitor recognition with customized welcome messages

**02/Smart Access Control – Efficient and Accurate Entry Management**

Supports facial recognition and card-based access

Precise access permission management for employees, whitelist users, and visitors

Blacklist monitoring and real-time alerts

**03/Security Management – AI-Powered Visual Monitoring**

Real-time monitoring of indoor and surrounding building areas

Supports detection of personnel activity, fire safety, vehicle movement, etc.

Automatically identifies anomalies and triggers alarms

**04/Visitor Appointment – Business Visitor Reception**

Schedule visits in advance via mobile app with QR code access

On-site temporary registration and verification, paperless operation

Integrated with meeting systems to automatically reserve meeting rooms

**05/Elevator On-Demand Maintenance – Elevator Safety Management**

Elevator operation safety monitoring

One-touch emergency alarm with two-way voice intercom

**06/Smart Meeting Scheduling – Efficient Meeting Management**

Multi-channel booking via PC, mobile devices, and room signage

Customizable enterprise information display

Displays meeting room schedules and real-time availability status
