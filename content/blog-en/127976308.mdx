### Hardware & OS Testing Solutions

Solution Overview

Server Hardware, OS Compatibility, and Certification Testing

The core technology for all data center and cloud computing services is scalability, built upon the foundation of large-scale distributed server deployments. However, the current market features a vast array of server types and a multitude of corresponding operating systems. This places extremely high demands on the compatibility between various servers and operating systems. Ensuring good compatibility and long-term operational stability between underlying hardware products and their supporting operating systems, coupled with continuous investment in hardware compatibility testing and certification to enrich the support list for components and software, is essential to ensure that the data center or cloud computing services provided to customers gain an advantage in fierce market competition, enhancing user satisfaction and competitiveness.

Server Hardware and Chip Performance Testing

High-performance computing (HPC) technology enables faster time-to-market and lower development costs. It allows for rapid prototyping, enables digital simulation and modeling to reduce costs, and can be used for interaction, making the creation of tools and step-by-step tools more widespread and easier, contributing to social development and economic growth. The future of HPC is leaning towards ARM. Concurrently, the future of HPC will undoubtedly be dominated by CPU+GPU hybrid accelerated computing platforms with higher energy efficiency ratios, where energy consumption will be reduced to one-fifteenth to one-thirtieth of current levels.
