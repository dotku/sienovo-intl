For male intellectual workers, what are the best principles for rest? Passive rest often easily replaces active rest. Experts from Shanghai Wanzhong Andrology Hospital offer some advice to men who only focus on smoking and drinking after work to pass the time.

1. Approach life with a positive and optimistic attitude.
This greatly helps maintain the stability of the nervous system. For men living in an era of information overload, approaching life with a positive and optimistic attitude is undoubtedly very beneficial in overcoming frequent nervous tension. Engaging in sports activities, establishing a reasonable work-rest schedule, practicing personal hygiene, and regularly immersing oneself in nature to cultivate one's temperament — these activities indirectly reflect a person's level of optimism towards life. Optimism itself is a great form of rest.

2. Overcome inertia and rest actively.
Rest does not mean loose, uninteresting idleness. Rest should be vibrant and engaging. It's important to understand that men's rest should not just be simple relaxation; it should be a source of physical strength, joy, vitality, and harmony within the family and society. Resting by spending all day with cigarettes, alcohol, or gambling is not advisable.

3. Rest when it's time to rest.
Men often like to show off their capabilities, sometimes working relentlessly without regard for their health, either to demonstrate their abilities or out of a strong sense of responsibility. In reality, the human body has its own biological rhythms. Only by working and living according to one's own pace can one align with their biological clock and work more effectively. Every man who knows how to work also knows how to rest. They do not easily give up evenings, public holidays, or vacations, because they deeply understand how crucial these rest days are for their future work!

4. Vary your resting methods.
When planning your workday, consider how to alternate between sitting and standing; when arranging holiday activities, pay attention to alternating between outdoor and indoor activities; when planning a week of entertainment, think about the difference between cultural and sports activities, and so on. Regularly changing your resting methods makes life feel more engaging, and even with a heavy workload, all fatigue will dissipate after such a subtle combination.
