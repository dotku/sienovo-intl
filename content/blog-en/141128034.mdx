Fully Automatic Nucleic Acid Extractor

ARM: NXP IMX8/IMX6U, TI AM62X/AM5728, RK: RK3588/RK3568

FPGA: Xilinx A7/K7/SPARTAN6/ZYNQ/MPSOC, Domestic Fudan Microelectronics, Anlogic, Gowin Semiconductor, Unigroup

A fully automatic nucleic acid extractor is a high-precision, high-efficiency nucleic acid extraction device primarily used for extracting nucleic acids from samples. Its principle involves using an automated control system to perform nucleic acid extraction steps on samples through various reagent kits and software algorithms. It features simple operation, high speed, high efficiency, and high accuracy, and is widely used in biological laboratories, disease diagnosis, gene sequencing, and other fields.

The fully automatic nucleic acid extractor is a simple, fast, and cost-effective instrument that can automatically purify DNA or RNA from samples such as whole blood, viruses, tissues, plants, bacteria, and cultured cells. It is now widely used in nucleic acid detection.

Features:

1. Rich Interfaces

❶ Display Interface: Connects to screens of various specifications and sizes, providing a good human-machine interaction experience;

❷ USB Interface Expansion: Connects to various external devices such as USB drives, mice, WiFi modules, 4G modules, printers, etc.;

❸ Communication Interface: Supports 10/100/1000M Ethernet, 4G modules, and WiFi modules to connect to servers for data upload and storage;

❹ External Bus: CAN/RS485/RS232 for motor control, valve control, and AD sampling;

❺ External Storage: Connects to TF and SD cards for storing operational data and analysis results.

2. Long Lifecycle

Medical product certification and clinical testing take a long time. The Core-A9 core board has a lifecycle of over 10 years.
