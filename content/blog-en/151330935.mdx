Driven by the "Dual Carbon" strategy, the scale of distributed photovoltaic (PV) applications continues to expand. Efficient, stable, and intelligent operation and maintenance (O&M) management is crucial for ensuring both revenue and safety.

RK3568 Version:

**·****Wide Operating Temperature Range (-20°C ~ 70°C)****:** Easily handles harsh environments such as outdoor cabinets and rooftops, ensuring 24/7 uninterrupted data acquisition.

**·****4 Isolated Serial Ports**** (RS232/485 optional, with isolation)****:** Flexibly adapts to Modbus RTU/Modbus TCP protocols of various inverter brands, as well as serial communication for smart meters and temperature/humidity sensors.

**·****4 Isolated Digital Inputs (DI):** Accurately collects critical switch signals, such as combiner box circuit breaker status and access control switch signals.

**·****2 Isolated Digital Outputs (DO):** Enables remote control (e.g., starting/stopping ventilation equipment, emergency disconnection of faulty equipment).

**·****2 Gigabit Ethernet Ports:** Provides high-speed, redundant wired data backhaul channels. Optional 4G/5G modules perfectly address network coverage challenges at remote sites.

**Key Application Value:** The rich interface design easily connects to various inverter models and sensors, supports security camera integration, with 4 DI channels for real-time monitoring of combiner box status. Dual network ports ensure stable data transmission even in humid and salt spray environments. After deployment, the data acquisition completeness rate significantly improved, laying a solid foundation for subsequent analysis.

RK3588 Model:

**·6 TOPs Flagship AI Computing Power:** Enables localized processing of data streams from hundreds of devices and real-time execution of AI diagnostic algorithms (e.g., solar module hot spot detection).

**·6 Isolated Serial Ports:** Simultaneously connects to multiple inverter clusters, high-precision weather stations, and smart meter groups, compatible with protocols like Modbus/IEC 104.

**·16 Isolated DI/DO Channels:** Provides comprehensive monitoring of switch status chains in power distribution cabinets (DI high-level trigger) and remote control of ventilation/dehumidification equipment (DO high-level conduction).

**·2 Isolated CAN Channels:** Millisecond-level response to energy storage system PCS and tracker control commands.

**·8-Channel 1080P Video Input Capability (MIPI+USB expansion):** Real-time analysis of infrared video streams via NPU to accurately identify module defects and security risks.

**·Dual Gigabit Ethernet Ports + 9~36V Wide Voltage Input:** Ensures high-bandwidth data backhaul and adapts to voltage fluctuation scenarios in PV power stations.

**Key Application Value:** Integrates 3 centralized inverter clusters via 6 serial ports; 16 DI/DO channels provide full coverage for 32 switch states in the power distribution room; dual CAN bus achieves millisecond-level response for energy storage PCS commands. It can simultaneously connect to 8 security cameras and further integrate infrared cameras via 8 MIPI interfaces. Leveraging the 6 TOPs NPU, it performs real-time analysis of hot spot risks across 40,000 m² of module area (97.2% recognition accuracy), significantly reducing annual power generation loss.
