A safety solution based on visual AI intelligent algorithms can be designed for heavy-duty trucks such as large vehicles, commercial vehicles, mining construction machinery, port construction machinery, industrial park vehicles, dump trucks, mixers, and freight trucks. It achieves intelligent recognition and early warning through on-board cameras for panoramic blind spot detection, driver voice alerts, in-cabin displays, and speakers, by collecting image data. When AI vision cameras detect pedestrians, vehicles, etc., approaching within 3 to 5 meters of the vehicle, the in-cabin display immediately magnifies and shows the video image on the right side, helping the driver improve visual perception. When the distance of pedestrians or vehicles exceeds a certain threshold, the AI-enabled device immediately triggers audible and visual warning alerts.

1.  1.  Product Overview

The XM-AIBOX-16 intelligent edge analysis all-in-one machine is a high-performance, low-power edge computing product. Equipped with the domestic TPU chip BM1684, it boasts INT8 computing power of up to 17.6 TOPS, can process 16 channels of high-definition video simultaneously, and supports hardware decoding of 32 channels of 1080P HD video and 2 channels of encoding.

This product highly integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms based on computer vision and deep learning networks, as well as a comprehensive intelligent video management platform. The AI intelligent algorithms cover various algorithms for scenarios such as industrial parks, communities, construction sites, and campuses, and can be combined and configured as needed for specific scenarios. The comprehensive intelligent video management platform supports front-end device management, real-time video preview, alarm push, forensic snapshot, online algorithm loading and optimization, data situational analysis large screen display, and more. The device is easy to operate and plug-and-play, and it also features rich northbound API interfaces, enabling integration with upper-layer business application platforms.
