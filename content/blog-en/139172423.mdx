**Servers are the infrastructure for big data, artificial intelligence, blockchain, cloud computing, and the metaverse. China ships around 4 million servers annually, with projections exceeding 5 million by 2025 (according to the China Business Industry Research Institute). High-performance enterprise-grade interconnect chips control the nervous and circulatory systems of servers.**

**Market Pain Point: Currently, over 95% of domestic demand is monopolized by U.S. companies such as Broadcom.**

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/139172423/01_f80c1e2bad5d2d354426595c4108a03f.png)

**Interconnect chips = IO interconnect + network interconnect, representing the shortest board among the "four key components" of servers.**

System-level perspective from complete equipment development, IC technology expertise accumulated from DSP chip R&D, and management experience gained from large-scale engineering and team leadership  
**have laid a solid foundation for breaking through technological barriers and enabling product industrialization.**

**In data centers, specialized teams are already working to close domestic gaps in upstream CPUs and downstream end storage.**  
**Our focus lies in algorithms, networking controllers, IO expansion chips, and NIC (network interface controller) processing chips.**
