The Phytium multi-CPU, multi-serial industrial control host is a domestically developed, autonomous, and controllable solution based on the high-performance, eight-core Phytium D2000 processor platform. Equipped with domestic firmware and supporting Chinese operating systems such as UOS and Kylin, it meets the secure computing requirements of financial systems. It enables full domestication, autonomy, and control from hardware and operating system to applications, making it a flagship solution for China's financial information security control systems.
