### 4 AI Channels (16-bit, 3.5 MS/s/ch), 2 AO, 24 DIO, Multifunction I/O Device

The PCIe‑6374 is a synchronous sampling multifunction DAQ device. This module provides analog I/O, digital I/O, four 32‑bit counters, and analog and digital triggering. Onboard NI‑STC3 timing and synchronization technology delivers advanced timing capabilities, including independent analog and digital timing engines and retriggerable measurement tasks. The PCIe‑6374 is suitable for a wide range of applications such as IF digitization, transient recording, manufacturing test for ISDN, ADSL, and POTS in the telecommunications industry, ultrasound and sonar testing, and high-energy physics.
