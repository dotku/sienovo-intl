## Edge Computing Box

Rockchip RV1126 | 2.0TOPS INT8/INT16

● Integrated NPU with computing power up to 2.0TOPs@INT8/INT16.

● Supports H.264/H.265/MJPEG video encoding and decoding; supports multi-level video quality configuration and encoding complexity settings.

● Encoding and Decoding Performance

    3840 x 2160@30 fps + 1080p@30 fps encoding

    3840 x 2160@30 encoding + 3840 x 2160@30 fps decoding.

● Maximum decoding 4096x2304@30fps.                      

● Supports a series of deep learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, MXNet, PyTorch, and Caffe, meeting diverse developer needs.

● Rich peripheral interfaces: Ethernet, HDMI in/out, RS232, RS485, Line in/out, Wi-Fi, TF, etc.

The XM-RV1126 Edge Computing Box is a deep intelligent edge computing device designed by Xinmai Technology based on the RV1126. The core RV1126 adopts a highly integrated precision design architecture, based on a quad-core ARM Cortex-A7@1.5GHz & RISC-V architecture. The entire device features lightweight design, high performance, low power consumption, and convenient access, making it widely applicable in various fields such as facial recognition, smart security, and behavior analysis.
