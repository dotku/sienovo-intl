• Dual high-speed, high-precision data acquisition channels
• Supports precise internal and external triggered sampling modes
• Extensive bus and control interfaces
• Strong anti-interference capability

The high-speed data acquisition box is a dual-channel, high-speed, high-precision data acquisition device featuring a 500 MSPS sampling rate and 14-bit resolution. It has an analog input bandwidth of 200 MHz, utilizes a Xilinx high-performance MPSoC processor, and includes built-in 2GB DDR4 and 16GB eMMC storage. It supports trigger input/output functions. Additionally, it offers rich communication interfaces such as Gigabit Ethernet, CAN, RS485, and RS232.
