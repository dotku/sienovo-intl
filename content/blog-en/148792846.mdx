The Distribution Visualization Gateway is an edge intelligent gateway application developed based on NB2, suitable for various industrial-grade edge intelligent scenarios such as power grids.

The Transmission and Distribution Visualization Gateway primarily focuses on monitoring complex areas of transmission networks. It can locally perceive and identify transmission line environments, as well as special events such as wildfires, ice accretion, foreign objects, and tower cranes, and interact with the master station. The gateway is developed based on an NB2 stamp-hole core board, which, combined with an expansion backplane, forms a power edge intelligent platform. This platform can support requirements such as sensor data aggregation, processing, reporting, and interaction, particularly featuring functions like image acquisition, AI edge computing, and real-time backhaul. The gateway also meets IP67 protection standards and can adapt to harsh industrial-grade outdoor application environments.
