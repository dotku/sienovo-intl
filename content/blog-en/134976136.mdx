**6U 4-slot CPCI Portable System**

The HW-3646 is a portable system that includes an embedded CPCI controller, a CPCI backplane, and a ruggedized chassis. This CPCI portable system features a professional industrial design and an all aluminum-magnesium alloy compact structure. It integrates a 15.6'' HD display, an industrial resistive touchscreen or multi-touch capacitive touchscreen, an industrial touchpad, a waterproof silicone keyboard, and a measurement and control power supply. It boasts high integration, ruggedness, and portability, making it suitable for various harsh indoor and outdoor environments.

The HW-3646 features a built-in 6U 4-slot CPCI backplane, compliant with CPCI bus standard specifications. It has one 6U CPCI system slot and three 6U CPCI expansion slots, and can integrate functional modules such as data acquisition, avionics bus, and communication interfaces.

The HW-3646 fully leverages the CPCI bus's characteristics of stability, reliability, good compatibility, robust structure, and high performance. Depending on the project application, this measurement and control platform can integrate various CPCI modules. It enables the testing and measurement of signals such as signal simulation, prototype verification, voltage and current, temperature and frequency, stress and strain, vibration and shock, and various avionics bus interfaces. Users can quickly build various measurement, testing, and control systems on this portable measurement and control platform. It is suitable for field combat applications in military defense, aerospace, weaponry electronics, and shipborne systems, as well as scientific research and experimental scenarios.
