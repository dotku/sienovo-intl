30-Channel (24-bit) High-Precision Data Acquisition Board

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677437/01_d74199c5c6ce42abbba002764dcea496.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677437/02_4b559cbebb32486ea80225a2547722ad.png)

Utilizing the ZYNQ7020CLG400 SOC as the primary controller, a total of 30 channels are designed (24 fast channels, 6 slow channels). This board is primarily intended for applications with stringent requirements for compactness, channel count, high precision, and low noise.

It features a modular board design, with the boards categorized into two types: current acquisition boards and vibration acquisition boards. The current acquisition board is designed to collect 4-20mA current signals, while the vibration acquisition board collects standard 2-wire IEPE signals.
