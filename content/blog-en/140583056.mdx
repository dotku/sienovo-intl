Face-scan entry for the entire subway network | Dedicated computers for subway smart ticketing systems available

Recently, Chengdu Metro officially launched its "Smart Ticketing" system across its entire network of subway stations. The full-channel contactless "face-scan entry while wearing a mask" method has optimized passenger travel efficiency and reduced the risk of cross-infection during the pandemic, thus receiving widespread praise!

**01、Innovative Development of Subway Ticketing Systems**

Ticketing management systems are a crucial component of urban rail transit construction. They leverage electronic technology, automatic control technology, wireless transmission technology, computer network technology, and more, to achieve seamless integration between terminal devices such as automatic ticket vending machines (TVMs) and smart gates, and electronic ticketing and settlement systems. This enables intelligent functions such as computer network-based ticket sales, ticket inspection management, and real-time data statistical analysis. With the deep integration and application of advanced technologies like the internet and artificial intelligence in urban rail transit projects, ticketing management systems have also begun their intelligent transformation and upgrade. Chengdu Metro's recently launched "Smart Ticketing" system is a prime example of this.

It is understood that Chengdu Metro's "Smart Ticketing" system, launched this time, introduces technologies such as facial recognition, IoT, and embedded computing. It not only supports recognition while wearing a mask but also, when passengers enter or exit the gates, it can determine the front-to-back order of passengers based on images captured from the video stream, thereby preventing "tailgating" or misuse of the gates by nearby individuals. Furthermore, the electronic ticketing system is interconnected with automatic ticket vending machines, smart gates, and other devices. When passengers use the "face-scan" function, any account anomalies or verification failures can be monitored and alerted in real-time, ensuring the timeliness, accuracy, and security of face-scan passengers.

We believe that the implementation of Chengdu Metro's "Smart Ticketing" system is one of the trending applications in contemporary urban rail transit digitalization. The stable operation and multi-functional expansion of the "Smart Ticketing" system are inseparable from the hardware platform support of high-quality, high-performance embedded computers.
