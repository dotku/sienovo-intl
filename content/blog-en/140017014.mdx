**Solution Architecture**

The overall technical architecture consists of an AI Vision Box, network cameras, a management backend, a mobile app, an NVR, and other components, providing a complete closed-loop solution from front-end intelligent monitoring to back-end data processing.

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/140017014/01_937b0fec734a44996bd969f6bd281a87.png)

**Solution Value**

**01/Reduced Labor Costs**

① AI algorithms perform real-time intelligent detection of surveillance content with high accuracy, effectively reducing errors and omissions, eliminating the need for multiple manual guards.

② Automatic alarms are triggered upon detection of abnormal states, with real-time upload of alarm information, effectively reducing the workload of inspection personnel.

③ Automatic analysis of surveillance content, self-tagging, and cloud storage of effective surveillance information effectively reduce the workload for surveillance queries.

**02/Enhanced Venue Safety**

① High-accuracy AI facial recognition algorithms provide real-time monitoring and early warning for strangers and blacklisted individuals, ensuring the safety of personnel in the venue.

② Intelligent analysis of personnel behavior provides real-time alarms for misconduct, dangerous actions, falls, and injuries, preventing hazards from occurring immediately.

③ Real-time detection of conditions within the scene, providing remote early warnings for incidents such as fires and equipment damage.

**03/Data Storage and Security**

① Supports cloud storage or autonomous local area network storage, auditable and controllable, with no risk of data leakage, ensuring data security.

② Supports upgrades from local or cloud sources, including full package and incremental package upgrade management for software applications and algorithm components.

③ Domestically developed and independently controllable, utilizing domestic chips and proprietary algorithms, ensuring reliability and controllability.

**04/Convenient Device Deployment**

① The AI Vision Box is plug-and-play, with built-in algorithms compatible with existing security systems, independently controllable hardware, reducing deployment costs.

② Compatible with various general-purpose network cameras, supporting standard video streaming protocols (e.g., RTSP/GB28181) and high-definition resolution.

③ Local technical team support, including device installation and deployment training services, enabling rapid go-live for convenient device deployment.
