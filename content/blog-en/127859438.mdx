The Intelligent Vision Inspection System is a system solution provided for Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) applications. The vision system features excellent performance, low power consumption, intelligent control, and a long service life. Powered by the new Intel® Core™ processor, the AIISG vision inspection system can provide enhanced computing and graphics capabilities. Furthermore, the AIISG series features rich I/O interfaces, offering PoE and USB 3.0 camera controllers for vision inspection applications. Advantech's vision systems are primarily used for packaging inspection, label inspection, water quality inspection, calibration inspection, and other applications that rely on machine vision.
