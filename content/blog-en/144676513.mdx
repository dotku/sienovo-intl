8-Channel Vibration + 4-Channel Temperature (24-bit) High-Precision Terminal Data Acquisition Board

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144676513/01_a2d3b25fe27743d6acc78d1815d498f6.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144676513/02_31160491658e40bfaea504a8d1729e39.png)

The core processing unit features an EP4CE10F17I7 + STM32H743 hardware combination. This represents an upgrade from the 8-channel vibration + 4-channel temperature (16-bit) version and is a 12-channel data acquisition device designed for industrial field environments. It is primarily aimed at applications with high sampling rate requirements (supporting a maximum sampling rate of 102.4KHz), 24-bit sampling resolution, and stringent anti-aliasing requirements (featuring an internal 32-order digital filter, with anti-aliasing performance even surpassing some foreign boards).
