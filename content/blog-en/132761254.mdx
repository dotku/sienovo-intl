Industrial-grade design for the entire unit, communication peripherals with isolated protection, wide voltage power supply design (9~36V DC).

Rich communication interfaces to meet control and communication needs for various scenarios. Quad-core industrial-grade processor with high performance, low power consumption, and high reliability. Fanless design with a compact enclosure.

Equipped with SylixOS, a large-scale real-time operating system with a 100% independently developed kernel, supporting POSIX interface specifications. It possesses fully independent and controllable intellectual property rights, meeting localization requirements, and features high real-time performance, high reliability, and high security. A convenient and mature programming development suite, RealEvo-IDE, is provided.

Supports CODESYS for user secondary development.

Supports protocol stacks such as EtherCAT and CANOpen.

Supports QT, providing a convenient graphical development environment.

Supports hard real-time functions, with task switching in microseconds and interrupt response in nanoseconds.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/132761254/01_c5e1efa3a118600a8b7bb18f5b11fd75.png)

Wide range of application fields, including industrial control: robot controllers, CNC controllers, motion controllers, AGV controllers, Soft PLCs, HMI integrated control units; power industry: relay protection devices, power distribution terminals, and other fields. Users can quickly complete verification based on this platform, and can also use this hardware platform (core board) with self-developed baseboards to rapidly design products.

Reduces R&D costs. Users do not need to concern themselves with underlying platform development, allowing them to invest more resources into application layer development and enhance product competitiveness.

Provides professional technical support and secondary development services from the original manufacturer.
