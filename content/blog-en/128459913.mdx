XM-1203- FPGA Phytium Custom Motherboard

Autonomous and controllable, featuring a domestic CPU, BIOS, and domestic Linux operating system. The FT-2000/4 processor offers stable performance, diverse functional interfaces, strong expandability, high integration, rich interfaces, and excellent electromagnetic compatibility.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/128459913/01_3516310ed28b83ceb9c8292b710d0c23.jpeg)

The XM-1203-FPGA is a non-standard custom processor board based on the Phytium 4-core CPU FT-2000/4, featuring clock speeds of 2.2 and 2.6GHz. This quad-core processor product offers low power consumption, strong overall performance, high stability, rich interfaces, and strong expandability, making it particularly suitable for the standard embedded industrial control industry.
