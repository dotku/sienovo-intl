02. Introduction to AM57xx SOM Core Board

◆  SOM572x is an industrial-grade core board developed by Xinming Technology based on TI's AM5728 processor.

◆  Industry's smallest form factor: 69mm\*52mm

◆  Uses high-quality, reliable 4\*80-pin precision BTB connectors to ensure signal integrity and easy installation/removal

◆  Dual Gigabit LAN, supports EtherCAT master/slave, ProfiNet, and other industrial Ethernet protocols

◆  Supports up to 10 video inputs

◆  Supports 2 CAN interfaces

◆  Supports 10 UART interfaces

◆  Supports 1 USB 3.0 & 1 USB 2.0

◆  Supports 3 LCD interfaces

◆  Supports 1 HDMI interface

◆  Supports 8 McASP audio interfaces

◆  1 SATA interface

◆  1 PCIe 3.0 interface, supports 1\*2-lanes or 2\*1-lane

◆  Supports Linux 4.9.28, DSP RTOS, and real-time RT-Linux systems

◆  Operating temperature range: -40℃~85℃ (industrial grade)
