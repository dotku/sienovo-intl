**I. 8-channel Vibration + 4-channel Temperature (16-bit) Data Acquisition Board Solution**

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144676345/01_0799347bfb54481f843208cabe0acb0e.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144676345/02_69c649dca033433281f3aa33900f30eb.png)

Using the STM32H743 as the main control chip, this 12-channel data acquisition device is designed for industrial environments. The analog signal conditioning circuit of the acquisition unit features a modular design, with configurable and expandable front-end channel modules. The first 8 channels are compatible with IEPE, 4-20mA, and voltage acquisition, while the latter 4 channels support 4-20mA, voltage, and PT100/PT1000 acquisition.

Supports fully domestic versions and OEM customization.

**II. 20 (30)-channel (16-bit) Terminal Acquisition Board**

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144676345/03_098ef9db89804d659e6d54e5a7f2f6b5.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144676345/04_b16ea88652504af8ac0bda3f7ebbbb1b.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144676345/05_0f33830603264885b8a8d9eca6991ec0.png)

Using the STM32H743 as the main control chip, two versions have been designed: a 20-channel version (16 fast channels, 4 slow channels) and a 30-channel version (24 fast channels, 6 slow channels). These are primarily used in applications where high sampling rates are not critical, but compact size and a high number of channels are required.

It adopts a modular board-level architecture. The boards are divided into two types: current boards and vibration boards. The current board is designed for acquiring 4-20mA current signals, while the vibration board is for acquiring standard 2-wire IEPE signals.
