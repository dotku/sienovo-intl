We have developed a series of products around CAN, and the stability of the CAN bus is beyond doubt.

## CAN Bus Physical Structure and Characteristics

### CAN Bus Network

The CAN bus network primarily relies on CAN_H and CAN_L. Each node achieves serial differential signal transmission through these two lines. To prevent signal reflection and interference, a 120-ohm termination resistor is required between CAN_H and CAN_L. But why 120 ohms? This is because the characteristic impedance of the cable is 120 ohms.

### CAN Transceiver
