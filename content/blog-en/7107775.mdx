## CNCC2011 - Microsoft Research Asia "Mobile Computing" Technical Reception

**2011 China National Computer Conference – Microsoft Research Asia "Mobile Computing" Technical Reception**

**Event Introduction:** The China National Computer Conference (CNCC), hosted by the China Computer Federation (CCF), is the largest and highest-level academic, technical, and industrial conference in China's computing field. CNCC2011 will be held in Shenzhen from November 24 to 26.

In conjunction with the conference, Microsoft Research Asia will host a technical reception on the evening of November 24, focusing on "Mobile Computing." We will invite speakers from academia and Microsoft Research Asia to share their ongoing research in mobile computing. The event will also include a buffet dinner, providing a relaxed environment for technical discussions. All interested conference attendees are welcome to participate.

For more details, please visit: [http://msra.cn/wp/](http://msra.cn/wp/)

**Time** November 24, 6:00–8:30 PM

**Venue** Shenzhen Convention & Exhibition Center (lecture hall TBA)
