In recent years, cloud terminal products have been widely adopted in industries such as office, education, and industrial control due to their practicality, low maintenance requirements, and enhanced data security, making them highly favored in the market. However, under complex conditions—such as variable lighting, heavy pedestrian and vehicle traffic, and diverse human movements—image quality, visual performance, and detail rendering capability are key technical indicators for evaluating IPC (Internet Protocol Camera) solutions.

The combined solution of RV1109 and RK3568 for network camera IPC transmission improves network transmission efficiency. RK3568 supports simultaneous decoding of multiple video streams, featuring a next-generation security-focused ISP (Image Signal Processor) with 8M@30fps processing power. It enables time-division multiplexing, dual-channel simultaneous scaling output, and advanced noise reduction, delivering fine-grained images even under low-light conditions.

Dual Gigabit Ethernet ports for faster network speed  
Equipped with dual Gigabit adaptive RJ45 Ethernet ports, enabling access and transmission of data from both internal and external networks, significantly improving network transmission efficiency and meeting the multi-port requirements of products such as NVRs and industrial gateways. The LAN (POE) port supports POE+ (802.3AT, 30W output power), allowing both data transmission and power supply over a single cable, ensuring reliable device operation.

RV1109 Chip Features

The RV1126 and RV1109 solutions demonstrate clear advantages across various scenarios, thanks to six key technologies: multi-level noise reduction, 3-frame HDR, sharpness & contrast enhancement, Smart AE (intelligent auto exposure), AWB (auto white balance), and distortion correction.

![在这里插入图片描述](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/128164318/07_a26b8dee1fce4f2a60d270cf6fba9063.png)![在这里插入图片描述](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/128164318/07_a26b8dee1fce4f2a60d270cf6fba9063.png)

Using the "AWB auto white balance" technology, RV1126 and RV1109 accurately reproduce true scene colors. In wide-angle comparison tests, they perform exceptionally well—thanks to chip-level distortion correction algorithms, geometric distortion is precisely corrected, ensuring natural rendering of humans, doors, and other objects.

The Ultimate "Best Match" Highlights:  
1. RV1126 and RV1109 utilize Smart265 encoding technology, which maintains high video clarity while significantly reducing file size. Under the same conditions, Smart265 can reduce file size by up to 50% compared to conventional encoding.

2. RV1126 and RV1109 come with built-in AI algorithms, enabling intelligent applications such as intrusion detection, face detection, and license plate recognition, accelerating product deployment. By integrating AI algorithms into traditional IPCs, these chips enable next-generation smart security cameras capable of multiple intelligent applications.

3. RK3568 supports up to 16 simultaneous video inputs—four times the capacity of standard 4-channel systems—saving time and effort while significantly improving efficiency.
