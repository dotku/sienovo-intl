3-channel, 102.4 kS/s/ch, ±30 V, C Series sound and vibration input module  
The NI‑9232 can measure signals from both integrated electronic piezoelectric (IEPE) and non-IEPE sensors, such as accelerometers, tachometers, and proximity probes. The NI‑9232 is also compatible with intelligent TEDS sensors.  

The NI‑9232 integrates software-selectable AC/DC coupling, IEPE open/short detection, and IEPE signal conditioning. Input channels can synchronously acquire signals. Each channel also features a built-in anti-aliasing filter that automatically adjusts to the sampling rate. When used with NI software, this module provides processing capabilities for condition monitoring applications, such as frequency analysis and order tracking.
