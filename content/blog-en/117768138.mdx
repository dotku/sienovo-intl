In vitro diagnostic methods enable rapid and accurate diagnosis at early stages of diseases, playing a crucial role in clinical medicine and related fields, and have experienced rapid growth in the global healthcare market. ZLG provides tailored solutions for commonly used equipment in the in vitro diagnostics industry, continuously collaborating with renowned medical device manufacturers to launch a series of intelligent in vitro diagnostic devices that align with healthcare professionals' operational habits.

#### Semi-Automatic Biochemical Analyzer

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/117768138/01_314616ac46f97e0404d53c52fb58dc81.png)

A biochemical analyzer is a device that uses photoelectric colorimetry to measure specific chemical components in body fluids. It automates key steps in medical analysis such as sampling, reagent addition, incubation, reaction, detection, and result calculation. It features fast measurement speed, high accuracy, and low reagent consumption. Biochemical analyzers are generally categorized into semi-automatic and fully automatic types.

For semi-automatic biochemical analyzers, Xinming offers the Cortex-A15 platform main control solution—the AM5728 industrial core board—running open-source Ubuntu, delivering customers a cost-effective development platform with rich interface resources for easy application expansion. Combined with Xinming's proprietary products such as keypad driver chips and thermal micro printers, the solution significantly shortens development cycles.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/117768138/02_521b24dbeceb873f47162504d46a360c.png)

Module development and customization based on the Xinming XM5728-IDK-V3.

Key Features:

- Based on the TI AM5728 floating-point dual DSP C66x + dual ARM Cortex-A15 industrial control and high-performance audio/video processor;  
- Heterogeneous multi-core CPU integrating dual-core Cortex-A15, dual-core C66x floating-point DSP, dual-core PRU-ICSS, dual-core Cortex-M4 IPU, and dual-core GPU, supporting multi-core development via OpenCL, OpenMP, and SysLink IPC;  
- Powerful video encoding and decoding capabilities: supports hardware encoding/decoding of 1 channel 1080P60, or 2 channels 720P60, or 4 channels 720P30, and software decoding of H.265 video;  
- Supports up to 1 channel 1080P60 full HD video input and dual outputs via LCD and HDMI 1.4a;  
- Dual-core PRU-ICSS industrial real-time control subsystem supporting industrial protocols such as EtherCAT, EtherNet/IP, and PROFIBUS;  
- High-performance GPU with dual-core SGX544 3D accelerator and GC320 2D graphics engine, supporting OpenGL ES 2.0;  
- Rich peripheral interfaces including dual Gigabit Ethernet, PCIe, GPMC, USB 2.0, UART, SPI, QSPI, SATA 2.0, I2C, DCAN, and other industrial control buses and interfaces, with support for high-speed USB 3.0;  
- Development board exposes V-PORT video interface for flexible connection to video input modules;  
- Ultra-compact size: only 86.5mm × 60.5mm;  
- Industrial-grade precision B2B connector with 0.5mm pitch, stable, easy to plug/unplug, reverse-insertion protected; high-speed connectors used for critical high-data-rate interfaces to ensure signal integrity.
