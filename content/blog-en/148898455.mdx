An X86-based industrial PC dedicated to acoustic monitoring and analysis must be designed to combine industrial reliability, high-performance computing, and professional acoustic processing requirements. Below are its key features and application highlights:

#### **I. Core Hardware Configuration Requirements**

1.  **High-Performance Processor**
    Utilizes multi-core Intel Core i5/i7 or equivalent processors (e.g., 13th/14th Gen), supporting real-time operation of complex acoustic algorithms (such as noise spectrum analysis, sound source localization) 511.
2.  **Large-Capacity Memory and High-Speed Storage**
    *   DDR5 memory (≥32GB), supporting parallel processing of multi-channel acoustic data 811.
    *   NVMe SSD (PCIe 4.0 interface), ensuring high-speed data read/write (e.g., 6GB/s image/audio stream caching) 78.
3.  **Professional Expansion Interfaces**
    *   Multiple PCIe slots: supporting acoustic acquisition cards, GPU accelerator cards (for AI voiceprint recognition) 36.
    *   High-speed network interfaces: 2.5G/10 Gigabit Ethernet ports, enabling synchronized multi-sensor data acquisition and low-latency transmission 714.

#### **II. Industrial Environment Adaptability**

1.  **Wide Temperature Operation**
    Supports -20℃ to 65℃ environments, suitable for extreme conditions such as outdoor monitoring stations and factory workshops 34.
2.  **Anti-Interference Design**
    *   Enhanced electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) to prevent industrial equipment interference with acoustic signal accuracy 414.
    *   Dustproof and waterproof chassis (IP-rated), ensuring long-term stable operation 512.
3.  **Vibration and Shock Protection**
    Reinforced mechanical structure to reduce the impact of equipment vibration on acoustic sensors 512.

#### **III. Dedicated Acoustic Analysis Functions**

1.  **Multi-Channel Data Acquisition**
    Supports high-precision MEMS microphone array access, enabling noise source localization and 3D sound field modeling 1114.
2.  **Real-time Algorithm Processing**
    *   Built-in A/C weighting, FFT spectrum analysis, and equivalent continuous sound level (Leq) calculation 14.
    *   Compatible with acoustic simulation software such as MATLAB and COMSOL, supporting AI noise pattern recognition 11.
3.  **Remote Management and Cloud Collaboration**
    *   4G/5G modules enable real-time upload of monitoring data to the cloud 79.
    *   VPro technology supports remote BIOS debugging and system maintenance 7.

#### **IV. Typical Application Scenarios**

*   **Industrial Noise Monitoring**
    Real-time analysis of factory equipment noise (range 30-130dB), with automatic alerts for exceedances 14.
*   **Environmental Acoustics Research**
    Long-term monitoring of urban traffic noise and soundscapes in ecological protection zones 911.
*   **Intelligent Medical Diagnosis**
    Combines AI to analyze abnormal breath sounds and heart sounds, assisting remote medical diagnosis 10.
