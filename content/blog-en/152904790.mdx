**Solution Introduction**

To develop more complex and advanced autonomous driving systems, extensive sensor data and ground truth data must be collected through road tests to validate sensor functionality and train autonomous driving algorithms.

Accurately and efficiently collecting, storing, and transferring data generated by various high-bandwidth sensors on vehicles requires a data acquisition system with multi-sensor acquisition capabilities and powerful computing performance. Such a system must ensure stable device performance, offer flexible configuration and expansion, and provide flexible software-level configuration and data processing to enhance the efficiency and quality of data collection.

Addressing the extensive sensor data acquisition and validation needs during the development and testing phases of autonomous driving systems, Sienovo introduces a specialized autonomous driving data acquisition solution. This solution includes the necessary hardware, software, and customized services to help improve R&D efficiency.

**Solution Advantages**

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb8.r2.dev/blog/152904790/02_5f1a462ee8ab5eac41fba232ac94cad5.jpeg)

**Precise Timing and Synchronization**

Supports the capture and processing of data from various sensors (e.g., Lidar, Camera, IMU) with nanosecond-level time synchronization accuracy, ensuring the effectiveness and accuracy of autonomous driving algorithm analysis.

Camera and Lidar timestamp synchronization error less than 1ms.

Lidar gPTP timing accuracy less than 20μs.

Multi-channel camera trigger mode exposure synchronization error less than 10μs.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb8.r2.dev/blog/152904790/03_606b66bb216a1d0e35ac7f7ada49f9d6.jpeg)

**High-Bandwidth Transmission and Storage**

Supports high-bandwidth transmission and storage, ensuring data can be rapidly and accurately transferred from acquisition points to the processing center, and stored quickly and securely, providing a solid foundation for subsequent data analysis and processing.

High-speed data acquisition at 10GB/s.

Supports disk array card + SAS SSDs for a theoretical maximum of 8 GByte/s.

Lossless storage of raw data up to 120TB.

Supports data compression ratios of 50 to 200 times.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb8.r2.dev/blog/152904790/04_8598aecf72154f844a9de26dce67a6b1.jpeg)

**Bypass Acquisition for Non-Intrusive Diversion**

Supports bypass acquisition, ensuring real-time monitoring of data transmission and data analysis without interfering with the normal operation of the main system.

Does not disrupt the original data flow; the host system remains unaffected.

Supports multi-channel data transmission.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb8.r2.dev/blog/152904790/05_ccca1fc76854e14e100844ee80a2fb62.jpeg)

**High Data Consistency**

Achieves a high degree of consistency for various types of sensor data during acquisition, transmission, and storage, ensuring data accuracy and reliability.

Unified timing for acquired bus data and camera data, with a ΔT time error of less than 1 millisecond.

Supports continuous storage without frame loss.
