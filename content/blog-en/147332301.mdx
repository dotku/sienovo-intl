With the development of power technology and increasing demand, smart grid construction has garnered global attention. The construction of the power 'five-prevention' system is a core task for ensuring China's power safety, and the grounding management system is a critical component, essential for the safe, stable, and efficient operation of power systems. Industrial Panel PCs, with their excellent performance, stability, and environmental adaptability, and ability to meet EMC requirements for the power industry, including Power Level 3B and Level 4B certifications, play a crucial role in power grounding systems.

![图片](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/147332301/01_f5340b3a80e98f3cb51bb00033c1597a.png)

A grounding system, also known as a grounding grid, consists of multiple buried metal grounding electrodes and conductors connected in a mesh structure. Its main functions include protecting personnel and equipment safety, and reducing electromagnetic interference. In the current power technology landscape, it is also evolving towards intelligence. For example, by utilizing sensors and monitoring technology, the status of the grounding system can be monitored in real-time, allowing for timely detection of issues and maintenance. Furthermore, techniques such as big data analysis can be employed to predict and optimize the performance of the grounding system, further enhancing its reliability and efficiency.

Industrial Panel PCs are specifically designed for industrial environments, featuring **durability, high performance, multiple interfaces, and stringent environmental requirements**. They can operate stably under harsh conditions, boasting a robust structure and dustproof and waterproof characteristics. These characteristics make them widely applicable to equipment within power grounding systems. As data acquisition terminals, they can collect and monitor grounding system data in real-time, such as resistance and current.

Equipped with an Intel® 11th-U mobile platform CPU, integrating dual Intel® Gigabit Ethernet ports, supporting dual hard drive storage, and supporting APQ aDoor module expansion and WiFi/4G wireless expansion. The front panel meets IP65 requirements, featuring dustproof, waterproof, shockproof, and high-temperature resistance characteristics. It can operate stably in harsh industrial environments and can **meet the EMC requirements of the power industry, including Power Level 3B and Level 4B certifications**.

In practical applications, serving as a touch display device for distribution cabinets in a client's grounding management system, it collects operational data from the distribution cabinets and uploads and integrates this data via network interfaces such as Ethernet and WiFi to monitor the real-time operational status of the distribution cabinets.

**_Its application advantages are reflected in the following aspects:_**

**1**

**Real-time Monitoring and Data Acquisition**

It collects real-time operational status data from various devices in the grounding management system, including critical parameters such as voltage, current, temperature, and humidity. Through built-in sensors and interfaces, data is rapidly transmitted to the monitoring center, providing real-time and accurate monitoring information for O&M personnel.

**2**

**Intelligent Analysis and Early Warning**

Based on the powerful data processing capabilities of the PL156CQ-E6, the grounding management system can intelligently analyze this real-time data to identify potential safety hazards and fault risks. Through preset warning rules and algorithms, the system can automatically issue warning signals, reminding O&M personnel to take timely measures to prevent accidents.

**3**

**Remote Control and Operation**

The PL156CQ-E6 supports remote control and operation functions, allowing O&M personnel to remotely log in via the network to control and operate distribution cabinets. This remote operation method not only improves work efficiency but also reduces safety risks for O&M personnel.

**4**

**System Integration and Interconnection**

A grounding management system is a comprehensive framework that requires the integration of multiple subsystems and devices. Industrial Panel PCs offer excellent compatibility and expandability, enabling easy integration and interconnection with other subsystems and devices. Through unified interfaces and protocols, they ensure data sharing and collaborative work among various subsystems in the application, enhancing the overall intelligence level of the grounding management system.

**5**

**Security and Reliability**

In power grounding management systems, security and reliability are paramount. Industrial Panel PCs utilize over 70% domestically produced chips and feature completely independent R&D and design, ensuring security. Furthermore, they possess high reliability and stability, capable of maintaining consistent performance during long-term operation and in harsh environments.

The application solution of Industrial Panel PCs in power grounding management systems, through its advantages in real-time monitoring and data acquisition, intelligent analysis and early warning, remote control and operation, system integration and interconnection, and security and reliability, provides a strong guarantee for the safe, stable, and efficient operation of power O&M. In the future, with the continuous development of smart grids, Industrial Panel PCs will play an increasingly important role in more fields, driving the continuous advancement of industrial intelligence.
