The RK3588+FPGA core module features Rockchip RK3588, a new-generation flagship octa-core 64-bit processor. It supports 8K video encoding and decoding, multi-screen 4K output, and can achieve 12-screen video wall splicing, simultaneous display, and differentiated display. Compatible with various operating systems, it is widely applicable in fields such as exhibitions, advertising content delivery, new retail, and supermarkets for displaying various media content.

　　**Product Features**

　　1. The RK3588 core module utilizes an 8nm LP process; it features an octa-core 64-bit CPU with a main frequency of up to 2.4GHz; integrates an ARM Mali-G610 MP4 quad-core GPU, and has a built-in AI accelerator NPU, providing 6Tops of computing power.

　　2. Supports 8K@60fps H.265/VP9 video decoding and 8K@30fps H.265/H.264 video encoding, with simultaneous encoding and decoding, achieving up to 32 channels of 1080P@30fps decoding and 16 channels of 1080p@30fps encoding.

　　3. Supports 4 inputs and 4 outputs, configurable with up to 64GB of ultra-large operating memory (RAM).

　　4. Supports multi-screen video wall splicing, simultaneous display, and differentiated display.

　　5. Features multiple display interface inputs and outputs (HDMI 2.1/eDP1.3/MIPI-DSI/DP1.4/BT.1120), integrates a 48MP ISP with HDR&3DNR, and supports dual MIPI-CSI camera inputs.

　　6. Integrates PCIe3.0/GMAC/SDIO3.0/USB3.0. Expandable with multiple Gigabit Ethernet ports, WiFi6/Bluetooth, and 5G/4G LTE.

　　7. Integrates PCIe3.0/SATA3.0 interfaces, supporting expansion of multiple SSD/HDD large-capacity storage devices, allowing the device to easily expand to [TB-level](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=243772970&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=TB%E7%BA%A7&zhida_source=entity "TB级") ultra-large capacity.

　　8. Features expansion interfaces such as PCIe3.0, SATA3.0, I2S, I2C, CAN, UART, SPDIF, SDIO3.0, MIPI-CSI, MIPI-DSI, USB3.0, USB2.0, SPI, and GPIO.

　　9. Supports operating systems such as Linux and Ubuntu.
