> ChatGPT's capabilities have now extended to the field of robotics, allowing intuitive control via language over various platforms such as robotic arms, drones, and home assistant robots. Will this change the future of human-robot interaction?

Have you ever imagined telling a robot what to do in your own words, just like speaking to a person?

For example, simply telling your home assistant robot 'Please heat up my lunch' and it can then find the microwave itself. Pretty amazing, right?

Although language is the most intuitive way for humans to express intentions, for a long time, people have heavily relied on writing code to control robots. However, with the advent of ChatGPT, this situation is about to change.

In a recent study, the Microsoft team explored how to use OpenAI's new AI language model, ChatGPT, to enable natural human-robot interaction.
