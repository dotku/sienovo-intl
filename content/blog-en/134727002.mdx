The power industry involves multiple stages including power generation, distribution, transmission, substation, and electricity sales. It features an extensive variety of equipment, spans vast geographical areas, and profoundly impacts national economic and social development—making it unparalleled among industries. With national policies and the three major power grid enterprises clearly defining strategic goals for digitalization and intelligent development, Yingma Technology follows national directives and industry trends to introduce a smart power solution. This solution can be applied to various power-specific scenarios such as AI-powered intelligent substation inspection, intelligent defect identification in power transmission and distribution lines, automated drone inspection, perimeter intrusion detection, and personnel operation compliance monitoring.
