The XM-6815 is a wall-mounted PC based on the Intel 11th Gen Core i-series platform CPU, featuring an expandable memory slot, 1× mSATA, 3× Gigabit Ethernet ports, 6× COM ports, 4× USB 3.0, and 4× USB 2.0. This product is suitable for wall-mounted applications such as industrial robot controllers and machine vision controllers.
