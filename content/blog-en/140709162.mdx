Industrial Panel PCs with Intel 10th Gen Core Processor Series for Medical Applications

Currently, emerging information technologies such as artificial intelligence, big data, and the Internet of Things are being deeply integrated into the healthcare industry, driving continuous advancements in hospital informatization. As a result, industrial panel PCs are seeing increasingly widespread use in medical equipment.

![1694410283387057.jpg](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/140709162/02_e2c1e019306b809d57f3ef8e767372a9.jpeg)

Modern hospitals are gradually adopting more intelligent, networked, and digital medical devices to optimize resource utilization, reduce the workload of medical staff, and improve the quality and efficiency of healthcare services. Industrial panel PCs, with their powerful data processing and human-machine interaction capabilities, high reliability, stability, and support for customized development, can be highly integrated into various medical devices. They provide functions such as data processing, human-machine control, information storage and retrieval, and voice interaction, thereby enhancing the modernization of medical equipment and supporting hospital informatization.

Currently, industrial panel PCs are integrated into various medical devices, including medical imaging equipment like CT scanners, in vitro diagnostic devices such as fully automated coagulation analyzers, and patient monitors. With the rapid development of the "AI + Healthcare" concept, industrial panel PCs are poised for even broader adoption in hospital environments, offering vast market potential. To meet this demand, we have introduced industrial panel PC solutions based on the Intel 10th Gen Core processor series for medical equipment applications.

**Industrial Panel PC Solution with Intel 10th Gen Core Processors for Medical Equipment**

As a provider of specialized embedded computing products, Xinmay offers a range of high-performance industrial panel PCs based on the Intel 10th Gen Core processor series, available in various sizes (12.1", 15", 15.6", 17", 18.5", 19", 21.5") tailored for medical equipment applications. Customers can select the appropriate model based on their specific environmental and performance requirements.

In terms of data processing capability, this product series supports 10th Gen Intel Core i3/i5/i7/i9 LGA1200 processors, delivering high-performance computing. It features 1× SO-DIMM memory slot supporting DDR4-2933/2666/2133MHz, with up to 32GB of RAM. Storage expansion is supported via SATA and M.2 M KEY 2280 (supporting either PCIe x4 or SATA protocol, selectable). Multiple onboard Gigabit Ethernet ports, USB 2.0/3.0, and COM interfaces are provided, along with optional expansion modules for Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, 4G, and 5G, meeting the requirements for multi-device networking and high-speed, large-capacity data transmission, storage, and processing.

For human-machine interaction, the series adopts a modular, wire-free hard connection design, with flexible I/O interface configurations to support various peripheral connections and centralized management. The integrated touch and display design uses a capacitive touch screen with multi-touch support. A single HDMI interface enables 4K resolution output, delivering superior touch and display performance.

Regarding reliability and usability, the series features a new fanless wide-temperature heat dissipation structure, ensuring silent operation and low power consumption. It is designed with rugged characteristics including wide-temperature operation, shock resistance, electromagnetic interference protection, dustproofing, and moisture resistance. Installation options include panel-mounting and VESA wall mounting, making it suitable for use in diverse industrial and commercial environments.
