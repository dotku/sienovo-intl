##### **Laser Marking Machine Equipment Industry Applications**

**Project Background**
Smart marking machine systems hold a mainstream position in the market due to their flexible recognition algorithms, high precision, and integrated processing and vision capabilities. They are primarily applied in scenarios requiring higher refinement and precision, such as processing fields like electronic components, integrated circuits, mobile communications, automotive parts, jewelry, and precision instruments. As modern precision processing equipment, laser marking machines can significantly improve production efficiency and reduce enterprise tooling and labor costs. They are suitable for various automated production line applications and intelligent upgrades, making them the optimal choice for enterprises seeking intelligent equipment upgrades.

**Solution Requirements**
● Requires higher vibration resistance
● Needs high-speed Ethernet ports for high-definition image transmission

**Solution**
Provides DPC series industrial panel PCs
DPC series industrial panel PCs integrate vision and motion control to achieve workflows for material positioning, marking, and inspection, making laser marking equipment more stable and precise. DPC series products come in various sizes and support multiple installation methods, meeting the needs of different industrial sites and addressing customer challenges.
