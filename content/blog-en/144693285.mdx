8-channel voltage, 20 MS/s PCIe data acquisition card

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144693285/01_dc7bf5fb1b4b4745bcc5870bd1c80648.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144693285/02_ab2d0cf2af1642159218cfa6a20b7b32.png)

Equipped with an XC7A100T-2FGG484I FPGA, this is a high-speed PCIe acquisition card specifically designed for industrial PCs, offering a maximum sampling rate of 20 MS/s. It functions as an add-on card that can be inserted into a PCIe slot of an industrial PC. Data acquired through the PCIe interface is transferred to the CPU, allowing application-layer software to be developed directly on the CPU. It is primarily used in scenarios where it needs to be combined with an industrial PC or a desktop computer, where high sampling resolution is not critical, and cost is a significant factor.

Each acquisition card integrates 16 channels. Further channel expansion depends on the available PCIe slots in the industrial PC or desktop computer. The total number of channels = Number of PCIe slots * 16.
