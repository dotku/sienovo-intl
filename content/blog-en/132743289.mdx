### Xinmai Modular Data Acquisition Solutions

With the rapid development of the test and measurement market, rapidly growing technological demands are emerging in various fields, such as electric vehicles, 5G communication, and new battery technologies, creating new testing requirements.

#### Modular Design, Excellent Flexibility

*   Modular Design: iDAQ chassis and various iDAQ modules help you customize measurement and control systems according to different application requirements.
*   Hot-Swappable Support: iDAQ I/O modules can be flexibly inserted into the iDAQ chassis to quickly build custom systems.
*   Flexible Expansion: The iDAQ-934 built-in USB3.0 chassis supports up to 5 chassis for flexible expansion.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/132743289/04_c975d870ada74cf8f7d457890f8c79b8.png)

Precise Time Synchronization and Simplified Wiring

*   Time synchronization is achieved between iDAQ modules.
*   Modules within the chassis are automatically synchronized via an internal bus, eliminating the need for complex wiring.
*   Multiple iDAQ-934 chassis can also be synchronized via Programmable Function Pins (PFP).

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/132743289/05_d607571b045b8ec79aad8946886ea560.png)

*   Wide Temperature Design: -20~60°C.
*   Aluminum alloy casing provides better noise and EMI protection.
*   DB15 connectors provide better vibration resistance and are tested under 5Grms random vibration and 30G shock.
