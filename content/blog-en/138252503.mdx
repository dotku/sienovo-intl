### ±10 V, 250 kS/s, 16-Bit, 32-Channel C Series Voltage Input Module

The NI-9205 performs single-ended or differential analog input, with four programmable input ranges per channel. This module delivers a high channel count and high speed at a lower cost, making it suitable for economical, multifunction systems. The module also provides four programmable input ranges. To protect against signal transients, the NI-9205 offers up to 60 V of overvoltage protection between the input channels and common terminal. Additionally, the NI-9205 features dual channel-to-ground isolation barriers, ensuring safety, noise immunity, and a high common-mode voltage range. It provides 1,000 Vrms of continuous overvoltage protection.
