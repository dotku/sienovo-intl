## Edge Computing Box

8-core A53 | 10.6 TOPS Computing Power

● Computing power up to 10.6 TOPS, a single chip supports real-time decoding of up to 8 channels of H.264 & H.265.

● Expandable with 4G/5G/WIFI wireless network connectivity, providing convenience for edge business deployment.

● Supports RS232 /RS485/USB2.0/USB3.0/HDMI OUT/Dual Gigabit Ethernet, etc.

● Low-power design, combined with enclosure heat dissipation.

● Supports -20℃～+60℃ wide operating temperature range.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/134754567/01_278b76b2459f9a40b52c2562e462a157.png)

A highly integrated smart workstation with powerful computing performance, featuring a built-in third-generation TPU. The processor is an 8-core ARM Cortex-A53 with a clock speed up to 2.3GHz, and INT8 computing power up to 10.6 TOPS. This smart workstation supports wide operating temperature environments and can be flexibly deployed in various AI scenarios, finding wide applications in smart factories, smart construction sites, smart urban management, smart gas stations, and other fields.
