Content:
1.  1.  Product Overview

The INT-AIBOX-RK4 intelligent edge analytics all-in-one device is a high-performance, low-power edge computing solution. Powered by the domestic TPU chip RK3588, it features an integrated 6 TOPS NPU for AI computing and supports up to 8K video encoding and decoding.

This product integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms based on computer vision and deep learning networks, along with a comprehensive video intelligence management platform. The AI algorithms support various application scenarios such as industrial parks, residential communities, construction sites, and campuses, and can be flexibly combined and configured according to specific needs. The video intelligence management platform enables front-end device management, real-time video preview, alarm notifications, evidence capture, online algorithm loading and optimization, and data visualization dashboards. The device is easy to operate, plug-and-play, and provides rich northbound API interfaces to empower large-scale upper-layer business platforms.

1.  1.  Product Features

**Ultra-High Performance Computing and Codec Capabilities**

*   Up to 6 TOPS peak NPU computing power;
*   Supports full-process processing of up to 4 channels of 1080P HD video;
*   Supports hardware decoding of up to 4 channels of H.264/H.265 1080P@25FPS video;
*   Supports hardware encoding of up to 4 channels of H.264/H.265 1080P@25FPS video.

**Built-in Rich AI Algorithms**

*   Comes with over 30 built-in AI algorithms, supporting flexible combinations and custom configurations;

(Supported algorithms include: flame detection, smoke detection, smoking detection, phone call detection, mobile phone usage detection, face mask not worn detection, personnel absence detection, personnel sleeping on duty detection, personnel fall detection, area people counting, insufficient personnel in area, overcrowding detection, abnormal personnel count, intrusion detection, workwear detection, safety helmet detection, reflective vest detection, electric vehicle detection, proper parking (illegal parking) detection, entrance/exit pedestrian flow counting, vehicle flow counting, perimeter climbing intrusion, personnel crossing boundary detection, loitering detection, trash not in bin detection, overflowing trash bin detection, garbage disposal reminder, camera abnormal displacement detection, etc.)

*   Each video channel supports up to 3 simultaneous AI analysis tasks;
*   Supports up to 4 concurrent video AI analysis tasks; when more than 4 tasks are required, polling analysis is supported.

**Rich Interfaces and Flexible Deployment**

*   Rich interface support: 1000M Ethernet, USB3.0/USB2.0, HDMI, RS-485, RS-232;
*   Supports wide operating temperature range from -20°C to +60°C;
*   IP40 protection rating, fanless heat dissipation design;
*   Northbound interfaces: HTTP, MQTT, GB28181;
*   Southbound interfaces: GB28181, ONVIF, RTSP.

**High Reliability and Encryption Protection**

*   Supports high-capacity eMMC with optional primary/backup partitioning;
*   Supports alarm and protection mechanisms for abnormal faults;
*   Equipped with programmable encryption chip for privacy data protection.

**User-Friendly Toolchain and Flexible Development**

*   Supports mainstream deep learning frameworks: Caffe, DarkNet, TensorFlow, PyTorch, MXNet, ONNX, PaddlePaddle;
*   Supports classification and detection network models, with support for custom operator development;

Supports Docker containerization for rapid algorithm application deployment.
