In recent years, with the rapid development of national highway construction and the fast growth in highway mileage, while facilitating public travel and promoting economic and social development, highway traffic volume has also increased rapidly. Numerous problems such as vehicle speeding, overloading, illegal lane occupation by large vehicles, non-emergency stops, and traffic congestion on highways have gradually become prominent. The number of road traffic accidents and casualties on highways has shown a gradual upward trend, causing significant losses to the lives and property of the nation and its people. Comprehensive control of road conditions, standardized vehicle behavior, full coverage monitoring without blind spots, emergency rescue, vehicle and personnel supervision, and timely sharing of traffic information have become urgent requirements for the informatization development of various business systems within traffic management departments.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138307177/01_39b5bb54366418db7145f1c7674b3c62.png)

Xinmai and Phytium's Smart Transportation solution, supported by a big data cloud platform, focuses on the management operations of traffic police, the construction of intelligent transportation safety management platforms, and traffic dispatch centers. It proposes an integrated intelligent transportation safety management platform solution, thereby effectively promoting the development of intelligent platforms for highway traffic police, and achieving the development goals of improving traffic safety, enhancing highway management capabilities, efficiency, and standards. Ultimately, it aims to establish an orderly, safe, smooth, economical, and environmentally friendly highway transportation network system, further advancing police informatization, elevating the intelligence level of traffic management, strengthening the innovation capabilities of traffic police detachments in traffic management, and leveraging the important role of intelligent management in highway construction to better serve the public.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138307177/02_c65fbaa26d275d3101801764ef145733.png)

1. Front-end Subsystems
Includes video surveillance systems, high-definition checkpoint systems, violation capture systems, and speed measurement systems.

2. Traffic Operation Monitoring and Early Warning Platform
The operation monitoring platform primarily focuses on management operations, establishing a unified working environment for comprehensive traffic operation monitoring. It mainly achieves monitoring and management of highway operations, overload control on highways, road administration, urban parking, regional traffic, and central road network operations. It enables comprehensive access, integration, and real-time monitoring of information data from various traffic domains, enhancing comprehensive traffic operation monitoring capabilities.

3. Traffic Emergency Command and Coordination Platform
By building an emergency command and coordination platform, efficient management of emergency information and resources is achieved. The platform utilizes analytical models to enable intelligent applications such as decision analysis for emergency command, traffic guidance, and evacuation. Furthermore, through functions like unified information reporting, coordinated handling of operational incidents, and customized monitoring information and video sharing, it facilitates information flow between traffic-related departments, multi-level collaborative traffic operation monitoring, and enables various sectors to play a coordinated role in traffic congestion relief during emergencies.
From a business perspective, the emergency command and coordination platform includes functions such as emergency prevention and preparedness, emergency response, and traffic organization support for major events.

4. Traffic Auxiliary Decision Support Platform
The business functions of the auxiliary decision support platform include a comprehensive traffic dynamic data analysis system, traffic simulation analysis, and an automatic traffic operation monitoring report generation system.

5. Traffic Information Service Platform
The information service platform collects comprehensive traffic data and utilizes multiple channels such as travel portals, mobile apps, traffic radio, and road guidance screens to publish comprehensive traffic travel information, including road traffic, urban public transportation, and inter-provincial passenger transport. It provides comprehensive, accurate, and timely information services to improve travel efficiency.
The main functions of the information service platform include information release management, basic information services, and dynamic information services.
