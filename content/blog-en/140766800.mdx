This is an SOM Express module based on the RDC 3306. The RDC 3306 X86 architecture CPU is a high-performance, highly stable processor. It is a flexible and compact mainboard (measuring 91.8mm × 68.6mm) that can be flexibly integrated into user carrier boards, saving development costs. The module's connectors use a pin-header format, ensuring stability during use.
