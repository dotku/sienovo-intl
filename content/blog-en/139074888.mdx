1. Product Introduction  
    1.1 Product Overview

The 4U dual-processor Xeon ruggedized server serves as an information processing server capable of loading application software and performing functions such as data processing and information exchange. It features high integration, efficient heat dissipation, stable performance, and convenient maintenance. Its environmental adaptability and electromagnetic compatibility comply with national standards.

1.3 Coating

Exposed surfaces of the chassis are coated. The enclosure shall be ice gray (GY09) as specified in GB/T 3181-2008, with a textured powder coating finish. Markings and characters shall also conform to GB/T 3181-2008.
