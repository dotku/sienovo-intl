VU37P FPGA with HBM

A powerful solution for accelerating memory latency-constrained applications.

Designed for compute acceleration, this PCIe board features a Xilinx VU37P FPGA integrated with HBM2 memory. The HBM2's capacity and speed (8G/16GB, up to 512GB/s) can accelerate memory-bound applications. The board's 100G QSFP28s are ideal for clustering, and OCuLink connectors allow for expansion.
