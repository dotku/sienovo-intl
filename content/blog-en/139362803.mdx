The Phytium cloud terminal is a domestically developed, independent, and controllable solution based on the high-performance 8-core Phytium D2000 processor platform. Equipped with the domestic Kunlun firmware and supporting domestic operating systems such as UOS and Kylin OS, it meets the requirements for secure domestic information processing. It achieves full domestication, independence, and control from hardware, operating system to applications, making it a flagship solution for domestic secure desktop cloud computing.
