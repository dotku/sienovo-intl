*   ****Core Hardware Configuration****

****1. Controller Chip****‌

*   Utilizes the domestic Zhongxingwei 63104 controller chip, supporting PCIe transfer rates of 2.5GT/s, 5GT/s, 8GT/s, 16GT/s, and 32GT/s, with support for 96+8 lanes. It supports 6 x16 groups and 1 x8 group, with each group supporting 1 to 8 ports. The x16 groups support x16, x8, x4, x2 ports; the x8 group supports x8, x4, x2, x1 ports.
*   Basic Mode: Extends PCIe interfaces and supports multi-level expansion, as shown in the figure below.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/149193840/01_d6faeef9a6684508b95f981a771f4c98.jpeg)

*   NT Mode: Multiple PCIe domains are interconnected, supporting NTB and DMA features, as shown in the figure below.

*   ‌P2P Mode: Supports data transfer between any DPs, effectively increasing transmission bandwidth and reducing transmission latency.
*   Supports non-blocking crossbar architecture.
*   Integrates SM2/SM3/SM4 encryption engines to ensure secure data transmission and meet domestic replacement requirements.
*   Supports standard hot-plug and surprise hot-plug.

****2. Expansion Interfaces****‌

*   Upstream Bus Interface: PCIe Gen5 x16, gold finger connector.
*   Downstream Interfaces and Connectors:

*   1 PCIe Gen5 x16, connector model 10146070-113Z0LF, external gold finger connector.
*   4 PCIe Gen5 x16, connector model G97V24332HR, external cable connection.
*   2 PCIe Gen5 x4, connector model G97V21352HR, external cable connection.![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/149193840/02_269fe7b6a5e84096bb89f85b68e1a011.png)

*   ****‌Performance****

*   Supports 32GT/s single-lane speed, with bidirectional bandwidth up to 128GB/s (x16 aggregation).
