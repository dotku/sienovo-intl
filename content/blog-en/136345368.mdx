**Product Overview**

**Product Features**

¾ Phytium computing platform with 100% domestic localization

The VPX-MPU6902 is a computing blade based on the Phytium FT-2000+/64-core processor, operating at a main frequency of 2.2 GHz, responsible for managing and scheduling service data flows. Paired with the Phytium X100 chip featuring an integrated independent graphics unit, it is suitable for various terminal and server application scenarios, meeting high-performance computing requirements in industries such as banking, telecommunications, rail transit, and power.

1. Onboard Phytium FT-2000+/64-core high-performance processor;
2. Onboard Phytium X100 chip with integrated independent graphics;
3. Single-blade double-precision floating-point processing capability ≥192 GFlops or single-precision floating-point processing capability ≥384 GFlops;
4. 32GB memory, onboard 128GB solid-state storage;
5. Provides external interfaces including four 10G Ethernet ports, four Gigabit Ethernet ports, and two USB interfaces;
6. Runs domestic operating systems Kylin OS (Galaxy) or Hunan Kylin; supports reporting of blade temperature, voltage, and fault status monitoring information, capable of receiving and recording status monitoring data from each blade and chassis.
