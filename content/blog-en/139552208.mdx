The LCR Impedance Test Platform is a precision LCR meter with a basic measurement accuracy of up to 0.1%. It supports various test excitation modes and features a wide, continuously adjustable test frequency range from 20 Hz to 2 MHz, along with continuously adjustable test levels from 0 to 2 Vrms or 0 to 20 mArms. It also includes a DC bias function adjustable up to 2 V. This platform can test various impedance parameters, achieving precise measurements at speeds as fast as 5 ms. Its compact, modular design offers a cost-effective solution for impedance parameter testing and measurement of components, materials, semiconductors, MEMS, and more in production lines.

##### **Application Scenarios**

Primarily applied in impedance parameter testing and measurement for components, materials, semiconductors, MEMS, and similar applications.
