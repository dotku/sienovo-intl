Application Scenarios

Defect detection plays an extremely important role in the manufacturing process of products. Smart industrial defect detection can replace traditional manual inspection, reduce human error and omissions, significantly improve product quality, and simultaneously lower factory labor costs. Smart process defect detection technology can detect point, line, and surface defects, enabling faster identification of product surface flaws and defects, thereby promoting product quality in enterprises and factories, and advancing intelligent automation in manufacturing.
