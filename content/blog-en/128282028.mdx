### Digitalizing Smart Devices Through AI and Data Transformation

Data collection, visualization, automatic control, and AI analysis effectively facilitate device intelligence (M2I) and Industrial AI, serving as the foundational elements and two gears driving the advancement of smart manufacturing. By empowering smart device functions through "Device Intelligence" and "Industrial AI," smart operations and management can be achieved.

#### Providing Comprehensive Services for the Semiconductor and Automated Test Equipment Industries

Semiconductor manufacturing is a complex process composed of many individual steps. From wafer fabrication/sorting to assembly/testing/shipping, many process steps have very strict tolerances. Each step affects subsequent steps, and each parameter is the result of many different inputs.

#### Realizing the Future of Smart Manufacturing Through Automated Guided Vehicles

In the past decade, AGVs have made significant progress in improving logistics efficiency. Due to frequent errors or human intervention when handling heavy objects, moving goods around the workshop can lead to various problems. Coupled with the impact of COVID-19, there is an urgent need for fully automated and intelligent solutions to reduce manual operations.

#### Machine Vision Solutions for the Food and Beverage Industry

More and more manufacturers are using Advantech machine vision technology to improve product yield and quality and reduce defects. Advantech automated visual inspection solutions can verify product quality in food, beverage, pharmaceutical, automotive, semiconductor, and other mass production fields. From product positioning capture to moving line capture, we provide high-speed, high-precision 3D analysis.

#### Equipment Condition Monitoring and Predictive Maintenance Solutions

Predictive maintenance for mechanical equipment has become an indispensable part of Industry 4.0. Machine Condition Monitoring (MCM) systems upgrade routine consumable replacement and equipment maintenance to preventive maintenance, and even predictive maintenance. This can prevent machine failures without warning, which would lead to additional costs.

#### Automotive Component OEE Solutions

Lean manufacturing emphasizes delivering the right thing to the right place at the right time in the right quantity to improve Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) based on three metrics: availability, performance, and quality. By using Advantech automotive component testing and measurement solutions, automotive OEMs and their suppliers can perform inventory forecasting, ensure quality control, and accelerate time-to-market.
