**Solution Overview**

The high dynamic range stereo vision system leverages the exceptional dynamic range of its stereo cameras to adapt to complex lighting conditions, providing stable visual perception for mobile robots. It is compatible with embedded platforms and integrates deep learning algorithms, supporting dynamic object tracking, 3D reconstruction, and semantic understanding. Its industrial-grade protection design (IP67 waterproof, shock-resistant, temperature-tolerant) ensures long-term reliable operation in harsh outdoor and industrial environments.

**Solution Advantages**

**High Dynamic Vision**

Proprietary technology adapts to complex lighting environments, including strong and weak light, ensuring stable and precise visual output.

Supports >120dB HDR high dynamic imaging

CMOS low-light enhancement
