Introduction

The ECU-461 is an embedded communication management platform based on Phytium FT-2000/4 compatible with D2000/8 domestic processors. It features 8 onboard Gigabit Ethernet ports and 8 isolated serial ports, supporting 4 or 8 serial ports to be replaced with CAN communication. An internal PCI-E bus interface is reserved for custom expansion of more LAN or COM ports, as well as wireless communication methods.

Sienovo provides a fully localized solution.
