1.  1.  1.  **Smoke and Fire Detection Algorithm**
            1.  **Function Description**

Smoke and fire detection refers to presetting a monitoring area in a surveillance scene, and triggering an alarm when smoke or fire occurs within that area. The detection distance is 10-20 meters, and the resolution of smoke and fire in a 1080p image should be ≥ 30*30 pixels.

1.  1.  1.  1.  **Recommended Scenarios**

Scene requirements: Can be used indoors and outdoors, avoid direct sunlight or backlight conditions; only supports full-color images at night, if the camera does not support full-color mode, please use white light for illumination.

Target distance requirements: Can detect events beyond 3 meters from a normal 3-meter pole height.

Target pixel requirements: The resolution of the detected target in a 1080P image should be ≥ 30*30 pixels.

Flame colors: Supports detection of red, orange, and yellow flames.

Smoke colors: Supports detection of irregular smoke such as off-white and dark gray.

Recall rate: 90%
