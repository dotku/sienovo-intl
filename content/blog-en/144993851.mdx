*   ******Product Design Philosophy******

The HS-P2-2D is a 360-degree panoramic imaging system developed to address blind spots in heavy-duty vehicles.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144993851/02_2e46d30561094659a56875d38ae34517.png)

This safety driving assistance system uses ultra-wide-angle cameras installed around the vehicle to synthesize a panoramic bird's-eye view via algorithms. The bird's-eye view allows drivers to clearly see the surroundings of the vehicle, greatly reducing traffic accidents caused by blind spots.

*   ******Product Features******

1.  A 360-degree panoramic imaging system specifically developed for trucks and special vehicles, featuring proprietary 360-degree stitching algorithms for heavy-duty vehicles, a compact size, high integration, and a wide-temperature design, with an operating temperature range of -30°C to 70°C.

2.  The product complies with ISO-7637 standards and vehicle power supply specifications, featuring a wide voltage design of DC10-36V, reverse polarity protection, enhanced TVS+ESD design, and a supercapacitor in the main power supply to ensure reliable recording in case of unexpected power loss.

3.  The product supports multiple video outputs, including CVBS/VGA/AHD.

4.  The product supports our intelligent voice control system specifically developed for 360-degree panoramic imaging systems in heavy-duty vehicles, enabling voice control of the 360-degree panoramic imaging to ensure driving safety.

5.  The product supports proprietary algorithms, utilizing sensors within the main unit, allowing the 360-degree panoramic imaging to automatically display different perspectives based on the application scenario.

6.  The product offers two storage designs, supporting SD card storage, with a compact size, low power consumption, and excellent shock resistance.

7.  The product supports an electronic rearview mirror mode, featuring the main unit's unique dual AHD output function, which allows the 360-degree view to be displayed simultaneously on two screens. It includes dedicated software designed for electronic rearview mirrors, supports vehicle speed detection, and can output control signals to switch the electronic rearview mirror display at a set speed.

8.  The product supports 4-channel AHD 720P camera functionality and dual AHD simultaneous output, with one channel for the 360 host unit and the other for the DVR host unit.
