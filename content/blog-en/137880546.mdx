**ESOP Workstation Industry Applications**

**Project Background**

E-SOP is a management information platform that enables the digitalization of work instructions and provides unified management and centralized control. Xinmai Technology's ESOP terminal is a compact, full-featured, cost-effective industrial PC. The upper layer connects to the MES system via the network, while the lower layer connects to a display to show work instructions. The ESOP control terminal features stability and durability, making it suitable for 24/7 continuous operation in factories.

**Solution Requirements**
● On-site workstations are not fixed, locations are complex, and installation conditions are demanding.
● Factories operate 24 hours a day, so workstations must be capable of uninterrupted operation.
● Requires both wired and wireless communication capabilities.

**Solution**
The provided solutions include:
1. Embedded Industrial PC + Industrial Monitor
● Embedded Fanless Industrial PC EBC-1122
● 10.4″~19″ multi-size industrial monitors
**●** 1D/2D Barcode Scanner
