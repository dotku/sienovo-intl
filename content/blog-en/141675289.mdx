*   Onboard one XC7VX690T FPGA processing node

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/141675289/01_15e87e081e95481b94e7924ac29a321b.png)

This high-performance real-time signal processing platform is based on a 6U VPX architecture. It utilizes two TI KeyStone series multi-core DSPs, TMS320C6678, as the main processing units, and one Xilinx Virtex-7 series FPGA, XC7VX690T, as the co-processing unit. The platform features two FMC mezzanine card interfaces, and all processing nodes are interconnected via high-speed serial buses. The board adopts a standard 6U VPX Eurocard design, boasting excellent anti-vibration design, heat dissipation performance, and unique environmental protection design, making it suitable for aerospace, aviation, marine, and other application scenarios.

#### Technical Specifications

*   Standard 6U VPX form factor, compliant with VITA 46 specification;
*   Two multi-core DSP processing nodes, one Virtex-7 FPGA processing node;
*   Processing Performance:
    1.  DSP Fixed-point operations: 40 GMAC/Core * 16 = 640 GMAC;
    2.  DSP Floating-point operations: 20 GFLOPs/Core * 16 = 320 GFLOPs;
*   Storage Performance:
    1.  DSP processing nodes: 4 GByte DDR3-1333 SDRAM;
    2.  DSP processing nodes: 4 GByte Nand Flash;
    3.  FPGA processing node: Two groups of 2 GByte DDR3-1600 SDRAM;
*   Interconnect Performance:
    1.  DSP to DSP: HyperLink x4@5Gbps/lane;
    2.  DSP to FPGA: SRIO x4@5Gbps/lane;
    3.  FPGA to FPGA: Two x8 GTH links@10Gbps/lane;
    4.  FPGA to Host Controller: PCI Express x4@8Gbps/lane;
    5.  FPGA to FMC interface: Two x4 GTH links@10Gbps/lane;
*   Physical and Electrical Characteristics
    1.  Board dimensions: 100 x 233mm
    2.  Board power supply: 5A max@+12V (±5%)
    3.  Cooling method: Conduction cooling (metal cold plate)
*   Environmental Characteristics
    1.  Operating temperature: -40°C to +85°C, Storage temperature: -55°C to +125°C;
    2.  Operating humidity: 5%~95%, non-condensing

#### Application Areas

*   Software-defined radio;
*   Radar and baseband signal processing;
