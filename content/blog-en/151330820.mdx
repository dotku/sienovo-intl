As global energy transition accelerates, energy storage power stations and substations, as critical nodes in power systems, are rapidly evolving towards intelligence and digitalization. These scenarios place extremely high demands on the computing performance, communication capabilities, and reliability of underlying hardware devices.

**Equipped with 6 Gigabit Ethernet ports (RJ45 form factor)**, it provides ample physical interfaces for the multi-layer network architectures of energy storage power stations and substations. In typical applications, these network ports can be used for:

**·** Connecting intelligent devices such as station protection devices and measurement and control devices for real-time data acquisition;

**·** Accessing the dispatch data network and station control layer network to achieve data interaction with the control center;

**·** Building redundant network links to enhance system communication reliability.

**The multi-port design avoids the cost and space occupation of additional expansion switches, significantly improving system integration and reliability.**

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/151330820/01_544bb0b7881047af91cc5194ae497808.png)

**Multi-Serial Port Support**

**01**

The device is equipped with **6 RS485 serial ports**, perfectly adapting to the communication needs of various traditional devices and smart meters in power station scenarios. The RS485 bus features strong anti-interference capability and long transmission distance, enabling stable connection to power energy acquisition devices, environmental monitoring sensors, fire controllers, and other equipment. Through protocols such as Modbus RTU, centralized management of distributed devices can be achieved, providing precise underlying data support for the station's monitoring system.

**High-Speed Interfaces and Dual Display Output**

**02**

It provides **4 USB 3.0 ports**, supporting quick access to debugging tools, external storage devices, or security keys, significantly reducing data migration and maintenance operation times. Meanwhile, **HDMI and VGA dual display interfaces** allow O&M personnel to flexibly connect various display devices, facilitating on-site debugging or visual display in the monitoring center, enhancing the system's human-machine interaction experience.

**Robust Industrial Stability Design**

Adopting a 1U standard rackmount design, it integrates powerful functions within a limited space, suitable for deployment in compact environments such as substation relay protection rooms and energy storage power station containers. The product leverages Sienovo's mature hardware design experience, selecting industrial-grade components and wide-temperature materials, supporting 7x24 hour uninterrupted operation, adapting to harsh conditions such as high temperature, high humidity, and dust, ensuring long-term stable operation of power systems.

**Practical Applications in Energy Storage Power Stations**

In large-scale energy storage power station projects, serving as a station control layer data acquisition and processing unit, it parallelly collects battery management system, inverter, and grid data through multiple network ports, and connects to environmental monitoring sensors via serial ports. Its efficient data processing capability and multi-protocol compatibility achieve millisecond-level command response and panoramic visualization of system status, helping operators optimize operation strategies and improve system reliability.
