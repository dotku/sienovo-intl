With the rapid development of technologies such as 5G, artificial intelligence, cloud computing, IoT, and 4K/8K ultra-high-definition video, the smart commercial display market is evolving towards intelligence, real-time interaction, and AI perception. New products and scenarios are continuously emerging, among which the Versatile Smart Display, as a new type of intelligent display, is attracting significant attention in the smart commercial display and smart home fields. It can meet various user needs such as fitness, cooking, office work, online classes, video conferencing, and binge-watching shows. Due to the adoption of advanced artificial intelligence and IoT technologies, terminal devices have higher demands for the performance of core motherboards.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138811817/01_0d6804e13322f0b5372a17a88e1c4e3b.png)

To this end, VisionMEI provides its partners with higher-configuration and more cost-effective core motherboard solutions for Versatile Smart Displays. Multiple projects have already been successfully implemented, demonstrating maturity and stability, and supporting high-level customization for customers to meet personalized needs.

**RK3588A: All-Scenario Smart Terminal Motherboard Based on the RK3588 Platform**

**This is Xinmai's all-scenario smart terminal motherboard built upon Rockchip's new-generation flagship AIoT chip RK3588. It features an octa-core 64-bit CPU with a main frequency up to 2.4GHz, integrates an ARM Mali-G610 MP4 quad-core GPU, and has a built-in AI accelerator NPU that can provide 6 TOPS of computing power. It supports mainstream deep learning frameworks, and its excellent performance can bring a superior user experience to Versatile Smart Displays.**

**IoT-3399E (HDMI IN Version): High-End Smart Terminal Motherboard Based on the RK3399 Platform**

**The IoT-3399E is a high-end smart terminal motherboard based on Rockchip's server-grade hexa-core processor RK3399. It features a big.LITTLE architecture with dual-core Cortex-A72 + quad-core Cortex-A53, a main frequency up to 1.8GHz, and integrates a quad-core Mali-T860 GPU. With excellent performance, it comes onboard with various expansion interfaces, multiple display interfaces, and communication serial ports, offering maturity, stability, and reliable quality.**
