XM7016 Ethernet/USB Data Acquisition Card

XM7016 is an Ethernet/USB high-speed data acquisition card, featuring 16 true differential input channels, 24-bit resolution, and a maximum single-channel sampling rate of 256 ksps. It offers a total synchronous sampling rate of 4.096 Msps across 16 channels, precision pre-gain amplification, and integrated IEPE/ICP hardware support. This product utilizes multiple high-precision 24-bit ADC units combined with a pre-differential amplification module developed by our company over many years, giving it advantages such as high speed, high resolution, high accuracy, ultra-low noise, high common-mode rejection ratio, wide measurement range, and low temperature drift. It is suitable for various applications requiring precise high-speed data acquisition.

The data acquisition card is available in a series of versions, including the USB model XM7016H. Highly customized services are supported. All components of the XM7016N data acquisition card are industrial grade, featuring full metal shielding, making it suitable for applications in environments with strong industrial interference, and offering advantages such as moisture resistance, shock resistance, and anti-interference capabilities.
