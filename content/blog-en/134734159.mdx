The Transparent Kitchen solution enables customers to directly observe whether kitchen staff operations are standardized, hygiene conditions are up to standard, and whether any prohibited items are present. At the same time, Transparent Kitchen serves as a supervisory mechanism for restaurant employees. Yingma's Transparent Kitchen solution provides technical support for "transparency" in kitchen operations, enabling both internal and external stakeholders to "see," "hear," and "supervise" kitchen activities. This helps create a safe, hygienic, and trustworthy dining environment for consumers.
