T527+FPGA Solution:

Features an 8-core Cortex-A55, with a maximum frequency of 1.8GHz; a G57 MC1 GPU, 2 TOPS NPU; also integrates 1×RISC-V + 2×DSP cores, providing powerful 4K high-definition decoding performance, and is equipped with multiple display interfaces, 2×Gigabit Ethernet ports, 4×RS485 (5KV isolation, surge protection) and other interfaces.

It can achieve video wall splicing of up to 12 screens, synchronized display, and asynchronous display, compatible with various operating systems, and is widely applicable in fields such as exhibitions, advertising content delivery, new retail, and supermarkets for displaying various media content.
