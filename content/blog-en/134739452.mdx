## **Riverbank Litter Accumulation**

**Scenario Description:** River debris is often carried by currents and deposited on riverbanks. This system provides 24-hour real-time identification of litter accumulation along riverbanks, aiming to improve the efficiency of river cleanup and reduce the workload of environmental workers.

**Applicable Scenarios:** Applicable for identifying litter accumulation around riverbanks during daytime or nighttime with good lighting conditions.

**Model + Post-processing:** River Litter Detection

**Camera:**

Resolution: 1K and above, 4K and below

Target Image: Litter

## **03**

## **Area Intrusion**

**Scenario Description:** Traditional perimeter defense often uses infrared beams, which have a high false alarm rate. Therefore, an area intrusion algorithm is introduced to eliminate interference from factors like animals and leaves, identifying illegal intrusion by personnel into electronic fences or restricted areas. This algorithm can also be used to prevent people from approaching critical areas.

**Applicable Scenarios:** Applicable for reservoir perimeter defense and critical area protection, suitable for surveillance during daytime and nighttime with supplementary lighting; typically used in scenarios where certain areas must be restricted from entry/intrusion/work by personnel.

**Model + Post-processing:** Pedestrian Detection + ROI Area Delineation

Resolution: 1K and above, 4K and below
