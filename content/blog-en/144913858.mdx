![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144913858/01_968689a713419663f13cd8690acafa96.jpeg)

These manufacturers utilize advanced visual recognition technology to achieve image acquisition, processing, and analysis. The systems can automatically complete tasks such as quality inspection, material sorting, and equipment monitoring, significantly improving production efficiency and product quality. At the same time, the systems offer high flexibility and scalability, allowing for customized development based on customer needs to meet the visual recognition requirements of various industries. Furthermore, these manufacturers also provide comprehensive technical support and after-sales service to ensure stable system operation and continuous optimization.

Some industrial visual recognition system manufacturers choose to use RK3588 motherboards paired with Kylin domestic operating systems, instead of Intel I3 or I5 motherboards and Windows systems, primarily for the following reasons:![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144913858/02_737c6ff7110a4287b3cf976335f8c5a3.png)

1.  **The perfect combination of high performance and low power consumption:**

*   The RK3588 motherboard integrates high-performance CPUs, GPUs, and AI accelerators, capable of meeting the high-performance computing demands of industrial visual recognition systems.
*   Compared to Intel I3 or I5 motherboards, RK3588 offers similar performance while consuming less power, which helps reduce overall system energy consumption and heat generation, improving system stability and reliability.

2.  **Advantages of domestic production and independent control:**

*   The Kylin domestic operating system is a domestic operating system developed based on the Linux kernel, possessing a high degree of independent controllability, capable of meeting national requirements for information security and independent control.
*   As a domestic chip, RK3588, when combined with the Kylin domestic operating system, can better achieve domestic substitution, reduce reliance on external technologies, and enhance system security and reliability.

3.  **Rich interfaces and expandability:**

*   The RK3588 motherboard provides various interface options, such as PCIe, USB, HDMI, etc., facilitating connection to various industrial sensors, cameras, and other peripherals, meeting the diverse needs of industrial visual recognition systems.
*   The Kylin domestic operating system also supports various peripherals and drivers, ensuring system compatibility and stability.

4.  **Customization and optimization:**

*   Both the RK3588 motherboard and the Kylin domestic operating system support customized development, allowing for optimization based on the specific requirements of industrial visual recognition systems, enhancing system adaptability and flexibility.
*   Manufacturers can deeply customize the system according to actual application scenarios to meet specific customer needs.

5.  **Cost and cost-effectiveness:**

*   Compared to Intel I3 or I5 motherboards and Windows systems, the RK3588 motherboard paired with the Kylin domestic operating system, while offering similar performance, may have a higher cost-effectiveness.
*   This helps reduce the overall cost of industrial visual recognition systems and enhances product market competitiveness.

In summary, the RK3588 motherboard paired with the Kylin domestic operating system offers significant advantages in terms of high performance, low power consumption, domestic production, rich interfaces, customization, and cost-effectiveness, thus becoming the preferred solution for some industrial visual recognition system manufacturers.
