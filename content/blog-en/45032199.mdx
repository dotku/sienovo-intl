1. The role of pthread_join

Without pthread_join in the code, the main thread may terminate quickly, causing the entire process to exit, which in turn terminates all created threads before they even have a chance to start executing. By adding pthread_join, the main thread will wait indefinitely until the targeted thread completes its execution, ensuring that the created thread has the opportunity to run.
