##### Intelligent Port Applications

For port operations, optimizing every process—from unloading containers from ships to stacking in the yard and exiting the facility—to improve efficiency and reduce costs is a critical factor in profitability. To ensure container terminal customers can confidently use Terminal Operating Systems (TOS) to schedule and control various cranes, forklifts, trucks, and personnel, Davicom has developed a series of industrial computers tailored to different container terminal environments. Combined with reliable wireless communication networks and related technologies, these systems enable control centers to seamlessly manage computer terminals deployed anywhere across the terminal, significantly enhancing operational efficiency.

**Industry Applications**  
● In-vehicle computers for container trucks  
● In-vehicle computers for gantry cranes and mobile machinery  
● Integrated data and trunked radio communication solutions  
● Smart terminal gate solutions  
● OCR (container number recognition) solutions
