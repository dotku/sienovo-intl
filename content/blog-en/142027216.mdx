### 4-Channel Power Measurement Conditioner: A Domestic Alternative to NI

The RM-26999 is a rack-mount signal conditioning device that can be connected to multiple synchronous sampling multifunction I/O devices for power measurement. This device features four voltage input channels with a peak voltage of up to 2000 V, and four current sensor ports for current measurement. The current sensor ports are optimized for fluxgate sensors, providing the necessary power, communication, and signal lines for accurate measurement.

Shenzhen Xinmai offers customized domestic alternatives for NI products.
