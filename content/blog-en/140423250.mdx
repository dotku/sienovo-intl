A 4-channel 10G fiber optic adapter based on a PCI Express bus architecture, the board features four SFP+ 10 Gigabit fiber optic interfaces, an x8 PCIe host interface, and one group of 64-bit DDR3 SDRAM as high-speed cache, enabling high-speed acquisition, real-time recording, and broadband playback of 4-channel fiber optic network data.
The board also features four SATA interfaces, enabling high-speed storage of fiber optic data, supporting the SATA 3.0 standard, with a maximum storage bandwidth of up to 1.6 GByte/s.
The board is a standard full-height, half-length PCIe form factor, suitable for current mainstream servers or workstations, and can be widely used in data centers, server acceleration computing, and other scenarios. It can also be used in hardware-in-the-loop simulation systems.

#### Key Features

*   Standard PCI Express half-length card;
*   Compliant with PCI Express Gen2.0 specification, optional x1, x4, or x8 modes, bandwidth 40Gbps;
*   Fiber Optic Transmission Performance: Supports 4-channel SFP+ 10 Gigabit fiber, 10Gbps/lane line rate;
*   Fiber Optic Transmission Protocols: Supports multiple high-speed protocols such as Aurora and RapidIO;
*   Dynamic Storage Performance: One group of 64-bit 2GByte DDR3 SDRAM, theoretical bandwidth 12.8GByte/s, efficiency up to 90%;
*   SATA Storage Performance: Supports x4 SATA 3.0;
*   The board features 4-channel optocoupler-isolated digital discrete inputs and 4-channel optocoupler-isolated digital discrete outputs;
*   Onboard 1 PMCIO interface for GPIO expansion;
*   Onboard 128MByte BPI NorFlash for FPGA loading;

#### Interface Characteristics

*   Front panel supports 4-channel SFP+ Cages (maximum support 10Gbps/lane);
*   Onboard 4-channel SATA interfaces;
*   Onboard 1 PMCIO interface;
