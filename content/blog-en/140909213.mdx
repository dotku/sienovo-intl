AIoT Product Solution Supporting Energy Online Monitoring System Applications

In recent years, the smart energy industry has shown robust growth, benefiting from national policy support, market demand, and advanced technological backing. To drive the development of smart energy, we have created an AIoT product solution that supports energy online monitoring system applications.

**Market Potential of Energy Online Monitoring Systems**

With the rapid development of the social economy, energy demand continues to grow. Notably, China's wind and photovoltaic power generation installed capacity already ranks first globally. China is also one of the world's largest energy consumers. Against this backdrop, promoting energy transformation and building a green, low-carbon, safe, and efficient modern energy system has become a national development strategy for China. Meanwhile, the maturity and advancement of technologies such as artificial intelligence, the Internet of Things, and big data provide strong support for the development of smart energy. This has led to rapid development in the energy management-related hardware and software industries, including energy online monitoring systems that innovatively apply information and intelligent technologies.

An energy online monitoring system is a system used for real-time monitoring and management of energy consumption in enterprises or facilities. Relying on computer systems for control and integrating advanced data acquisition equipment, sensors, etc., it can assist managers in making more scientific and effective decisions, improving energy efficiency, and reducing losses by real-time collection, processing, and analysis of energy usage data. With the continuous development of the smart energy industry, energy online monitoring systems, as one of the essential means to achieve energy saving and carbon reduction, have also entered a golden period of development. They are currently widely applied in fields such as green buildings, smart grids, and industrial production.

**Application Challenges of Energy Online Monitoring Systems**

• Efficient Processing of Large Datasets: The system needs to collect, store, and analyze vast amounts of energy usage data in real-time, and generate various reports for visual display to allow users more intuitive understanding and convenient operation.

• Interconnection of Multiple Smart Devices: The system needs to perform real-time data acquisition and online monitoring of energy consumption for various energy-consuming devices.

• Long-term Stable Operation: Requires selecting safer, more reliable, and more durable computer hardware to enhance overall system performance.

**Energy Online Monitoring System Product Solution**

We have always been committed to providing diverse embedded computer products based on X86 and ARM architectures, along with customized services, to customers across various industries. For the smart energy sector, our embedded AIoT motherboard, built on the RK3588 processor, can enable energy online monitoring system applications.

![华北工控嵌入式AIOT主板EMB-2582.jpg](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/140909213/02_8d3ea1562ed2c263f165ae2af948f0df.jpeg)

Equipped with Rockchip's flagship AIoT chip RK3588, it delivers powerful data and image processing performance:

• Supports RK3588 (4\*A76@2.4GHz+4\*A55@1.8GHz) octa-core processor, with a built-in NPU offering 6 TOPs of computing power, supporting INT4/INT8/INT16/FP16 mixed precision operations to meet the demands of massive data processing and analysis.

• Onboard 4/8GB LPDDR4X memory, supports 1\*32/64GB eMMC and 1\*mSATA for expanded storage capacity.

• Integrated Mali-G610 MP4 quad-core GPU, supports 8K@60fps H.265/VP9 video decoding and 8K@30fps H.265/H.264 video encoding, as well as simultaneous encoding and decoding. Supports 1\*mini HDMI, 1\*eDP, and 1\*MIPI display interfaces for independent multi-display output.

Equipped with rich communication interfaces and I/O ports, supporting users in achieving interconnection of multiple smart devices and diverse expansion:

• Supports 2\*Gigabit RJ45 Ethernet ports, supports onboard Wi-Fi and 1\*MINI-PCIe slot for 4G/5G module expansion.

• Supports 2\*USB3.0 and 2\*USB2.0 interfaces for basic interconnection of more smart devices.

• Supports 2\*RS232/TTL and 1\*RS485 interfaces for higher-speed data transmission over long/short distances.

• Supports 1\*Headphone and 1\*2\*5W AMP (1\*4 2.0mm white connector) audio interface for power amplification.

• Supports 5\*GPIO, 2\*CAN, and 1\*FAN interfaces for users to implement more expansion applications.

Features industrial-grade high reliability and easy deployment, suitable for multiple scenarios:

• Supports Linux operating system and features a watchdog function, providing a more stable and easy-to-maintain system operating environment.

• Fan-cooled design meets wide temperature operating requirements from 0℃ to 65℃, providing higher heat dissipation efficiency, stability, and load capacity in limited spaces.

• Dimensions are 105x80mm, supports single DC 12V power input, with high functional integration and strong ease of use.
