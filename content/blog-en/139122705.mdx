### 8 AI (16-bit, 100 kS/s), 2 AO (5 kS/s/ch), 13 DIO USB Multifunction I/O Device

The USB‑6003 is a low-cost multifunction DAQ device. It provides analog I/O, digital I/O, and a 32-bit counter. The USB‑6003 provides basic functionality for applications such as simple data logging, portable measurements, and academic laboratory experiments. With screw-terminal connectivity, you can easily connect sensors and signals to the USB‑6003.
