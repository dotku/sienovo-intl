01. Industrial Automation Solution

![Industrial automation solution](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/108425967/01_1762d18d94b9ad210750d63e48430562.png)

Solution Advantages

Designed and customizable based on the Xinming AM5728-IDK-V3 platform.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/108425967/02_66f8d75e6f5206030b67dd64fdcb0447.png)

■ Based on RT-Preempt Patch real-time Linux system, meeting high real-time requirements for industrial control;  
■ Dual 1000M + dual 100M Ethernet ports, supporting Ethernet/EtherCAT functionality;  
■ Equipped with industrial bus interfaces such as RS485 and CAN;  
■ High-speed 32-channel input and output ports, supporting differential/single-ended/pulse output modes, enabling multi-device control in automated production lines;  
■ Fanless design with 24Hrs\*7 continuous operation capability, ensuring long-term stable operation under harsh industrial temperature conditions;  
■ Applicable to industrial production line automation, industrial motion control equipment, industrial robots, and other application fields.

Industrial Automation Solution: Core Board Overview

■ Supports up to 2 PRU MII and 2 PRU UART interfaces;

■ Industrial-grade temperature range: -40°C to +85°C.
