The smart campus solution leverages emerging technologies such as IoT and artificial intelligence to build an intelligent campus environment for teachers and students. It covers multiple scenarios including campus security, smart classrooms, attendance management, and smart canteens, delivering more humane, convenient, and efficient services across work, study, and daily life.

**Solution Architecture**

![Image](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138810480/01_8a295fabe603730441ff64e47cbdcf4c.jpeg)

**Key Features**

Comprehensively senses the physical campus environment, seamlessly integrating the school's physical space with digital space. By leveraging computational analysis in cyberspace, it captures operational patterns of the campus and provides feedback and control over the physical environment, establishing an intelligent, open educational setting and a convenient, comfortable living environment for faculty and students.

**Solution Functions**

####

**01/Secure Campus**

Real-time AI-powered video analytics, perimeter surveillance, and behavioral analysis of individuals within campus. Automatically detects and alerts on abnormal events such as fighting, loitering, or unauthorized intrusions.

![Image](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138810480/03_2cd1aab9556a74c4927af55f8a58ab98.png)

**02/Access Control**

Management of staff, students, visitors, and vehicle access.

Intelligent visitor appointment system supporting multiple identification methods including facial recognition, ID cards, QR codes, and fingerprint scanning.

![Image](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138810480/04_cf489424ae0d29a7c46ef2db284f45fa.png)

**03/Digital Information Publishing**

Multimedia information dissemination across campus

Indoor navigation and route guidance

Smart search and voice interaction

![Image](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138810480/05_63546ae3e60c3998a95d356c0286b730.png)

**04/Smart Classroom**

Digital class schedules, student attendance, exam room management, and interactive inquiry

Interactive smart desks that enable dynamic classroom engagement, enhancing learning interest

Unmanned self-service library systems for book borrowing, returning, renewal, membership registration, and search

![Image](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138810480/06_69ce0955b416b2e863e256ded7674eba.png)

**05/Smart Canteen**

"Transparent Kitchen" solution enabling real-time monitoring of kitchen operations to ensure food safety

Self-service meal selection with intelligent weighing and checkout, supporting contactless payments via campus card, QR code, or facial recognition. Displays nutritional information for dishes and provides spending analysis.

![Image](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/138810480/07_bd737ad180aab93e21d5a955eab0d914.png)

**06/Student Dormitories**

Smart access control for dormitory entry and exit using facial recognition, with real-time log recording

Security-enabled video intercom system for emergency response
