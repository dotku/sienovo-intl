The three-coordinate low-altitude target tracking radar is an all-day, all-weather, high-resolution, high-precision, and low-cost three-coordinate surveillance radar developed to meet the detection and tracking needs of low-altitude targets such as UAVs, paragliders, and hot air balloons in complex background environments.

This radar employs a composite pulse Doppler system, utilizing technologies such as solid-state transmission, integrated front-end, MTD, MTI, software display and control, Ethernet communication, and a fully sealed structure. It can achieve accurate detection, precise positioning, and timely early warning for multi-attribute targets. Its technical and functional features include advanced technology, high reliability, strong operability, low false alarm rate, diverse installation platforms, and wide deployment areas.
