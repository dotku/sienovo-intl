### PXIe, 4-Channel AI (16-Bit, 4 MS/s/ch), 2-Channel AO, 24-Channel DIO, PXI Multifunction I/O Module

The PXIe-6124 is a simultaneously sampled multifunction DAQ device. This module provides analog I/O, digital I/O, two 32-bit counters, and analog and digital triggering. The PXIe-6124 is suitable for a wide range of applications, including IF digitization, transient recording, ISDN, ADSL, and POTS manufacturing testing in the telecommunications industry, ultrasonic and sonar testing, and high-energy physics.
