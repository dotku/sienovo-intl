### 10 kS/s/ch, 350 Ω Quarter-Bridge Strain Gauge, 8-Channel C Series Strain/Bridge Input Module

The NI-9236 enables synchronized dynamic strain measurements across all channels, supporting high-speed, simultaneous data acquisition. This capability is critical for applications requiring comparison of multiple channels at precise moments, such as impact testing.

The NI-9236 provides built-in voltage excitation for quarter-bridge sensors. It also features 60 VDC channel-to-earth isolation and 1,000 Vrms transient isolation, delivering high common-mode noise rejection and enhanced safety.

Product Details  
Bridge Configuration: Quarter-Bridge  
Maximum Number of Differential Analog Input Channels: 8  
Analog Input Voltage Range: -29.4 mV/V to 29.4 mV/V  
Sealed: Yes  
Maximum Sampling Rate: 10 kS/s/channel  
Front-Panel Connection Type: Spring-Terminal  
Supported Hardware Platforms: C Series  
Bridge Resistance: 350 Ohms
