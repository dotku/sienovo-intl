The XM-5145 industrial switch is a Layer 2 managed switch specifically designed and developed for harsh environments such as rail transit, marine, and vehicle-mounted applications. The product design complies with rail transit industry certifications. It uses M12 connectors to ensure stable network communication in vibrating environments. The entire unit adopts a fanless passive cooling design, ensuring long-term stable operation in a -40°C to 75°C operating environment. The unique Bypass design ensures network connectivity even in the event of unexpected power loss.
