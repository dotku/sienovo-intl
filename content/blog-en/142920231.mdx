Based on NXP's LS1043A high-performance 64-bit ARM quad-core processor. The LS1043A processor is a quad-core 64-bit ARM processor launched by NXP for embedded networking. It supports flexible I/O packaging for fanless designs, provides over 10 Gbps performance, and is a solution specifically designed for small-form-factor networking and industrial applications. With a new 23x23 package, it supports pin-compatible designs and is scalable to the LS1046A (quad-core A72 processor) or LS1088A (ten-core A53 processor). The LS1043A continues the QorIQ series' consistent I/O flexibility, integrating the QUICC Engine®, and continues to provide seamless support for HDLC, TDM, or Profibus.
