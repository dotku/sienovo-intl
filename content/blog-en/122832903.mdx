### **1 Evaluation Board Introduction**

Xinmai Technology XM-ZYNQ7045-EVM is a high-end heterogeneous multi-core SoC evaluation board designed based on Xilinx Zynq-7000 series XC7Z045/XC7Z100 high-performance processors. The processor integrates a PS-side dual-core ARM Cortex-A9 + PL-side Kintex-7 architecture 28nm programmable logic resources. The evaluation board consists of a core board and an evaluation baseboard. The core board has undergone professional PCB layout and high/low temperature testing, ensuring stability and reliability, and meeting the requirements of various industrial application environments.

The evaluation board offers rich interface resources, providing dual Gigabit Ethernet ports, four SFP+ optical ports, CameraLink, HDMI, FMC HPC, GTX, PCIe, USB, Micro SD, and other interfaces, facilitating users in quickly evaluating product solutions and conducting technical preliminary research.
