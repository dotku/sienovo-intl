Industrial control systems play a crucial role in modern manufacturing and automation. CODESYS, a controller programming and development software platform based on the IEC 61131-3 standard, is suitable for developing control systems across various industries, enabling users to quickly and easily program and configure automation projects, and complete project development, software testing, and application debugging.

This time, STM32, in collaboration with its partner CODESYS, will guide you through an in-depth understanding of how to leverage STM32 and CODESYS to create powerful industrial control systems, as well as the underlying logic of industrial control systems. Through practical examples, we will help you seamlessly apply the knowledge gained to real-world engineering projects.

Codesys

CODESYS is a complete [development environment](https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E5%BC%80%E5%8F%91%E7%8E%AF%E5%A2%83/10119007?fromModule=lemma_inlink "开发环境") for [programmable logic control](https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E5%8F%AF%E7%BC%96%E7%A8%8B%E9%80%BB%E8%BE%91%E6%8E%A7%E5%88%B6/53417077?fromModule=lemma_inlink "可编程逻辑控制") [PLCs](https://baike.baidu.com/item/PLC/275974?fromModule=lemma_inlink "PLC") (CODESYS is an acronym for Controlled Development System). When PLC programmers are programming, CODESYS provides a straightforward method for powerful IEC languages.
