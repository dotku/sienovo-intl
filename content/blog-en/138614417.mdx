The FPGA+DDR+SERDES+USB3.0 solution consists of a transmitter and a receiver. It employs fiber optic communication, allowing data to be transmitted unidirectionally only, thereby preventing reverse attacks and ensuring system security.
Customer Applications: Scenarios requiring high security and high reliability.
Key Features:
1. Supports USB3.0 transmission, achieving up to 350MB/s in actual tests.
2. Supports simultaneous transmission of different data over 3 optical fibers to enhance speed.
3. Supports simultaneous transmission of identical data over 3 optical fibers to enhance data reliability.
4. Supports AES256 encryption and decryption to ensure data security.
5. Supports dual-system backup to ensure system reliability.
6. Achieved 0 errors after continuously transmitting over 10TB of data in actual tests.
