1.  1.  1.  **Area Entry Detection Algorithm**
            1.  **Function Description**

Area entry detection involves presetting a monitoring area in a surveillance scene and triggering an alarm when people or vehicles are detected entering that area. The resolution of detected targets in a 1080p image should be no less than 30*30 pixels.

1.  1.  1.  1.  **Recommended Scenarios**

Scenario Requirements: Can be used indoors and outdoors. Avoid direct sunlight or backlight conditions. At night, only full-color images are supported; if the camera does not support full-color mode, please use white light for supplementary illumination.

Target Distance Requirements: Can detect events beyond 3 meters from a normal 3-meter pole height.

Target Pixel Requirements: The resolution of detected targets in a 1080P image should be greater than or equal to 30*30 pixels.

Recall Rate: 95%

            1.  **Function Description**
