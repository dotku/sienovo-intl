32 AI (16-bit, 1 MS/s), 48 DIO, PXI Multifunction I/O Module  
The PXI-6254 provides analog input, correlated digital I/O, two 32-bit counters/timers, and analog and digital triggering. This device delivers reliable, low-cost DAQ functionality for a wide range of applications—from laboratory automation and research to design verification/test and manufacturing test. SCC or SCXI signal conditioning modules can be used to add sensor and high-voltage measurement capabilities to the device. The included NI-DAQmx driver and configuration utilities simplify setup and measurement.
