##### **RFID + Forklift PC Industry Applications**

**Background**
By installing forklift PCs, RFID antennas, and readers on forklifts in warehouse management, the efficiency of automation in warehouse management can be greatly enhanced. The RFID detection antenna is installed on the front fork, allowing it to automatically scan RFID tag information from goods. The received information is transmitted via cable to the RFID reader, and the reader then inputs it to the computer via wired or wireless connection, thus completing automatic goods identification. Users can then set up WiFi, Narrow Band, or directly use 4G mobile networks as the warehouse's wireless communication network to transmit data back to the backend server. This entire process reduces manual involvement and greatly improves work efficiency.

**Solution Requirements**
● **Touch-display integrated vehicle-mounted computer**
● Mounting brackets suitable for various forklift models
● Compatibility with diverse forklift battery voltages, such as 12V, 24V, 48V, 80V, etc.
● Requires connecting RFID readers via I/O interfaces, and addressing power supply for scanning devices
● Or built-in Bluetooth module for data communication with wireless RFID readers

##### **Barcode Scanner + Forklift PC Industry Applications**

**Background**
To improve warehouse management efficiency, users can set up WiFi, Narrow Band, or use 3G/4G mobile networks as the warehouse's wireless communication network architecture, according to actual needs. Then, forklift PCs and industrial barcode scanners are installed on forklifts. This allows the warehouse management system to send instructions via the wireless network to forklift operators to move goods to designated locations, and use barcode scanners to directly identify goods. The goods information and work results are then transmitted back to the system via the network. This significantly increases forklift work efficiency and reduces labor.

**Solution Requirements**
