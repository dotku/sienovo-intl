**Project Introduction**

A Jiangsu-based automation technology company, built on automation control, is dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions for marine power plant and power distribution systems, marine engine room automation systems, marine main engine control and steering control systems, marine network transmission systems, marine safety monitoring and explosion-proof systems, marine dynamic positioning systems, marine electric propulsion systems, and marine variable frequency drive and control systems.

Leveraging the scientific research capabilities and marine industry background support of Jiangsu University of Science and Technology, the company primarily focuses on the production and services of marine power distribution series products, engine room automation consoles, variable frequency drive and electric propulsion products, main engine remote control and steering consoles, and safety monitoring system products. It provides reliable product support and technical services for marine and offshore engineering manufacturing enterprises.

**System Description**

To ensure the normal and safe operation of vessels, reliable control of all onboard equipment is required. Due to the wide variety of marine equipment, control requirements vary, and the forms, parameters, performance, and technologies used in control equipment also differ. Vessels are equipped with various control boxes, such as deck machinery control boxes, multi-functional cabin equipment control boxes, diesel engine control boxes, dredge control systems, and rudder propeller control boxes. For equipment with fixed functions, various models of dedicated controllers have been developed, as well as general-purpose controllers whose parameters can be modified and set according to user requirements. All controllers are equipped with communication interfaces and remote control interfaces, providing convenience for user monitoring systems.
