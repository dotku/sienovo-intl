A Baytrail-based PCI 104 embedded motherboard. The main feature of this motherboard is its PCI 104 expansion interface. It integrates three tiers of CPUs (J1900, N2930, N2807) under the Baytrail architecture, offering users more choices. Onboard DDR3L memory, support for WinXP (no hardware graphics acceleration) operating system, and wide temperature range are among the highlights of this product.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/140766308/02_d0d229c075df4b68b2dfe76fb9518cd7.png)

This is a Baytrail-based PC/104 motherboard. It features 2GB onboard memory and Mini SATA storage expansion, providing users with greater convenience and stability. PC/104's support for the ISA bus highlights its unique aspect.
