Sienovo EtherCAT master FPGA achieves high real-time performance with encryption for 32 axes.

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/93245163/01_073c3c79f809e50a1cf62c33769a7f50.png)

**Performance Advantages**

1.  Faster cycle time, achievable down to 31.25us
2.  Lower jitter, jitter time less than 0.004us
3.  Excellent synchronization performance, master and slave devices can achieve clock synchronization accuracy far less than 1us (50ns measured on 4 axes)

For the implementation of EtherCAT-based CNC systems, a feasible system solution based on real-time communication and DSP control using EtherCAT technology is proposed. The EtherCAT master network architecture is built, and the EtherCAT master's state machine, distributed clock, and CoE protocol are analyzed in detail.
