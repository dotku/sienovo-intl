**Solution Introduction**

Time synchronization solutions are high-precision time synchronization technologies with wide applications in various fields, including telecommunications, power systems, transportation, and finance.

Time synchronization solutions utilize various communication protocols (such as PTP/gPTP) to ensure that devices, systems, or sensors within a network share a unified time standard. This enables precise collaborative work and data synchronization between devices in various fields, including industrial automation, smart manufacturing, and communication networks.![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/152907753/01_6ab328b508f94e7f89930ab3eb025bdf.png)

**Solution Advantages**

The time synchronization solution offers features such as stability, reliability, ease of use, and broad compatibility. By integrating GPS access, it achieves high-precision time synchronization.

■ GPS Precision Navigation System: Compatible with PPS/GPRMC signals, capable of acquiring precise GPS latitude and longitude positioning data.

■ Precise Time Synchronization Solution: Seamlessly integrates with PCIe acquisition cards, enabling combined PPS and CAN timing to ensure synchronization accuracy within 20 microseconds.

■ External Trigger and Multi-channel Synchronous Output: The system supports external trigger signal input and features frequency multiplication and division functions, capable of outputting 8 channels of trigger signals at different frequencies, ensuring synchronization accuracy within 200 nanoseconds.

■ PTP Master-Slave Mode Compatibility: Provides PTP/gPTP master-slave interfaces, supporting both PTP and gPTP timing protocols.

■ Flexible Time Synchronization Source Switching: Achieves convenient switching of synchronization time sources between GNSS and gPTP/PTP Slave via a DIP switch for PPS signal master-slave mode.![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/152907753/02_60c89bab158e42b595c6b7b9d2fa7f69.png)
