##### **Application in the Tire Building Machine Equipment Industry**

**Project Background**
China is a major country in tire production and export. In the tire manufacturing process, the building (forming) process is a critical step. It involves assembling semi-finished components into a tire carcass (green tire) on a tire building machine according to technical specifications. The process is relatively complex, requiring high precision in equipment processing and positioning. Major tire building machine manufacturers are all striving to research and develop efficient, precise, and simple tire building equipment. China's tire equipment manufacturers are also continuously innovating in terms of process, technology, and efficiency to develop machines that meet production requirements. DPC series industrial panel PCs offer reliable and stable performance, adapt to harsh industrial environments, and provide users with an efficient, easy-to-install, and cost-effective experience.

**Solution Requirements**
● Limited control cabinet space, requiring small-size installation
● Harsh environment, requiring a high protection rating
● Needs a high-performance platform capable of stable and reliable continuous operation

**Solution**

As a host computer, it runs customer operating software, stores data, supports panel mounting and VESA mounting, and integrates seamlessly with the control cabinet, saving installation space. It connects downwards to instruments such as cameras and PLCs, providing reliable data transmission. It is capable of adapting to various complex and harsh industrial environments, providing strong assurance for customers to improve output and product quality.
