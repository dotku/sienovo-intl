Benefits of Software Engineers' Agility — Fostering Rapid Idea Generation

1.  With abundant documentation, one can quickly pinpoint issues through skimming and scanning, laying the groundwork for rapid problem-solving. This avoids the slowness, high pressure, and low efficiency of reading everything thoroughly, freeing up and gaining time, naturally allowing you to do many other things you want, such as learning and professional development.
2.  In debugging or laboratory settings, one can quickly identify problems.
3.  During coding, one can quickly find solutions and algorithms. Regarding optimization, refactoring remains a powerful tool afterward.
4.  When writing solution requirement analyses and detailed solution designs, one can more quickly establish an architecture and content, and refine it. This reduces work pressure.
5.  In meetings and presentations, one can gain recognition from leaders, paving the way for promotion.
6.  Become a sharp and insightful programmer.

Therefore, it's important to cultivate this mindset. Rather than just coding like an ascetic every day, if something can be solved with mental effort or keen observation, don't be dull; a bit of agility is all it takes.
