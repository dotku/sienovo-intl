8-channel Vibration/Voltage + 2-channel Tachometer PTP1588 Network Synchronized Edge Computing Acquisition Board

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677895/01_a228280ba5b045108456e028e4cdd2de.png)

![](https://pub-048dcb96257f476697b113fcb5939cb9.r2.dev/blog/144677895/02_7b0f4a981ee44b61a1a5939976b28870.png)

Utilizing an XC7A100T-2FGG484 + TMS320C6655 architecture,

it is configured with 8 standard vibration acquisition channels, 2 tachometer acquisition channels, and a standard Gigabit Ethernet interface.

For synchronization, this is the first application of PTP network synchronization technology. Due to the extensive edge computing requirements involved, the cost is relatively high. It is therefore more suitable for research and measurement fields that demand high-precision measurement, are sensitive to noise and electromagnetic environments, require significant edge computing, but where cost is not a primary concern.
