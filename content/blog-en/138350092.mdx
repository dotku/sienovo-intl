The modular instrument platform is built around an FPGA controller,  
paired with a rich and flexible selection of instrument modules such as multimeters, oscilloscopes, signal generators, data loggers, and audio analyzers. It covers high-precision, high-speed, and RF signal test, measurement, and processing, providing comprehensive test and measurement technologies and solutions from verification to prototyping and mass production. The platform has gained deep collaboration and recognition from leading international customers. Its embedded form factor features a compact structure,  
making it easy to integrate into equipment. The card-type instrument system can be used not only for prototype development but also as a multi-function instrument. The standalone instrument is small and compact, suitable for use as a single-function device. The integrated tester offers a complete multi-function turnkey solution in an industrial chassis,  
ideal for deployment in production line testing.
