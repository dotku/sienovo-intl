The Intelligent Mining All-in-One Machine is centered on preventing and mitigating major safety risks, fully leveraging the advantages of edge-side video intelligent recognition technology. Tailored for mining scenarios, it provides intelligent capabilities such as safety supervision, equipment monitoring, and disaster early warning. The product fully complies with the technical guidelines and acceptance standards for intelligent video system construction in mining scenarios.

The Intelligent Mining All-in-One Machine adopts the latest domestic high-performance NPU intelligent chip, possessing server-grade compute power performance with strong scalability, reaching up to 768 TOPS. It integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms and a comprehensive video intelligence management platform, capable of intelligently identifying and accurately assessing unsafe behaviors of personnel, unsafe equipment statuses, and unsafe environmental factors in mines. It also features data fusion analysis, risk prediction and control, tiered early warning and alarming, device linkage control, and intelligent auxiliary decision-making functions, meeting the needs for mining production safety assurance and supervision, and further enhancing the level of intelligent safety production in mines.

**Device Access Management**

Supports Onvif/GB28181/RTSP
/RTMP/private protocols for connecting to ordinary network cameras, smart cameras, network audio columns, network relays, and other devices.

**Video On Demand / Stream Forwarding**

Supports up to 16-grid video preview, can push video streams externally.

**Alarm Information Display**

Displays information such as event alarms, personnel alarms, vehicle alarms, and IoT events.

**Alarm Linkage / Push**

Supports alarm push via APP, official accounts, email, SMS, and voice calls.

**Open Interfaces / Ecosystem Empowerment**

Provides API interfaces for various protocols like HTTP/MQTT, supporting secondary development and integration by higher-level platforms for flexible invocation.

**1-to-N Master-Slave Management**

Supports 1 master device managing multiple slave devices, forming master-slave device management, enhancing flexibility and ease of operation.
