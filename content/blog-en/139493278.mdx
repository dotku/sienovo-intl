**China Railway Design Group Co., Ltd.** (CRDC), formerly China Railway Third Survey and Design Institute Group Co., Ltd. (CRTSDI), is the sole design enterprise affiliated with China State Railway Group Co., Ltd. Established in 1953 and headquartered in Tianjin, it is a large enterprise group primarily engaged in engineering survey, design, consulting, supervision, EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) contracting, and product industrialization for railways, urban rail transit, highways, and other projects. It holds a Class A comprehensive engineering design qualification certificate and is one of the first high-tech enterprises recognized by the state. Its comprehensive strength ranks among the top in the industry. In the 2022 ENR rankings, it was ranked 37th among the Top 150 Global Design Firms, 103rd among the Top 225 International Design Firms, 35th among the Top 80 Chinese Contractors, and 5th among the Top 60 Chinese Engineering Design Firms.

Bridge structure safety monitoring involves collecting key parameters such as deformation, stress, and environmental factors at critical structural points of the bridge. These are transmitted to a platform via an edge computer with a 5G/4G industrial wireless gateway BMG700, enabling real-time monitoring, statistical analysis, anomaly alarming, and remote management of bridge structure safety. It achieves 24-hour real-time monitoring: fully automated online monitoring of bridge deformation, stress, environment, etc., to grasp the overall operational status of the bridge in real-time. Automatic Report Push: Monitoring results are released in real-time, and monitoring reports are regularly pushed to users. Multi-level Tiered Warning: When data is abnormal, the system triggers a corresponding three-level alarm mechanism, notifying users immediately via SMS, fax, broadcast, and other forms. Emergency Response Handling: Relevant solutions are directly retrieved from an expert database, and measures such as personnel intervention and road closures are promptly taken to eliminate safety hazards in their infancy. Structural Trend Analysis: Quantitative safety evaluation of bridge structures and safety analysis of monitoring indicator data enable structural stability trend analysis. Provision of Reference Basis: Long-term stable storage of monitoring data provides analogous references for future engineering design and construction of similar projects.

1.  1.  Product Overview

The XM-AIBOX-16 Smart Edge Analysis All-in-One Machine is a high-performance, low-power edge computing product. Equipped with the domestic TPU chip BM1684, its INT8 computing power reaches up to 17.6 TOPS. It can process 16 channels of high-definition video simultaneously and supports hardware decoding of 32 channels of 1080P HD video and 2 channels of encoding.

This product highly integrates high-precision AI intelligent algorithms based on computer vision and deep learning networks, as well as a video intelligent comprehensive management platform. The AI intelligent algorithms cover various scenarios such as parks, communities, construction sites, and campuses, and can be combined and configured on demand according to the scene. The video intelligent comprehensive management platform supports front-end device management, real-time video preview, alarm push, evidence capture, online algorithm loading and optimization, data situational analysis large screen display, etc. The device is easy to operate and plug-and-play. It also has rich northbound API interfaces, enabling upper-layer business application platforms.
